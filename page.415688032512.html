House passes resolution urging Obama to send weapons to Ukraine to fight Russian-backed rebels

1WASHINGTON - The House on Monday overwhelmingly approved a resolution urging President Barack Obama to send lethal weapons to Ukraine to protect its sovereignty in its fight against Russian-backed rebels.
2The resolution was approved 348 to 48.
3There is bipartisan support in Congress to provide the arms to Ukraine forces battling the rebels.
4Russian President Vladimir Putin denies arming rebels in the war in eastern Ukraine, which began last April after Moscow annexed the mostly Russian-speaking Crimean Peninsula.
5State Department officials say Obama administration officials are discussing lethal assistance but are waiting to see whether the agreements that led to February's cease-fire are implemented.
