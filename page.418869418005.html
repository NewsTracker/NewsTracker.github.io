Greece in second day of preparatory talks with bailout creditors over new rescue agreement

1ATHENS, Greece - Emissaries from Greece's international creditors are holding a second day of preparatory talks with Greek officials, ahead of higher-level negotiations later this week on the country's new multi-billion euro lifeline.
2The aim is to thrash out the terms of the deal - worth about 85 billion euros ($94 billion) over three years - before Aug. 20, when Greece must make a debt payment that it cannot afford without new loans.
3It will be the country's third bailout in more than five years.
4So far, the country has received about 240 billion euros from the European Union and the International Monetary Fund.
