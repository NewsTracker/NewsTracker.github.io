Tsarnaev friend to be sentenced for misleading investigators after Boston Marathon bombing

1BOSTON - A friend who had dinner with the Boston Marathon bombers hours after the attack is to be sentenced this week for misleading investigators.
2Khairullozhon Matanov (hy-ROO'-luh-zhahn MAH'-tuh-nahf) was a friend of Tamerlan Tsarnaev and an acquaintance of Dzhokhar Tsarnaev.
3He pleaded guilty in March to misleading the FBI after the deadly 2013 bombings by deleting his computer files and failing to disclose contact he had with the brothers.
4Matanov, a native of Kyrgyzstan, worked as a cab driver and lived in Quincy, Massachusetts.
5He wasn't accused of knowing about the bombings ahead of time or participating in the attack.
6Matanov is to be sentenced Thursday in Boston to 2 1/2 years in prison.
7Tamerlan Tsarnaev died in a police search after the bombings; His brother was convicted and sentenced to death.
