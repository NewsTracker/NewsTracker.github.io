Germanwings says plane evacuated at Cologne-Bonn airport after bomb threat received

1The threat against flight 826 to Milan's Malpensa airport was received Sunday evening.
2The tower in Cologne alerted the pilot of the Airbus A320, which was taxiing toward the runway at the time, Germanwings said.
3The pilot took the plane to a position at the airport that is foreseen for such cases, where the passengers and crew left the aircraft.
4Federal police said sniffer dogs found no sign of explosives, news agency dpa reported.
5The threat was emailed to the airport 15 minutes before the plane's scheduled departure, prompting police to set the necessary measures in motion.
6Germanwings planned to fly the passengers to Milan on another plane.
