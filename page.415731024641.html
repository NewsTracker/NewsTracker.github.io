Zayn quitting 1D at 'right time'

1Zayn Malik has left boyband One Direction saying he wants the chance to be "a normal 22-year-old".
2The group said they would continue as a four-piece for their world tour and record another album - their fifth - later this year.
3Malik said: " My life with One Direction has been more than I could ever have imagined. But, after five years, I feel like it is now the right time for me to leave the band. I'd like to apologise to the fans if I've let anyone down, but I have to do what feels right in my heart.
4"I am leaving because I want to be a normal 22-year-old who is able to relax and have some private time out of the spotlight. I know I have four friends for life in Louis, Liam, Harry and Niall. I know they will continue to be the best band in the world."
5Malik quit the boyband's world tour last week after being "signed off with stress" and returned to the UK after he was forced to defend himself online and declare his love for fiancee Perrie Edwards when pictures emerged of him apparently close to another woman.
6In a statement, the rest of the group said: "W e're really sad to see Zayn go, but we totally respect his decision and send him all our love for the future.
7"The past five years have been beyond amazing - we've gone through so much together, so we will always be friends.
8"The four of us will now continue. We're looking forward to recording the new album and seeing all the fans on the next stage of the world tour."
9A spokesman for the band said Malik, who grew up in Bradford, is at home with his family and fiancee - Little Mix singer Perrie Edwards.
10Simon Cowell, who discovered the boyband on talent show The X Factor, said: " I would like to say thank you to Zayn for everything he has done for One Direction.
11"Since I first met Zayn in 2010, I have grown very, very fond - and immensely proud - of him. I have seen him grow in confidence and I am truly sorry to see him leave.
12"As for One Direction, fans can rest assured that Niall, Liam, Harry and Louis are hugely excited about the future of the band."
13Malik appeared in a leaked video last year smoking what appeared to be a joint while the group travelled in a van in South America.
14He was also forced to explain his absence from promotional commitments after a US TV host questioned his bandmates about ''rumours of substance abuse''.
15The band, who were put together after entering The X Factor as solo acts, have become millionaires on their way to being one of the biggest bands in the world.
16Their latest album, Four, topped the US Billboard Top 200 chart making them the first group in history to debut at number one with their first four albums.
17They have fans all over the world - their current tour takes in venues in countries including Indonesia and South Africa - and the video for their single, Best Song Ever, attracted almost 200 million views on YouTube.
