Canada to send 200 military trainers to Ukraine, says risk low

1Prime Minister Stephen Harper said in a statement that the troops would be based in western Ukraine, far from the fighting, until March 31, 2017.
2"Their activities will include explosive ordnance disposal and improvised explosive device disposal training, military police training, medical training, flight safety training, and logistics system modernization training," he said.

4Harper has taken a hard line in public against President Vladimir Putin over what he calls blatant Russian interference in Ukraine.
