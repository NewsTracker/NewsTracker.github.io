Banana peel hits Dave Chappelle on New Mexico stage

1A man tossed a banana peel at Dave Chappelle during a stand-up show, hitting in the comedian in the leg, police in this northern New Mexico city say.
2Christian Englander, 30, of Santa Fe, was arrested on suspicion of misdemeanor disorderly conduct and battery after the fruit throw Monday night, Santa Fe police Lt. Andrea Dobyns said.
