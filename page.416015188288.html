J.B. Holmes takes Houston Open

1J.B. Holmes used a strong start Sunday for a Houston Open victory in a three-way playoff against Jordan Spieth and Johnson Wagner.
2The 32-year-old Holmes birdied the first five holes and nine of the first 12 after starting the day six shots back of Jordan Spieth.
3He had a 64, the day's low round, and won on the second playoff hole for his fourth PGA Tour victory and the $1,188,000 check.
4Although Holmes' missed birdie putt had given Wagner another chance, his short putt for par lipped out.
5Shawn Stefani watches his tee shot on the first hole in the final round of the Houston Open golf tournament Sunday, April 5, 2015, in Humble, Texas.
