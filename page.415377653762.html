19"Such actions may be regarded as interference in the investigation," the statement said.
20The committee said that this was "a violation not only of the rules (of visiting rights) but also of the law," and said that both Babushkin and the journalist would be questioned by investigators.
21The committee did not confirm or deny Babushkin's claims that Dadaev had been mistreated.
