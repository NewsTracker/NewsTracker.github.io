At least two dead after Nepal quake

1KATHMANDU (Reuters) - At least two people were killed on Saturday when an earthquake measuring 7.9 magnitude struck Nepal and parts of northern India, authorities in both countries said.
2A 15-year-old girl was killed in northern India after the earthquake brought down the wall of her home in a village near the border with Nepal.
3Another girl was killed by a falling statue in a town outside Nepal's capital Kathmandu, according to Nepal's state radio.
