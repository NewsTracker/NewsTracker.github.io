Mexico nabs alleged head of gang that kidnapped over 100 in conflictive Tierra Caliente region

1A Federal Police statement alleges that 24-year-old Nicolas Trujillo Urieta led the group, which operated in the violence-plagued Tierra Caliente region of southern Mexico.
2Friday's statement said Trujillo was detained in the town of Atotonilco el Grande, Hidalgo state.
3No shots were fired.
