Kenya's Kandie wins Prague international marathon

1Felix Kipchirchir Kandie of Kenya won the Prague International Marathon on Sunday while Yebrgual Melese of Ethiopia claimed the women's race.
2Kandie completed the 21st edition of the race in 2 hours, 8 minutes and 32 seconds to lead a 1-2 Kenyan finish.
3Evans Kiplagat Chebet finished in 2:08:50, ahead of Deribe Robi of Ethiopia in 2:09:05.
4Yebrgual Melese crossed the finish line in 2:23:49, edging Sara Moreira of Portugal by 1 minute.
