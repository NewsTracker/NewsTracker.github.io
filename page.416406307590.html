Karl Lagerfeld and Beyonce show off their exclusive custom Apple Watches

1Apple is continuing its publicity assault for the Apple Watch, which finally hits shelves this week.
2But while various celebrities - including Katy Perry and Pharrel Williams - have already got their hands on the sought-after accessory (and have been duly Instagramming pics of it), a special few, or rather a special two, have bagged themselves a very exclusive version, so exclusive that even the richest non-celeb can't buy it.
3Both Beyonce and Chanel creative director Karl Lagerfeld have been given an Apple Watch with an 18-carat gold link bracelet - a design that is not available for us mere mortals to purchase.
4The closest we can get to the design is a version with a silver stainless steel link bracelet or a black stainless steel link bracelet, which start from 819 and 899 respectively.
5Karl Lagerfeld's custom-made Apple Watch with gold strap.
6"1st apple watch specially made for Karl!! Amazing!!! Thanks #apple!!!!!! [sic]" posted Lagerfeld's bodyguard, Sebastien Jondeau, alongside a photo of the coveted item (above).
7Meanwhile Beyonce used her own website to flaunt an image of her wearing the gold timepiece at Coachella this past weekend.
8Macrumours.com estimate that the custom gold design is worth around $25,000 (about 17,000).
9That figure takes into account the cost of the Apple Watch Edition, which is available with a just a gold-framed face and clasp from 8,000.
10Beyonce showed off her custom Apple Watch at Coachella.
11As to why the singer and designer are the chosen two, Apple won't comment, saying only "all Apple Watches available to our customers are on our website" when approached.
12But the move is a savvy one.
13Beyonce is world famous and much-copied when it comes to her fashion, accessory and beauty choices, while Karl Lagerfeld is arguably fashion's biggest influencer.
14As well as Chanel, he heads up Fendi and his own eponymous brand.
15Karl Lagerfeld and Anna Wintour attended the Apple Watch preview, pictured here with Apple's Jony Ive and Marc Newson.
16The 81 year-old is also a keen Apple fan - he has 20 iPads and 300 iPods at the most recent count.
17He's also been key in helping Apple to bring the watch to a fashion audience - he took Anna Wintour along to the watch's preview at Paris concept store Collette during last September's Paris Fashion Week, ensuring it got tons of fashion-led publicity.
18So, is Apple succeeding in its attempts to woo the fashion crowd?
19Well, let's just say we've got our eye on the 42mm 18-Carat Yellow Gold Case with Midnight Blue Classic Buckle.
20Shame about the 12,000 price tag.
