6AP: Your first name is unusual?
7Where does it come from?
8Kennedy: It's Corsican.

13AP: Were you a musical baby?
14Kennedy: I loved it right from the beginning.
15My mom always talks about the times she would play her guitar when she was pregnant with me, and my whole body would move to rest against the guitar.
16She would play for hours and I would lie there, enjoying the vibrations.
17AP: You play the piano already.
18Does that help portraying King?
19Kennedy: It's sort of ironic because Carol King was always very comfortable playing - it was her voice that she was nervous about.
20For me it's the opposite: I start shaking when I have to play in front of people but singing is a little easier for me.
21So it's been a great exercise in confidence building.
22AP: Has King influenced you as a singer-songwriter?
23Kennedy: I had this melody written for, I think, about a year or two years and I didn't know what to do with it.
24It felt like a Gaelic lullaby.
25I didn't know what it was for and then, all of a sudden, he was born.
26I sat in my apartment and I write the whole thing in one night.
27It was a song for my son.
28This whole time it was brewing.
29So, in a way, Carole King influenced that, for sure.
30AP: You're both playing King and taking over from an actress who won a Tony.
31Any pressure?
32Kennedy: I think the trick for me is just staying in the moment.
33If you think about the mountain, you can't climb it.
34All you have to do is just think about the steps that are right in front of you.
35If I'm really doing my job, there's no time for worrying about pressure or any of that kind of stuff.
36The job fills my whole being.
37AP: How are you approaching King?
38Kennedy: She always has a wonderful smile on her face.
39It's like she's sharing some personal secret with you every time she sings.
40I think if you can get that joy and that flavor, then you've got her.
41The rest is all details.
42AP: Is your son growing up with music like you were?
43Kennedy: He bounces around in the kitchen.

45He loves when I sing.
46I sing for him all the time.
47I was performing up until I was seven months pregnant.
48It's funny because I play the songs that he was listening to when he was inside me and I'm sure he recognizes them.
