11NEW YORK (AP) - Hundreds of people have rallied and marched in New York and Boston to protest the death of a Baltimore man who was critically injured in police custody as Philadelphia activists prepare for their own demonstration.
12At least 60 people were arrested in New York Wednesday night after protesters spilled into the streets of Manhattan, holding a die-in at Times Square and blocking some streets and a tunnel.
13In Boston, activists gathered in a park behind police headquarters and continued with a peaceful march to a park.
14Philadelphia protesters plan to demonstrate Thursday at city hall.
