Baltimore hospital says man injured in police encounter dies

1BALTIMORE (AP) - A spokeswoman at a Baltimore-area hospital says a man who sustained injuries last week after an encounter with police has died.
2Karen Lancaster of University of Maryland Shock Trauma Center in Baltimore on Sunday confirmed the death of 25-year-old Freddie Gray.
3The death comes a week after an encounter Gray had with four police officers on April 12.
4Baltimore Police say Gray was stopped and then tried to run away but was caught and arrested.
5He was then placed in a prisoner transport wagon bound for a police station but 30 minutes later, an ambulance was called for transport from the station house.
6Gray's family said he sustained spinal injuries after the encounter.
7The incident is under investigation, and the officers have been placed on routine administrative leave.
