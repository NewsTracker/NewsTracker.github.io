FDA modifies partial clinical hold on Tekmira Ebola study

1The company's application to study the treatment, TKM-Ebola, remains on partial clinical hold for doses above 0.24 mg/kg per day, Tekmira said on Friday.
2The FDA placed Tekmira's early-stage study on a clinical hold in July, asking the company for data on how the therapy works and requesting a change to design of the study related to multiple dosing.
3The clinical hold was modified in August into a partial hold asking only for a change in the study design.
4Tekmira had reported interim results in May showing that a higher dose of 0.30 mg/kg per day was well-tolerated.
5Tekmira said it plans to resume the early study in the coming weeks, and expects results from the study in the second half of 2015.
6Tekmira shares rose about 2 percent to $17.95 following the lifting of a trading halt imposed at 8.30 a.m. ET.
