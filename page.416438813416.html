Facebook starts testing mobile calling app Hello

1Facebook Inc said it started testing its mobile calling app Hello on Android, which allows users to connect with Facebook to see the identity and information about callers even if the number is not saved on the phone.
2Hello, built by the messenger team, can pull information people and businesses have shared on Facebook.
3The app also gives the option of blocking calls, which then go to a voicemail feature.
4Facebook said in March that it would add a feature to its messaging app that allows friends to send and receive money through it.
