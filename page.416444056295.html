9Rondo had 10 playoff triple-doubles with the Celtics, who won the title in 2008 and went the NBA Finals again two years later.
10The Mavericks gambled that they were getting the player Rondo was before missing large parts of two seasons because of a torn anterior cruciate ligament in his right knee.
11Instead, Dallas ended up with a bad fit for an offense that regressed after being among the league's best at the time of the trade.
12Rondo also missed six games because of a broken bone near his left eye.
13''My relationship with him was professional,'' Carlisle said.
14Rondo was benched late in a close game not long after the five-player deal that sent guard Jameer Nelson, center Brandan Wright and forward Jae Crowder to Boston.
15In February, Rondo and Carlisle had a shouting match on the court that resulted in a one-game suspension.
16In Game 2 against the Rockets, Rondo sat for all but the first 34 seconds in the second half.
