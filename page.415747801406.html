1 Killed in Bomb Attack on Pro-Islamic Magazine in Istanbul

1Turkish police say a bomb explosion outside the office of a pro-Islamic magazine in Istanbul has killed one person and injured three others.
2Police said Thursday that the device was placed outside the door of the monthly Adimlar magazine, on the third floor of a building, and exploded late Wednesday when the door was opened.
3Police identified the victim as 45-year-old Unsal Zor, who wrote for Adimlar, a magazine that is close to a Turkish militant Islamic organization.
4There was no immediate claim of responsibility and the motive for the attack was not known.
5Police were investigating.
