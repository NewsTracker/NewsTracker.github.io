Gravestone of Hillary Clinton's dad toppled in Pennsylvania

1SCRANTON, Pa.
2(AP) - The gravestone of Hillary Rodham Clinton's father has been found toppled over at a Pennsylvania cemetery.
3Police say vandals may be responsible for upending the gravestone of Hugh Rodham in the Washburn Street Cemetery in Scranton.
4Police Chief Carl Graziano tells The Times-Tribune (http://bit.ly/1Oc7yGg ) someone at the cemetery reported the stone was tipped over Tuesday, two days after Clinton announced she's running for president.
5The chief says it's unknown how it tipped over but it may have been vandals.
6He says he's "not sure how else it would have fallen over."
7Police say they receive occasional reports of cemetery vandalism.
8Rodham grew up in Scranton.
9He owned a small textiles business in Chicago.
10He died in Little Rock, Arkansas, in 1993 at age 82.
