Apple Music Has More Than 11 Million Listeners

1(Reuters) - Apple Inc s new music streaming service has attracted more than 11 million members during its free trial period, the company said Thursday.
2Apple Music rolled out a three-month free trial period on June 30.
3Nearly 2 million trial members opted for its family plan, which will cost $14.99 a month for up to six family members, the company said.
4Apple also said its App Store saw the largest-ever number of customers in July, yielding $1.7 billion in transactions.
5Apple set a high water mark for App Store customers in China in July.
6It said it has now paid developers almost $33 billion.
