Iraqi army in fierce battle with Islamic State militants after deadly ambush near Fallujah

1BAGHDAD - Iraqi military authorities say there are fierce clashes northeast of Fallujah between the army and Islamic State militants following an ambush Friday that killed a general and staff officers.
2Militants launched a complex ambush involving a bulldozer packed with explosives against a convoy, killing 1st Division commander Brig.
3Gen. Hassan Abbas Toufan and three staff officers in the Nadhem al-Taqseem region south of Lake Tharthar.
4Two Iraqi officials said Saturday that troops have been rushed to the area and are now fighting in this rural region dotted with small villages.
5They spoke on condition of anonymity because they were not authorized to speak to journalists.
6In the past weeks, the Iraqi army has been focusing on pushing Islamic State militants out of Anbar province and has been reporting incremental progress.
