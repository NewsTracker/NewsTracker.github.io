Bali Nine pair await Jakarta's next move

1Mary Jane Fiesta Veloso, who was scheduled to face the firing squad with the Australians, has been granted a judicial review.
2Supreme Court spokesman, Suhadi, told Kompas newspaper the Filipina's case "could take months".

5However the attorney-general's office says it's also considering taking some prisoners off the execution list pending their appeals, meaning they would execute fewer than 10 in the next round.
