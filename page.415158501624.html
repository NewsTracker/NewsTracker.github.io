Suge Knight Tells Judge He Is Going Blind

1Marion "Suge" Knight has told a judge he is suffering from blindness and other health issues.
2The 49-year-old former rap mogul also said in court on Monday that he had sacked the attorneys handling his murder and attempted murder case.
3Knight, who was taken to hospital after the hearing, complained that he was receiving inadequate medical care while in jail.
4The Death Row Records co-founder said he was having difficulty comprehending the proceedings, adding that he had been shot six times last year and had a blood clot in his lungs and other complications.
5Knight also said he had lost 35lbs (16kg) as a result of his injuries.
6Los Angeles Superior Court Judge James Brandlin transferred Knight's case to another judge.
7Knight has pleaded not guilty to murder, attempted murder and hit-and-run charges after he struck two men with his truck, killing one, on 29 January.
8One of the victims, 55-year-old actor Terry Carter, was declared dead at the hospital.
9A second man, 51-year-old Cle "Bone" Sloan, survived.
10Witnesses said Knight got into an argument before allegedly using a red pick-up truck to strike the two men.
11Knight, who is being held without bail, faces up to life in prison if convicted of killing Mr Carter.
