Facebook Messenger rolls out first game 'Doodle Draw'

1Facebook's Messenger platform is rolling out its first game: "Doodle Draw."
2The new drawing game, which is a take on Pictionary and very similar to the mobile game "Draw Something," is the first game to roll out since the platform launched in April.
3"Doodle Draw" let's users send drawings to friends and have them guess what it is.
4The game lets players select a prompt from a word list so they can illustrate it by using multiple colors on a white canvas.
5Users then share the image with their friends who can replay them and reply with their own drawing.
6The game also offers coins that can be used to unlock additional game content such as other colors and words.
7Facebook began by rolling out several other apps for Messenger that focused on GIFs, stickers and memes.
8Now, the social network will focus on expanding its features and integrate games just like its competitor messaging apps do.
9Apps such as WeChat, Line and Viber all have hosted games in their service to enhance the way family and friends communicate.
10"Doodle Draw" is available in the Messenger apps list menu and it's free.
11The game is available for both iOS and Android platforms.
