Obama: Nemtsov killing 'sign of worsening climate in Russia'

1Nemtsov was gunned down near the Kremlin on Friday night in a gangland-style murder.
2"This is an indication of a climate at least inside of Russia in which civil society, independent journalists, people trying to communicate on the internet, have felt increasingly threatened, constrained. 3The US president has called for a full investigation into the slaying of Nemtsov, a critic of President Vladimir Putin and a former deputy prime minister.
4"I have no idea at this point exactly what happened. What I do know is more broadly the fact that freedom of the press, freedom of assembly, freedom of information, basic civil rights and civil liberties inside of Russia are in much worse shape now than they were four or five, 10 years ago," he said.
