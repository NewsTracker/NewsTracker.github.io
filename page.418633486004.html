Man charged with attempted murder after officer stabbed

1A 20-year-old man has been charged with attempted murder after Baltimore police say he stabbed an officer after a domestic dispute inside a student apartment building.
2Police say James Goss stabbed the officer several times in the face and upper body early Saturday morning.
3The officer was taken to a trauma center, where he was in serious but stable condition.
4The stabbing occurred in the lobby of the Varsity Apartments, which houses University of Baltimore students.
5Police say a security officer called 911 after being threatened.
6Police say Goss and his girlfriend had argued over the keys to her apartment.
7Police say Goss punched the woman in the face and placed her in a chokehold.
8A university spokesman says the victim is a student but Goss is not.
