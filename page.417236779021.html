Susan Sarandon encourages tourists to visit earthquake-hit Nepal

1May 24, 2015.
2Oscar-winning Hollywood actress Susan Sarandon, left, comforts Saailee Tamang, who lost her home in the April 25 earthquake, at Ramkot village on the outskirts of Kathmandu, Nepal.
3Oscar-winning Hollywood actress Susan Sarandon is urging tourists to come to Nepal, where two powerful earthquakes in the last month killed thousands of people and made several hundreds of thousands homeless.
4Sarandon is in Nepal for five days, staying with the famed Kung-fu nuns in a Buddhist monastery and later in an orphanage that was damaged in one of the quakes.
5She said people should make reservations to come to Nepal after the rainy season ends in September to help people in the Himalayan nation recover from the disasters.
6A magnitude-7.8 quake struck Nepal on April 25, killing at least 8,490 people.
7A magnitude-7.3 quake on May 12 killed 158.
8Nearly 17,000 people were injured in the two quakes.
