CANNES WATCH: Natalie Portman dazzles in Dior

1The cast of A Tale of Love and Darkness, singer Gilad Kahana, left, and director Natalie Portman look to each other as they arrive for the screening of the film Mia Madre (My Mother) at the 68th international film festival, Cannes, southern France, Saturday, May 16, 2015.
2Actress and director Natalie Portman, center, introduces actor Amir Tessler, second from left, to festival director Thierry Fremaux, third from left, as she and other cast members of her film A Tale of Love and Darkness arrive for the screening of the film Mia Madre (My Mother) at the 68th international film festival, Cannes, southern France, Saturday, May 16, 2015.
3Natalie Portman is at home on the Cannes red carpet, but her appearance Saturday was her first as director as well as star.
4Portman wrote, directed and stars in the Hebrew-language festival entry "A Tale of Love and Darkness."
5She wore an elegant dress from Dior Couture's spring-summer 2015 collection with an A-line skirt in black-patterned pale blue silk and a black strapless bodice.
6The look was topped by sleek, pulled-back hair and white diamond De Grisogono earrings.
7Portman has received lukewarm reviews for the film, a handsomely mounted tale of a young boy coming of age in the newly formed Israeli state in the late 1940s.
8Portman plays the boy's sensitive, imaginative mother in the film, adapted from a novel by Amos Oz.
