Volcano-like underground fire is put out after a month

1WOODBURY, N.J. -
2Crews have put out a fire that burned underground in southern New Jersey for a month, melting the ground around it and reaching 1,000 degrees.
3Woodbury Mayor Bill Volk tells the Courier-Post (http://on.cpsj.com/1CVu2fj ) that the fire was extinguished Thursday, a month after it started during a severe storm.
