Explosions kill 14 in north Cameroon town near Nigeria

1YAOUNDE, Cameroon (AP) - A Cameroon military spokesman says two explosions went off at a bar in a northern town, killing at least 14 people.
2Col.
3Didier Badjeck said Monday three Chadian soldiers were among those killed by the bombs planted by suspected Boko Haram fighters in Fotokol, a town on the border with Nigeria's Borno state, on Sunday.
4He said no Cameroonian soldiers were hurt.
5Resident Abdoulaziz Bambam said the death toll may rise because more bodies are being recovered.
