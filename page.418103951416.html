Baltimore police partially identify officers who killed unarmed man

1Baltimore police patch.
2OWINGS MILLS, Md.
3-- Baltimore County police are identifying three officers who shot and killed an unarmed black man who had threatened to kill himself.
4Officers shot 41-year-old Spencer McCain Thursday in Owings Mills, about 15 miles northwest of Baltimore.
5On Saturday, police identified the officers by last name.
6They are Officer Wilkes, a six-year veteran of the department; Officer Besaw, an eight-year veteran; and Officer Stargel, a five-year veteran.
7A police spokesman says the department cannot release their first names due to a police union contract.
8The officers are on administrative leave.
9Police Chief Jim Johnson said Thursday that three officers all fired as McCain was in a "defensive position."
10Johnson says McCain was holding his arms and body in a manner that suggested he was armed.
11No weapon was found.
