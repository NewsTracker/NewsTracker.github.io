Celtics and Kings to play regular-season game in Mexico City (Yahoo Sports)

1SACRAMENTO, CA - APRIL 13: Sacramento Kings head coach George Karl presents Rudy Gay #8 and DeMarcus Cousins #15 of the Sacramento Kings their USA Championship rings prior to the game against the Los Angeles Lakers at Sleep Train Arena on April 13, 2015 in Sacramento, California.
2NEW YORK (AP) -- The Boston Celtics will play the Sacramento Kings in Mexico City in December, the NBA's third regular-season game in Mexico.
3It will be the league's 22nd game in Mexico overall, the most for any country outside the U.S. and Canada.
4The Dec. 3 game will be the centerpiece of a trip that will also include community programs and interactive fan events.
5The NBA is also planning two preseason games in China between the Charlotte Hornets and Los Angeles Clippers.
6More international games will be announced, the league said.
