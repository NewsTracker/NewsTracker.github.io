18The group has killed hundreds of Egyptian soldiers and police since the army toppled Islamist president Mohamed Mursi in 2013 after mass protests against his rule.

21A few have declared ties to the radical Islamic State and claimed high-profile attacks over recent weeks in what appears to be an intensifying campaign.
22Fears that the crisis in neighboring Libya could spill across the border have prompted Egypt to upgrade its military hardware.
