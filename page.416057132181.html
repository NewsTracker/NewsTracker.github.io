HBO Now available ? now on Apple TV

1We knew that HBO Now was coming to Apple TV.
2Tim Cook promised us as much at last month's Apple Watch event.
3We also knew that it would arrive just before the season premiere of HBO's hit show, Game of Thrones.
4But what we're just finding out is that the app is now available for download on Apple TV.
5Users of Apple's set-top streaming box can commit to a cable-free HBO subscription for $14.99 a month.
6What they'll get with the membership is access to a huge backlog of movies and classic TV shows like The Wire and The Sopranos, as well as new favorites like Veep and True Detective.
7What's more is that new subscribers will get the first month free.
8This is perfect if you just want a taste of the next season of Game of Thrones when it premieres April 12.
9(But how could you stop at just a taste?)
10HBO's cable-free streaming app has landed on the iOS App Store, too.
11By the end of the day (as of this writing), HBO Now should be available on your iPhone and iPad as well.
12And if you're using a Mac, you can access the service through the HBO Now website to boot.
