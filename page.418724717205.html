Technology stocks sink in midday trading after weak showings from Apple, Microsoft and others

1Technology shares are leading the way lower on the stock market after Apple and Microsoft turned in disappointing results.
2Apple was down 5 percent in midday trading Wednesday.
3The company gave a cautious outlook for the current quarter and didn't provide much detail on how its new smartwatch was doing.
4Microsoft fell 3 percent after booking a huge loss related to its purchase of Nokia.
5The Dow Jones industrial average was down 80 points, or 0.5 percent, to 17,840 as of 11:45 a.m. Eastern time.
6The Standard & Poor's 500 index fell five points, or 0.3 percent, to 2,113.
7The Nasdaq composite declined 37 points, or 0.7 percent, to 5,170.
8Bond prices rose.
9The yield on the 10-year Treasury note fell to 2.31 percent.
