U.S., allies conduct 14 air strikes against Islamic State: task force

1WASHINGTON (Reuters) - The United States and its coalition allies have staged eight air strikes against Islamic State militants in Syria and six in Iraq since early on Sunday, the Combined Joint Task Force overseeing the operations said in a statement on Monday.
2All eight air strikes in Syria targeted Islamic State fighting positions, tactical units and a vehicle near Kobani.
3In Iraq, strikes hit targets near Mosul, Ramadi and Sinjar, among other locations, the statement said.
