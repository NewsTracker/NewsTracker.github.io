New plaza funded by Southwest Airlines opens in Baltimore as grant program expands to 6 cities

1BALTIMORE - A new public plaza funded by a grant from Southwest Airlines is opening in Baltimore, and the airline is expanding its grant program for public places to six new cities.
2Southwest's Heart of the Community grants aim to reimagine and reactive underutilized public spaces.
3In Baltimore, a new plaza is opening Wednesday at Pratt and Light streets at the Inner Harbor.
4Baltimore is a former grant recipient.
5On Wednesday, Southwest is announcing the grant program will expand to six new cities this year.
6They are Albuquerque, New Mexico; Fort Myers, Florida; Jacksonville, Florida; Milwaukee, Wisconsin; Portland, Maine; and St. Louis, Missouri.
7Southwest works with the Project for Public Spaces on the grant project, and nonprofit groups in each city administer the grants.
