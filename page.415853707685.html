New Mexico nuclear enrichment plant OK'd for expansion

1The Hobbs News-Sun reports (http://goo.gl/je1FF0) that the panel gave the OK last week on URENCO's most recent license amendment application.
2That means URENCO can double its capacity over the course of two more phases beyond the current three-phase $4 billion facility.
3It also means the company can store more raw and depleted uranium on site and can re-feed uranium tails left over after the enrichment process back through their centrifuges to recover more enriched uranium for nuclear fuel.
