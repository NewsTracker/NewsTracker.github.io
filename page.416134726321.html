Mining co. in Mexico has 'good relationship' with cartels

1CULIACAN, Mexico (AP)?
2The director of the mining company robbed of $8.5 million in gold in Mexico has made the rare revelation it has a "good relationship" with area drug traffickers.
3Rob McEwen of Canada-based McEwen Mining Inc. says company employees ask the drug cartels for permission before they explore.
4McEwen spoke Thursday in a televised interview with Canada's Business News Network.
5He says the cartels sometimes deny access, telling the company to "come back ... we've finished what we're doing."
6He said the company had not suffered any other crime.
