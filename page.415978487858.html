Rights group urges Egypt to halt executions of 6 men

1New York-based Human Rights Watch said in a statement Saturday that the men are accused of being members of the insurgent group Ansar Beit al-Maqdis.
2The rights group says the men were part of a group of nine convicted of killing two officers in a 2014 shootout north of Cairo, and it says they should be given a retrial before a civilian court.
3Since the military ousted an Islamist president in 2013, courts have swiftly dealt out many harsh sentences, including life in prison and death sentences.
