1NEW YORK - President Barack Obama is envisioning a future of playing dominoes with retiree David Letterman.
2Obama joked about their quieter futures during his eighth "Late Show" appearance Monday, saying Americans have grown up with the 33-year veteran comedian.
3"After a tough day at the office or coming home from work, knowing that you've been there to give us a little bit of joy and a little bit of laughter, it has meant so much," Obama said.
4"You're part of all of us. You've given us a great gift and we love you."
5Letterman is filling his CBS show with prominent guests in the lead-up to his final show May 20.
