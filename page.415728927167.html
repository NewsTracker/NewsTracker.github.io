Cubs? Jackson blames Google Maps for missing start

1Cubs righty Edwin Jackson speaks with catcher Miguel Montero during a relief appearance on March 24 in Mesa, Ariz.
2He missed his scheduled start because he couldn't find the A's ballpark.
3Some pitchers have trouble finding the plate.
4Edwin Jackson of the Chicago Cubs had a much bigger problem: He couldn't find the ballpark.
5Jackson missed his spring training start Tuesday against the A's when he drove to the wrong stadium.
6Fitting, too - he once walked eight batters in a no-hitter, and has never been known for great location.
7Jackson took the blame for this errant wind-up.
8The 31-year-old veteran said he typed "Oakland Athletics spring training complex" into the Google Maps app on his phone and it directed him to Phoenix Municipal Stadium.
9Too bad for Jackson, he didn't realize Oakland no longer played there.
10Instead, the A's moved out after last spring and now host games about 5 miles away at Hohokam Stadium.
11"It was my fault for not looking to see where it was," Jackson said.
12Phoenix Muni, as the ballpark is called, is the current base for the Arizona State Sun Devils college team.
13So Jackson headed to Hohokam, which was the Cubs' longtime spring home.
14He eventually got to the right place, not that it helped him.
15He entered the game in the second inning and was tagged for eight runs and nine hits in only 1 2-3 innings of a 14-2 loss.
16Jackson joins an ever-growing list of ballplayers to get lost.
17A few years ago, a couple Cincinnati Reds went to Yankee Stadium when their game against the New York Mets was really at Citi Field.
18Roger Clemens once got mixed up trying to find Montreal's spring stadium in West Palm Beach, Florida.
19And perhaps Braves pitcher Pascual Perez faced the toughest jam.
20In 1982, after getting his driver's license earlier in the day, he famously spent two hours on I-285 circling the city, unable to figure out how to exit to Atlanta-Fulton County Stadium.
21Jackson said his late arrival didn't cause his lousy results.
22"I was already dressed and got here in time to get ready, but they didn't want me to rush things," he said.
23"I came to the stadium with the mindset of being ready. It was a crazy way to start the day."
