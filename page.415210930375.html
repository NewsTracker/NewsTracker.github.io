Californian tried to join Islamic State in Syria: prosecutors

1The suspect, Adam Dandach, 21, of Orange, California, was indicted by a grand jury in Santa Ana, California, the department said in a statement.
2Dandach was arrested in July when he tried to travel to Turkey with what the department said was the intent of crossing into Syria to join Islamic State militants.
3He believed killing U.S. soldiers was justified, the statement said.
4Dandach, who is also known as Fadi Fadi Dandach, is charged with attempting to aid a foreign terrorist organization, lying on a passport application in order to aid terrorism and obstruction of justice.
5The grand jury indictment supersedes the original one in July, in which he was charged with lying on a passport application.
6He has pleaded not guilty and is being held without bond.
