Student Shot in Boko Haram School Attack Dies in Nigeria

1One of five students wounded by gunfire in a Boko Haram attack on a school in northeast Nigeria died of his injuries on Monday, the college said.
2Musa Umar Gamari had been shot in the chest and was buried on Monday, in accordance with Islamic custom, according to a notice posted at the College of Business and Administrative Studies in Potiskum town.
3He is survived by his wife and one child, it said.
4A suicide bomber and a gunman attacked the school on Saturday with gunfire and two explosions before they were overcome by police and soldiers.
5A man arrested at the scene was later found to be innocent and released, police said.
6It was the first school attack reported since a 3-month-old multinational offensive drove the Islamic extremists out of towns seized last year.
7Nigeria's military says the insurgents' main fighting force is hemmed into the Sambisa Forest, where they are being pursued.
8Some 700 girls and women held in captivity by the militants have been freed as soldiers have destroyed about 20 forest camps.
9The nickname Boko Haram means "Western education is sinful."
