Patriots' Tom Brady doesn't practice day before hearing (Yahoo Sports)

1New England Patriots quarterback Tom Brady looks for a receiver during the first half of an NFL preseason football game against the Green Bay Packers, Thursday, Aug. 13, 2015, in Foxborough, Mass.
2(AP) -- Tom Brady wasn't on the practice field with the New England Patriots on Tuesday, a day before the court hearing for the NFL and the players' union.
3Both sides are due in federal court for the hearing Wednesday in New York with Judge Richard M. Berman over the quarterback's four-game suspension by NFL Commissioner Roger Goodell.
4Berman has been trying to move the sides toward a settlement of the dispute over deflated footballs.
5If there is no settlement, Berman will decide whether Brady's suspension remains in place at the start of the season.
6Brady participated fully in practice on Monday.
7Second-year quarterback Jimmy Garoppolo and recently signed veteran backup Ryan Lindley handled the snaps on Tuesday.
8''Brady is a key part of this team, a leader,'' said Patriots cornerback Malcolm Butler.
9''There's nothing like having Brady out there.
10Lindley was signed Aug. 10 after the team released veteran Matt Flynn.
11''He comes to work,'' Lindley said of Brady.
12''I think the biggest thing, though, that has impressed me is his leadership.
13Butler took advantage of the no-Brady day, nearly picking off a pass during 11-on-11 walkthrough drills.
14''I was going to catch it, but then again I said, 'Ah, it's walkthrough,' and I moved my hands.
15Butler said his near pick drew an on-field comment from coach Bill Belichick.
16''He just told me we needed that in a big-time situation,'' Butler said.
17NOTES: Offensive lineman Bryan Stork was present at practice, but was not in uniform.
