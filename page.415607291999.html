Spring Movie Preview 2015

1Though seasonal blossoms and warm days may still be a distant dream for many, the box office is heating up with a slew of blockbusters and indie surprises guaranteed to make you forget all about that stubborn patch of ice on your lawn that refuses to melt.
2From highly-anticipated sequels ("Insurgent," "Furious 7") to moving dramas ("Age of Adaline," "The Longest Ride," "Do You Believe?"
3So take a break from all the cold-weather talk and settle back and enjoy.
4Shailene Woodley, Theo James and Kate Winslet return in this follow to "Divergent."
5On the run from the power-hungry Erudites, Tris (Woodley) and Four (James) venture into the ruins of Chicago in search of answers, and most importantly, allies as they discover what transpired in the past may very well hold the key to the future of their world.
6"Insurgent" is the second movie based on the best-selling trilogy of young adult science fiction novels by Veronica Roth.
7WATCH: Latest "Insurgent" Trailer "Insurgent," starring Shailene Woodley, Theo James and Kate Winslet, arrives in theaters on March 20, 2015.
8"Do You Believe?"
9An ensemble drama about people facing crises of the soul, "Do You Believe?" stars Cybill Shepherd, Lee Majors, rapper Schwayze, Valerie Dominguez, Mira Sorvino and Ted McGinley as troubled characters whose lives intersect in the shadow of a large cross overlooking the Chicago River.
10From faith-based filmmakers Pure Flix, "Believe?" is a follow-up to the 2014 surprise hit "God's Not Dead."
11WATCH "Do You Believe?"
12Trailer "Do You Believe?" hits theaters March 20.
13Sean Penn is back on the big screen in this action thriller as former special-ops agent James Terrier.
14Battling PTSD, Terrier embarks on romantic quest to reconnect with his longtime love only to discover he has suddenly become the target of international hit-men.
15To find out why he is marked for death, Terrier must dig deep into his top-secret past.
16WATCH "The Gunman" Trailer "The Gunman," starring Sean Penn, hits theaters on March 20.
17Two of comedy's brightest stars join forces for this fish-out-water tale.
18Will Ferrell plays James, a fraudulent hedge fund manager who is given 30 days to get his affairs in order before reporting to San Quentin where he will be incarcerated.
19Kevin Hart plays Darnell, the man James turns to for instruction on how to "get hard" before being locked up.
20Little does James realize that Darnell is a hard-working small business owner who has never found himself on the wrong side of the law, let alone in prison.
21WATCH "Get Hard" Trailer "Get Hard," starring Kevin Hart and Will Ferrell, lands in theaters March 27.
22Jim Parsons, Rihanna, Steve Martin and Jennifer Lopez lend their voices to this animated tale about an alien called Oh (Parsons).
23On the run from his hive-minded race who have taken over the Earth and relocated the human population, Oh encounters Tip (Rihanna), a young girl who has so far eluded the conquering aliens.
24and set out to find Tip's relocated mother.
25"Home" Trailer "Home," featuring the voices of Jim Parsons, Rihanna, Steve Martin and Jennifer Lopez, lands in theaters March 27.
26The gang's all here for the next installment of this speed-obsessed franchise.
27When a dead man's brother seeks revenge on the Toretto gang, series stars Vin Diesel, Paul Walker and Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson jump into the driver's seat.
28Also along for the chase are returning favorites Chris "Ludacris" Bridges, Jordana Brewster, Michelle Rodriguez, Tyrese Gibson, Elsa Pataky and Lucas Black.
29In a tragic turn of events, Walker was killed in an unconnected car accident while filming "Furious 7."
30WATCH "Furious 7" Trailer "Furious 7," starring Vin Diesel and Paul Walker, crashes into theaters April 3.
31Scott Eastwood (son of Clint) stars as Luke, a champion bull rider looking to make a comeback in this cross-generational love story from "The Notebook" scribe Nicholas Sparks.
32When Luke falls for Sophia (Britt Robinson), a college graduate with her sights set on her New York City-based dream job, the couple must overcome their ideals and amend their future plans.
33Inspiring and encouraging their love is Ira (Alan Alda), an ailing romantic who shares memories of his decades-long relationship.
34WATCH "The Longest Ride" Trailer "The Longest Ride" arrives in theaters April 10.
35Everybody's favorite mall cop Paul Blart (Kevin James) is back in this sequel to the 2009 comedy that grossed more than $146 million at the domestic box office.
36Even mall cops need a break and we find Blart, along with his teenage daughter (Raini Rodriguez), heading to Las Vegas for some well-earned r&r and to attend a security-guard expo.
37While there, Blart inadvertently stumbles across a heist and must single-handidly thwart the crooks.
38And yes, the Segway also returns for the sequel.
39Set in a grim USSR outpost in the 1950s, this bleak suspense thriller stars Tom Hardy as a degraded military officer who, along with his wife (Noomi Rapace) and a general (Gary Oldman), set out to uncover who or what is responsible for a string of child murders.
40"Child 44" also stars Joel Kinnaman, Charles Dance, Vincent Cassel and Jason Clarke.
41WATCH "Child 44" Trailer "Child 44," starring Tom Hardy, Noomi Rapace and Gary Oldman, hits theaters on April 17.
42In this follow-up to 2014's "Bears," Disney's latest real nature story focuses on Maya, a South Asia monkey who welcomes her newborn son Kip into her extended family.
43When their jungle home is taken over by rival, neighboring monkeys, Maya, Kip and their group must relocate to new human-infested environs before an attempt to reclaim their home can be undertaken.
44Narrated by Tina Fey ("30 Rock"), "Monkey Kingdom" is the eighth installment of Disneynature's True Life Adventure series.
45Oscar-winner Russell Crowe directs and stars in this sweeping melodrama about a grieving Australian farmer (Crowe), who travels to Turkey in search of his three sons missing in action after the WWI battle at Gallipoli.
46In order to overcome bureaucratic military road blocks, Crowe's farmer arrives on the shores of Gallipoli in a fishing boat where he meets a senior Turkish military officer.
47Exploring the heartbreak of war from both sides of the battle lines, the two form an unlikely team as they search for information about the missing soldiers.
48After a freak accident in 1935, Adaline Bowman (Blake Lively) has lived a solitary existence.
49Miraculously, she has not aged since that fateful day when, at 29 years of age, her ability to grow physically older was halted.
50Never allowing herself to become close to anyone lest they discover her secret, Adaline steps out of the shadows when she meets and falls for a charismatic philanthropist (Michel Huisman).
51But a weekend trip to meet his parents threatens to reveal her decades-old secret.
52"Adaline" also stars Harrison Ford, Kathy Baker and Ellen Burstyn.
53WATCH "The Age of Adaline" Trailer "The Age of Adaline," starring Blake Lively and Harrison Ford, arrives in theaters April 24.
