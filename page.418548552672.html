Apple releases surprise iPod Touch refresh

1Apple is refreshing its iPod Touch music player for the first time in nearly three years, as the company seeks to make music a central part of its devices once again.
2The new Touch has a faster processor and better cameras, plus a software update that enables Apple Music, a $10-a-month service that offers unlimited playback of millions of songs.
3Apple Music launched June 30 as music fans increasingly embrace subscriptions over pay-per-song services such as Apple's industry-leading iTunes.
4The iPod was Apple's first successful foray beyond personal computers.
5Interest has waned, though, as many people now use smartphones such as the iPhone to listen to music.
