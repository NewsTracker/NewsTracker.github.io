1PS4 and Xbox One have had more games announced to being launching in the next 12 months, but you'll be surprised to hear which ones will be console exclusives.
2And when we say 'console exclusive' we of course mean that you can already play them somewhere else but will only be available on one living room gaming device.
3UPDATE MARCH: Elite: Dangerous will be released on PS4 and Mac, with the Xbox One launched a timed exclusive.
4Frontier chief David Braben confirmed the new on Twitter, writing: "Mac of course, then down the line we will support more, including PS4. The XB1 is a timed exclusive."
5Original: Announced at GDC 2015, Frontier Developments David Braben explained the decision to keep just for Xbox fans.
6"I think there were lots of factors - it was a hard decision, they're both great platforms," Braben told Gamespot.
7"We've had a long relationship with Microsoft.
8"We also had a launch title for Xbox One, so our tools and technology has been battle-hardened on Xbox One already.
9"We have a lot of experienced people at the studio who know Xbox One intimately, so it actually is a very logical choice for us."
10PS4 fans will be able to get their hands on Wasteland 2, however, which is coming to both consoles this summer.
11inXile entertainment announced the project this week, with the role-playing sequel, with PC fans being teased with news surrounding the port.
12inXile's Brian Fargo said: "Ever since Wasteland 2 debuted on PC, we've had RPG fans asking us to bring it to console.
13"Thanks to Unity 5 and some extra time, we're able to overhaul the game with really fantastic visual effects, upgraded environment art, physically based shading, add all new character models, a new Quirks and Perks systems, Precision Strikes (aimed shots) and new voice over work for some of the key characters.
14"And if you are backer of Wasteland 2 or have already purchased it on PC, fear not," he said.
15"We will announce great news for you later."
16The new games coming to Xbox One via ID@Xbox have also been revealed, with Shovel Knight and Goat Simulator making the list.
17Having made debut on Wii U, 3DS and Steam last year, Shovel Knight is now coming to PS4, Xbox One, PS3 and Vita.
18Yacht Club Games have announced Battletoads will be featured in Shovel Knight on Xbox One, following in the footsteps of God of War protagonist Kratos being available for PS4 and PS3 (released on April 21).
19Goat Simulator remains to be launched on just the Xbox One, following its success on PC.
20Bethesda have announced a new prequel project that will be available to buy on the Xbox One, PC and PS4.
21Wolfenstein: The Old Blood is a standalone prequel launching on May 5.
22Developed by MachineGames, the title is priced at 14.99 for both online and retail copies.
23The Old Blood focuses on hero BJ Blazkowicz going back to the year 1946 in an adventure that spans eight chapters and two interconnected stories (released together) that will lead into the events from The New Order.
24Rock Band 4 is also coming to Xbox One and PS4.
25The music series returns later this year and will be the only new Rock Band game to feature on the new consoles.
26"We don't envision a Rock Band 5, 6, 7 or 8," Harmonix product manager Daniel Sussman said.
27"What we would rather do is build on the Rock Band 4 feature set through very deliberate content and title updates that are developed in collaboration and conversation with our community."
28Stealth Inc studio and indie developer Ludo Land have announced that puzzle-platformer, Four Sided Fantasy, is to be launched on PS4, Xbox One, Wii U, PS3 and PS Vita.
29The new game is slated for earlier 2016 release.
30"I'm very pleased to be working with Curve Digital to bring my game to all major platforms at once," said Ludo Land's Logan Fieth.
31"Curve handling the console versions means that I can spend my time working on the game's development and puzzles and not need to worry about how to optimize and publish across several completely different platforms."

13BAGA SOLA, Chad (AP) - Kellou Abakar knew she was in trouble as the contractions started not long after an Islamic extremist group attacked her town in Nigeria.
14Her husband was nowhere to be found, and so she pulled her 4-year-old son onto her back and grabbed her two little girls by the hand.
15The 30-year-old pregnant woman ran as fast as she could to escape the Jan. 3 attack on her hometown of Baga.
16It was one of the worst massacres ever carried out by Boko Haram during its five-year insurgency.
17The jihadist group seeking to establish an Islamic caliphate is believed to have killed hundreds that January day, and Abakar still doesn't know whether her husband is among the dead more than two months later.
18Three of her other children disappeared in the chaos that ensued as the militants opened fire indiscriminately and threw people into the burning homes that had been set ablaze.
19The family made its way four hours on foot, and by the time she got to the shores of Lake Chad to board a boat to safety in neighboring Chad, she was too far along in her labor to get in.
20She gave birth in Nigeria.
21As soon as Aboubakar was born, she and the children got into a boat.
22"If I had stayed there they would have killed me too," she said softly inside a tent at a refugee camp now home to more than 6,000 Nigerians who have fled Boko Haram's violence.
23Abakar and her children arrived this week at the camp after taking refuge in several other villages.
24The camp on the edge of the Sahara Desert is jointly run by the UN and the Chadian government.
25As she sat breastfeeding Aboubakar, her older children played on the sandy floor, their cheerful voices drowned out by the wind rattling the tarp overhead.
26She hopes her husband will approve of the name she chose for him so he could get identity documents as a newborn refugee.
27There was no naming ceremony, no father was present to buy a sheep to slaughter in sacrifice.
28She named the child after his grandfather - for now.
29Many here hope their loved ones are not dead, but rather among those still hiding on the dozens of islands that dot Lake Chad which borders Chad, Nigeria and parts of Cameroon and Niger.
30The Chadian military tries to protect those fleeing here but even so, several weeks ago, Boko Haram militants aboard motorized boats attacked the peninsula of N'gouboua inside Chad, killing at least eight civilians.
31Since then, families have been brought from there to the camp where UNICEF offers health service and school and activities for the children.
32The families receive food from humanitarian agencies and are assigned tents erected in neat blocks.
33They seek shade from the desert sun under acacia trees.
34Refugees take water from a well.
35Men riding camels through the region also come to the well.
36Chadian authorities believe more than 2,000 people remain trapped on islands, awaiting transport to a refugee camp in one of the world's poorest countries.
37Some 100,000 Nigerians have fled to in neighboring Niger, with roughly another 60,000 in Cameroon.
38Mahamat Abakar, 60, last saw his wife and eight children two months ago as they divided themselves between two small wooden boats.
39Upon arriving at this refugee camp, he was told that one of his sons was here too.
40When he saw him, he sobbed.
41The 10-year-old said the boat he was on was sinking and the man paddling it ordered him and another child out.
42They clung onto tree branches until people in another boat spotted them and brought them to safety.
43As a Muslim, the father has only anger for people who claim they are carrying out attacks in the name of Islam.
