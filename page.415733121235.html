Mexico says arson at US company linked to extortion

1The attorney general in the Gulf coast state of Tabasco, Fernando Valenzuela, said that five attackers forced their way into the company's yard and burned three trucks.
2"Unfortunately, the motive was extortion," Valenzuela told the Radio Formula station.
3He confirmed local media accounts that the attackers said the arson was punishment for having ignored demands for protection payments.
4Thousands of smaller Mexican companies have been subject to such extortion demands by criminal gangs, but larger, foreign-owned firms have seldom been acknowledged as targets.
5However, repeated calls to the company's U.S. and Mexican offices went unanswered.
