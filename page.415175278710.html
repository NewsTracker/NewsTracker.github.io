Ukraine hikes benchmark interest rate to 30 percent

1National Bank chairwoman Valeria Gontareva said Tuesday that the measure was aimed at tempering panic in the currency market and reining in inflation.
2Gontareva has previously projected that inflation in Ukraine this year will reach 26 percent.
3Market watchers consider that a low estimate.
4The rate increase is to take effect Wednesday.
5Ukraine's central bank last raised the refinancing rate last month, from 14 percent.
