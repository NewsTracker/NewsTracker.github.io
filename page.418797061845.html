Palestinian official assassinated in refugee camp in Lebanon

1Lebanese security officials say gunmen have ambushed and killed a Palestinian official in a refugee camp in southern Lebanon.
2They say Talal al-Urduni, a senior security official with the mainstream Palestinian Fatah movement, died Saturday in a hail of gunfire along with his bodyguard after they were intercepted by the gunmen near the Nidaa hospital in a neighborhood of the camp.
3Lebanese security officials say Islamic extremists in the camp were likely behind the assassination Saturday.
4They spoke on condition of anonymity because they were not authorized to give official statements.
5The assassination triggered heavy gunfire inside the camp, which could be heart in the southern port of Sidon.
6Assassinations are common in Ein el-Hilweh, Lebanon's largest Palestinian refugee camp and a haven for militants and fugitives.
