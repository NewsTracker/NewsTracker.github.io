Ferguson voters go to polls to elect 3 City Council members

1Three of the six council seats are up for grabs in the St. Louis County town where 18-year-old Michael Brown, who was black, was fatally shot by a white Ferguson police officer in August.
2Officer Darren Wilson was cleared of wrongdoing by a grand jury and the Justice Department, but a separate DOJ report blasted Ferguson for racial bias and profiling in the police department and a profit-driven municipal court system.
3Fallout from that report led to the resignation of the city manager, police chief, municipal judge and two police officers.
4The municipal court clerk was fired for racist emails.
