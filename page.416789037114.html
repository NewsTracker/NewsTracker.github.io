3 accused of operating fake police department that claimed jurisdiction in 33 states, Mexico

2Sheriff's officials said Tuesday that Brandon Kiel, David Henry and Tonette Hayes were taken into custody last week on suspicion of impersonating police officers through their roles in the Masonic Fraternal Police Department.
