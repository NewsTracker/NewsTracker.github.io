Nicaragua asks US to remove embassy worker who had been in Liberia over Ebola concern

1Carlos Saenz, an official at Nicaragua's health ministry, told reporters Monday the U.S. was asked to help transport the unnamed 51-year-old worker to the United States.
2Saenz says the embassy worker was quarantined.
3The government is examining people who had contact with the worker.
4The U.S. embassy in Managua said in a statement that the employee was in Liberia, but denied there had been any contact with Ebola patients.
5It said the worker had been examined at the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention before traveling to Nicaragua.
