CANNES WATCH: Toni Garrn sparkles in silver on red carpet

1Model Toni Garrn poses for photographers upon arrival for the screening of the film The Little Prince at the 68th international film festival, Cannes, southern France, Friday, May 22, 2015.
2CANNES, France (AP) - German model Toni Garrn brought a touch of little mermaid to the Cannes red carpet for "The Little Prince" in a sparkly, swanky silver gown that trailed elegantly to the floor, with bare shoulders and a crossover cutaway midriff.
3Glittering fairytale elements infused several looks at Friday's premiere of the animated film, based on Antoine de Saint-Exupery's classic children's tale.
4Teenage "Twilight" actress Mackenzie Foy, who voices the central role of the Little Girl, was a picture of poised perfection in a glimmering princess-like frock.
