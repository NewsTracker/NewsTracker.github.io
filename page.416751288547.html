Foreign rescue teams start leaving Nepal, NDRF teams to return today

1NEW DELHI: The NDRF teams in Nepal will return to their headquarters in Patna on Tuesday, a day after Nepal called off serach and rescue operations and urged all 34 nations to withdraw their teams from the country.
2Rescue workers from different nations have started leaving Nepal.
3Cabinet secretary is scheduled to chair a meeting of National Crisis Management Commission today.
4Nepal government on Monday urged foreign rescue workers in the quake-hit capital to return home.
5Since the April 25 earthquake, 4,050 rescue workers from 34 different nations reached to Nepal to help in rescue operations, provide emergency medical care and distribute food and other necessities.
6The death toll from the quake, Nepal's worst in more than 80 years, has crossed 7,000.
