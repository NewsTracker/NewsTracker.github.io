Ex-CIA officer get 3.5 years for NY Times leak

1Former CIA officer Jeffrey Sterling was sentenced Monday to 3.5 years in prison for leaking classified information to a New York Times journalist.
2Sterling was convicted in January of revealing details of a US attempt during president Bill Clinton's administration to undermine Iran's suspected nuclear weapons program.
3"The court has to impose a very clear message," US District Judge Leonie Brinkema said at Sterling's sentencing in the Washington suburb of Alexandria, Virginia.
