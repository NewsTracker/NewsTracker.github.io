Three Greece players suffer minor injuries after being involved in fatal car crash following Euro 2016 qualifier with Hungary

1Three Greece internationals suffered minor injuries after being involved in a fatal car crash in Budapest on Monday following their Euro 2016 qualifier against Hungary.
2Panagiotis Tachtsidis, Vangelis Moras and Ioannis Fetfatzidis, who were travelling to the airport by taxi to return to their Italian clubs, were taken to hospital after a motorway collision in which the driver of the other vehicle involved was killed.
3'All three are in good health, contrary to reports claiming serious injuries,' the Hellenic Football Federation EPO said in a statement.
4Moras and Tachtsidis have already been released from a Budapest hospital while Fetfatzidis was expected to be released later Monday.
5Fetfatzidis reportedly received stitches to the head and Tachtsidis had stitches in a knee wound.
6Moras and Tachtsidis play for Hellas Verona in Italy, while Fetfatzidis plays for Serie A rival Chievo Verona.
7Greece and Hungary drew 0-0 Sunday in a qualifying match for the 2016 European Championship.
