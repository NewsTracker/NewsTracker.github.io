Armed robbers steal $11million of gold from refinery in Mexico

1A company statement says the theft occurred Tuesday at the El Gallo 1 mine and involved 900 kilograms of gold-bearing concentrate containing approximately 7,000 ounces of gold.
2The company said that the crime was being investigated by Mexican authorities.
3It said its insurance policies will not be enough to cover the losses.
4The statement said no one was injured and mining operations were not affected, but did not provide any more details about the robbery.
5The Toronto-based company has operations in Mexico, Nevada and Argentina.
