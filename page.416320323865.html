Recap: Boston vs. Baltimore

1Boston, MA (SportsNetwork.com) - Xander Bogaerts singled home Mike Napoli with the winning run in the bottom of the ninth as Boston clipped Baltimore, 3-2, in the opener of a four-game set from Fenway Park.
2Ryan Hanigan slugged a two-run shot for the Red Sox, while Bogaerts finished with a pair of hits.
3Koji Uehara (1-0) struck out two in a scoreless top of the ninth.
4Caleb Joseph went deep for the Orioles, who extended their major-league best home run total to 16 in defeat.
5Brian Matusz (0-1) walked three and was charged with the deciding run in his 1 1/3 relief frames.
