Islamic State affiliate claims Libya bombings that killed 12

1The Islamic State affiliate claimed responsibility for the Tuesday bombings in a post on an Internet bulletin board site known to be used by the group and its sympathizers.
2The claim included a picture of an alleged Tunisian suicide bomber it said was involved in the attack.
3A Libyan security official said Wednesday that the bombings targeted forces of Libya's elected government and allied fighters in Benghazi.
4He said the blasts killed 12 people and wounded 25.
