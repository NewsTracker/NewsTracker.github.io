Funeral set for Baltimore man who died in police custody

1BALTIMORE (AP) - Funeral plans are set for Freddie Gray, the Baltimore man who died a week after he was critically injured in police custody.
2A wake will be held Sunday.
3The funeral is set for 11 a.m. Monday at New Shiloh Baptist Church.
4Gray was arrested on April 12 and loaded into a police van.
5At some point, he suffered an unexplained spinal injury while in police custody.
6He died last Sunday.
7Local and federal authorities are investigating the death.
8Six officers have been suspended with pay.
9Numerous marches and rallies have been held over the last week to protest Gray's death.
