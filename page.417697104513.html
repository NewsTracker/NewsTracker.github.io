Argentine businessmen wanted in FIFA corruption investigation petition to remain free

1Two Argentine businessmen wanted in a FIFA bribery investigation are petitioning an appeals court to remain free while officials consider U.S. requests for their extradition.
2Lawyers for Hugo Jinkis and his son Mariano said Thursday their petition had been presented to the Federal Court of Appeals.
3The two are among 14 soccer and business officials indicted last month by the U.S. Department of Justice on charges of bribery, racketeering and money-laundering.
4The U.S has requested their extradition, which can be a lengthy process in Argentina.
5Lawyers Francisco Castex and Jorge Anzorreguy say their clients remain in Argentina but didn't provide more details.
