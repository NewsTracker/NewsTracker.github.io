FIFA Women's World Cup: Colombia upsets France

1Colombia goalkeeper Sandra Sepulveda makes a save against France as the underdogs shut out highly touted France, 2-0, to take over the lead in Group F.
2Lady Andrade and Catalina Usme scored goals as Colombia stunned third-ranked France 2-0 Saturday in one of the biggest upsets in Women's World Cup history.
3The underdog Colombians opened the scoring in the 19th minute.
4Andrade broke free from a pair of defenders for a tap-in goal.
5Usme clinched her country's first World Cup win with a goal on the counter attack in the 93rd minute.
6The French lost despite outshooting Colombia 21-3.
7France was unlucky as well.
8Colombian defender Daniela Montoya appeared to touch the ball with her hand inside the penalty area in the 67th minute, but a foul wasn't called.
9France wraps up group play against Mexico on Wednesday, while the Colombians face England.
