One Direction's Louis Tomlinson launches own record label

1The 23-year-old singer's new venture is being backed by Sony and is an imprint of Cowell's Syco label.
2Tomlinson tells Britain's The Sun newspaper, "This is something I've wanted to do for some time. To be given the opportunity to potentially give new artists a platform is an incredible feeling. I hope to sign some really exciting new artists down the line."
3His first signing is U.K. X Factor finalist Jack Walton, and Tomlinson is planning to to hold auditions for a new girl band.
