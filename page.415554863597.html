Baltimore Ravens RB Pierce charged with drunken driving

1The Baltimore Ravens released running back Bernard Pierce on Wednesday, hours after he was charged with drunken driving.

3Police spokesman Cpl.

7On Wednesday afternoon, the Ravens announced that Pierce, 23, had been cut in a press release consisting of one sentence.
