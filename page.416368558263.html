2PHILADELPHIA (AP) -- It's Tebow Time in the City of Brotherly Love.
3Tim Tebow is expected to sign a one-year contract with the Eagles on Monday, according to three people familiar with the deal.
4The people spoke to The Associated Press on condition of anonymity Sunday because the contract hasn't been finalized.
5Tebow hasn't played in the NFL since 2012 with the New York Jets.
6He was released by the New England Patriots before the 2013 season and spent last year working in television as an analyst for the SEC Network and ESPN.
7Despite being out of the league, the 27-year-old Tebow remained one of the most popular players around.
8He has a legion of fans who follow him because of his strong Christian beliefs.
9The former Heisman Trophy winner led Florida to two national titles and was a first-round pick by Denver in 2010.
10He started 16 games during two seasons with the Broncos, including a playoff victory over Pittsburgh in January 2012.
11Tebow was traded to the Jets after Denver signed Peyton Manning.
12The 27-year-old Tebow joins quarterbacks Sam Bradford, Mark Sanchez, Matt Barkley and G.J. Kinne on Philadelphia's roster.
13The addition of Tebow would be the latest move in a stunning offseason for coach Chip Kelly.
14He traded Nick Foles, LeSean McCoy and allowed Jeremy Maclin to leave in free agency.
15Tebow had some success in Denver, but his inaccurate passing and lack of pocket presence was an issue.
16His strength has been running the ball or improvising.
17Tebow has completed just 47.9 percent of his passes for 2,422 yards, 17 touchdowns and nine interceptions.
18He has 989 yards rushing, a 5.0 average yards per carry and 12 TDs.
19Tebow worked with former major league pitcher Tom House on improving his mechanics.
20He joins the Eagles in time to start their offseason program this week.
