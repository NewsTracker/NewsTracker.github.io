CANNES TREND: It's all eyes on shoulders on red carpet

1CANNES (AP) - It's all eyes on shoulders at the Cannes Film Festival, with red-carpet stars ditching the bust in favor of accentuating the upper torso and shoulders - through embellishments or bare skin.
2Charlize Theron wowed crowds at the French Riviera's "Mad Max: Fury Road" premiere in a black Valentino dress with a horizontal tulle section across her shoulders.
3Emma Stone repeated this coup in a black Oscar de la Renta lace dress with a sheer band across the upper torso at the "Irrational Man" photo call.
4Rachel Weisz chanelled a similar style but in white for "The Lobster" premiere - in a Louis Vuitton gown with a sequined section that drew eyes up to her shoulders.
5Elsewhere, the trend was for shoulders to be sexily exposed with truncated gowns - with the top parts lopped off - like Natalie Portman's delicate Dior strapless gown in black and pale blue.
6One of the best shoulder looks was comeback kid Sienna Miller who shone in a speckled gray Balenciaga off-the-shoulder dress from Fall 2015 with a band that accentuated her enviable upper torso.
7The secret for shoulders is: Toned, but not too skinny!
