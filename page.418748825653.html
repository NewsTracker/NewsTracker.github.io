Maple Leafs hire longtime Devils exec Lou Lamoriello as GM (Yahoo Sports)

1TORONTO (AP) -- The Toronto Maple Leafs have hired longtime NHL executive Lou Lamoriello as general manager.
2Lamoriello resigned as New Jersey Devils president to take the job.
3Maple Leafs President Brendan Shanahan said Thursday he wanted NHL experience, and he got almost three decades of it in Lamoriello.
4The 72-year-old Lamoriello stepped down as Devils general manager this summer when the team hired Ray Shero.
5New Jersey won three Stanley Cups during Lamoriello's stay.
6The Maple Leafs fired general manager Dave Nonis after missing the playoffs for the second straight season.
