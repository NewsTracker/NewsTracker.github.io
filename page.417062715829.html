Texas man dies in paraglider crash in northern New Mexico

1ANGEL FIRE, N.M. (AP) - Authorities say a Texas man is dead after his paraglider crashed outside a northern New Mexico airport.
2New Mexico State Police say 46-year-old Paul Johnson was found in a field just north of Angel Fire Airport on Saturday morning.
3The Office of the Medical investigator pronounced him dead at the scene.
4According to the friend, Johnson had landed in a field and was going to attempt a lift-off and return to the airport.
5Authorities say the cause of the crash is unknown.
