Big Euro teams to avoid small World Cup qualifying groups

1FIFA says the most commercially attractive European national teams should go into six-team World Cup qualifying groups.
2FIFA says a UEFA proposal wants England, France, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands and Spain to avoid being drawn into groups containing five teams.
3It would allow popular teams to play the most matches, and increase the value of broadcasting rights which UEFA sells on behalf of member associations.
4Teams in the seven six-nation groups will play 10 qualifying matches.
5Teams in the two five-nation groups play only eight qualifiers.
6UEFA says it applied the same proposal to the 2016 European Championship qualifying draw.
7FIFA's World Cup organizing committee must approve the proposal one day before the July 25 draw in St. Petersburg, Russia.
