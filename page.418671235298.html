FIFA presidential election to be held on Feb. 26

1FIFA will hold a presidential election on Feb. 26, giving Sepp Blatter seven more months in power before leaving the scandal-tainted governing body.
2FIFA is reeling under the weight of American and Swiss criminal investigations into corruption, which led to 14 officials being indicted in May when Blatter won a fifth term.
3FIFA's 209 members will return to Zurich next year to select a new president almost than nine months after Blatter's resignation statement.
