Egypt, Ethiopia and Sudan sign accord over Nile waters

1Egyptian President Abdel-Fattah el-Sissi, Sudanese President Omar Bashir and Ethiopian Prime Minister Hailemariam Desalegn welcomed the agreement in speeches in Khartoum's Republican Palace on Monday.
2They earlier watched a short film about the Grand Renaissance Dam that highlighted how it could benefit the region.
3Officials from the three countries had worked out the agreement beforehand in recent meetings in Khartoum.
4Egypt previously had voiced fears that Ethiopia's $4.2 billion hydro-electric project would diminish its share of the Nile, which provides almost all of the desert nation's water needs.
