Spanish lawmakers overwhelmingly approve 3rd Greek bailout, country to supply about 10B euros

1MADRID - Spanish lawmakers have overwhelmingly approved the government's support for the 86 billion euro ($93 billion) bailout package for Greece.
2Members of the nation's lower house of parliament voted 297-20 in support of the third Greek bailout already backed by the administration of Prime Minister Mariano Rajoy.
3Tuesday's vote was largely symbolic because Rajoy's Popular Party holds an absolute majority in Parliament.
4Spain will supply 10.1 billion euros to the Greek bailout.
5Its officials have been hawkish in demanding that Greece abide by strict terms such as budget cuts and reforms.
6Spain asked for and received a bailout of its hurting banks in 2012.
