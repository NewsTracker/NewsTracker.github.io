Croatian man kidnapped in Egypt; said to be energy worker

1ZAGREB, Croatia (AP) - A Croatian man has been kidnapped in Egypt by an unidentified armed group, Croatia's foreign ministry said Friday.
2The man was abducted Wednesday in Cairo while traveling to work for a French company, the foreign ministry said in a statement.
3It did not name the company.
4The statement said an armed group stopped the company car, forced the man out and drove away.
5The man was identified only by his initials T.S. , born in 1984.
6The foreign ministry said it has contacted the man's family and Egyptian authorities.
7An Egyptian security official said the abducted man is a petroleum engineer working for a multinational company.
8The official, who spoke on condition of anonymity because he is not authorized to speak to the media, said gunmen stopped the man's car on a highway west of Cairo and took him away.
9The car, its driver and the man's belongings were left behind, the official said.
10He added that a police investigation is underway.
