Obama meets with family of reporter killed by Islamic State

1MIAMI (AP) - President Barack Obama has met with the family of an American journalist who was killed last year by the Islamic State group.
2Steven Sotloff was a 31-year-old Miami-area native who freelanced for Time and Foreign Policy magazines.
3He had vanished in Syria, and then last September, militants released a video showing Sotloff's beheading.
4The White House says that during a trip to Florida on Thursday, Obama met with the reporter's parents, Art and Shirley Sotloff, and sister Lauren.
5The White House says Obama appreciated hearing more about Sotloff's work as a journalist and that the family spoke of Sotloff's passion for reporting the stories of people who are suffering.
6Obama also acknowledged the foundation the family has formed in Sotloff's memory to support journalists reporting from conflict torn areas.
