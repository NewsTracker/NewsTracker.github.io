Robert Durst pleads not guilty to firearm charges

1NEW ORLEANS - Millionaire real estate heir Robert Durst has pleaded not guilty to two weapons charges in a New Orleans court.
2He entered his plea during an arraignment on weapons charges accusing him of possessing a firearm after a felony conviction and possessing both a firearm and an illegal drug, marijuana.
3The weapons arrest has kept Durst in New Orleans even though he waived extradition to Los Angeles, where he's charged in the December 2000 death of a longtime friend.
4Durst is being held without bail in a Louisiana state prison.
5He was arrested in New Orleans on the eve of the finale of a six-part HBO documentary called "The Jinx" about Durst, the disappearance of his first wife in 1982, the death of a longtime friend in 2000 and the death and dismemberment of a neighbor in Galveston, Texas in 2001.
6A self-defense plea won Durst an acquittal in the death of Morris Black, but Durst is now charged in Los Angeles with murder in the death of his longtime friend, Susan Berman, 55, who was shot in the head in 2000.
7His lawyers, Dick DeGuerin and Billy Gibbens, have said repeatedly that he just wants to go to California to deal with that charge.
8Durst's lawyers also have challenged the weapons charges he faces in Louisiana on several grounds, including the allegation that the FBI "rummaged" through Durst's belongings before getting an arrest or search warrant.

10DeGuerin also has argued that the two felonies to which Durst pleaded guilty in Pennsylvania - possessing a firearm while a fugitive and doing so while under indictment - are not among those that would make it illegal for him to have a gun.
11Until his indictment Wednesday, Durst had been arrested but not formally charged in connection with the .38-caliber revolver authorities say was found in his room at the J.W. Marriott in New Orleans, where he was staying under the name Everette Ward.
12According to court testimony, an FBI agent recognized Durst in the hotel March 14 and escorted him to his hotel room.
13Durst was arrested early March 15 on the Los Angeles warrant and arrested separately on the weapons charges the next day.
