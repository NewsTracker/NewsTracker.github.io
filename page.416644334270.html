Bush: I admire Baltimore mom who punished son joining riot

1Jeb Bush says the Baltimore mother who chased her son away from a riot has a lot in common with his own mother, former first lady Barbara Bush.
2Bush was describing his mother's toughness when he drew the comparison to Toya Graham, whose smacking of her son was captured on video.
