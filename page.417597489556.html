Injured Iniesta to miss Spain's Euro qualifier with Belarus

1BARCELONA, June 7 (Reuters) - Barcelona midfielder Andres Iniesta will miss Spain's Euro 2016 qualifier in Belarus due to injury, it has been confirmed on the national team's website on Sunday.
2Iniesta looked sharp as Barca beat Juventus in the Champions League final on Saturday but has now been ruled out of a friendly with Costa Rica on Thursday as well as the clash against Belarus next Sunday.
3"Andres Iniesta will not be with the squad due to some problems he has had this week," read a statement on sefutbol.com.
