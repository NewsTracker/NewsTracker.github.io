Blatter: I've tried to tame soccer's monstrous success

1FIFA President Sepp Blatter says he has tried to "tame the monster" of soccer's success during his 17-year reign.
2In a rare interview during his re-election campaign, Blatter tells Swiss daily 20 Minutes his presidency benefited from a boom in soccer's finances and development fueled by television rights deals.
3Blatter says predecessor Joao Havelange once told him: "Sepp, you created a monster."
4In reply, Blatter says he told Havelange: "Yes, but I try to tame the monster."
5Blatter says the power of soccer is "wonderful even if at the same time it's scary."
6The daily newspaper reports that questions about the May 29 election were not allowed.
7Blatter says "I am the president and not a candidate. This is why I don't want to speak about this vote."
