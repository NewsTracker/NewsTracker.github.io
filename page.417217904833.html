Rebel Commander Reported Killed in East Ukraine Car Bombing

1Russian news agencies have reported the killing of a prominent separatist commander in eastern Ukraine when his vehicle was ripped apart by a bomb and then strafed by gunfire.
2The reports, citing a prosecutor in the separatist Luhansk region, said Saturday's attack killed commander Alexei Mozgovoi and at least six other people.
3Prosecutor Sergei Gorenko said the identities of the others were not yet known.
4The reports gave no suspected motive for the killing of Mozgovoi, but noted there had been previous attempts on his life.
5Mozgovoi was a vocal critic of the Russian-backed separatist leadership.
6He was one of the Cossacks, members of a semi-military group, who took control of towns in Ukraine's separatist Luhansk region during the uprising against Ukrainian government troops that began a year ago.
