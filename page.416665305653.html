Republican presidential candidate Rand Paul regrets how Baltimore comment was taken

1FORT MITCHELL, Ky.
2- Republican presidential candidate Rand Paul is regretting that he came across as dismissive of the anguish in Baltimore with an offhand comment he made this week about riots in the city.
3Paul told radio host Laura Ingraham that he had traveled through Baltimore by train on the night riots broke out following the death of an African-American man in police custody and that he was glad the train did not stop.
4He said that with a slight chuckle.
5On Friday, he said, "You always regret offhand comments after you say them because people misinterpret them."
6Paul has been trying to make inroads with minority voters in his campaign.
