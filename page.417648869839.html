Nokia Theatre, home of the Emmys, renamed Microsoft Theater

1This Sunday, June 7, 2015 photo provided by courtesy of Microsoft Theater/AEG shows the new Microsoft Theatre sign installation at Nokia Theatre in Los Angeles.
2Los Angeles' Nokia Theatre will now be known as the Microsoft Theater.
3Microsoft and theater owner AEG announced Tuesday, June 9, 2015, that the 7,100-seat venue is being rebranded following Microsoft's acquisition of the Nokia smartphone business last year.
4LOS ANGELES (AP) - Los Angeles' Nokia Theatre will now be known as the Microsoft Theater.
5Microsoft and theater owner AEG announced Tuesday that the 7,100-seat venue is being rebranded.
6Microsoft Corp. acquired Nokia Corp.'s smartphone business last year.
7The theater is in AEG's L.A. Live entertainment and shopping complex in downtown Los Angeles, across from the Staples Center.
8The venue has hosted more than 700 concerts and such ceremonies as the Emmys, BET Awards, American Music Awards, People's Choice Awards and MTV Video Music Awards.
9The next ceremony slated to take place there is the ESPY Awards on July 15.
10The 40,000-square-foot plaza outside the theater will similarly be known as Microsoft Square, and the nearby 3,100-seat venue Club Nokia will also be rebranded.
11The Nokia Theatre opened in 2007.
