Packers lose WR Jordy Nelson for the season to injury (Yahoo Sports)

1The Green Bay Packers' worst fears about the severity of receiver Jordy Nelson's knee injury have come true.
2The team said Monday only that the star receiver suffered a ''significant right knee injury'' that would cost him the season.
3Nelson, one of the top targets for NFL MVP quarterback Aaron Rodgers, was injured Sunday in a preseason game at Pittsburgh.

6But Rodgers' somber tone after the game foreshadowed the bad news for one of the league's best offenses.

9He and Randall Cobb formed one of the top receiving duos in the league, each catching more than 90 passes last season.
10Nelson is also one of the most respected veterans in the locker room for his leadership and work ethic.

12''Jordy Nelson is a key member of our football program both on the field and of the field, probably more so off the field,'' McCarthy said in Pittsburgh.
