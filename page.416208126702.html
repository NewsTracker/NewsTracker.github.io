Tight security to remain in place for 2nd Boston Marathon since deadly 2013 bombing

1FRAMINGHAM, Mass.
2- Tight security will be in place again for the Boston Marathon - the second since the deadly 2013 bombing at the finish line.
3About 30,000 runners are expected to participate in next Monday's race, with about 1 million spectators lining the route.
4As was the case in 2014, authorities say spectators are being asked not to bring backpacks, large bags or coolers.
5Any bags that are brought are subject to search.
6One new wrinkle this year: Police are warning against the use of any kind of drone or model aircraft along the course.
7The race will occur one day before the sentencing phase of convicted Boston Marathon bomber Dzhokhar Tsarnaev (joh-HAHR' tsahr-NEYE'-ehv) begins.
