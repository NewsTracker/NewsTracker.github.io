1Officials say New Mexico has notched its 31st consecutive month of over-the-year employment growth in March.
2The Department of Workforce Solutions said Tuesday that New Mexico's seasonally adjusted unemployment rate was 6.1 percent last month.
3That's down from 6.8 percent a year ago.
4The national unemployment rate was 5.5 percent.
