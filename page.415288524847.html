25His charity also advises Nigeria, Ethiopia, Rwanda, Sierra Leone and Liberia.

30Mr Blair is paid millions of pounds through one of his companies to advise the Kazakh regime.
31Mr Blair's involvement in Rwanda and his close friendship with Paul Kagame, the country's president, has also attracted much criticism.
