Spieth, Holmes, Wagner in playoff at Houston Open

1April 5 (Reuters) - Americans Jordan Spieth, J.B. Holmes and Johnson Wagner are headed to a playoff after finishing in a three-way tie at the Houston Open in Texas.
2They completed the regulation 72 holes locked at 16-under-par 272 at the Golf Club of Houston.
3Spieth got up-and-down for par at the difficult final hole to shoot 70.
4Holmes carded a brilliant 64, while Wagner made a rare birdie at the last for a 69.
