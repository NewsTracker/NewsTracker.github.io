Prince announces 'Rally 4 Peace' concert Sunday in Baltimore

1FILE - In this Feb. 8, 2015 file photo, Prince presents the award for album of the year at the 57th annual Grammy Awards in Los Angeles.
2Prince has announced plans to perform at a concert in Baltimore following recent unrest in the city over the death of a man who was fatally injured in police custody.
3A statement issued Tuesday, May 5, 2015, says the pop icon will perform Sunday, May 10, at "Rally 4 Peace," a concert at the Royal Farms Arena in Baltimore.
4A statement issued Tuesday says the pop icon will perform Sunday at "Rally 4 Peace," a concert at the Royal Farms Arena in Baltimore.

6Attendees of the concert are asked to wear gray.
