Chile's Villarica volcano erupts

1More than 3,000 people have been evacuated from the volcano's vicinity.
2The mayor of the nearby town of Pucon said residents had left "calmly".
3President Michelle Bachelet said she would travel to the area on Tuesday to assess the situation.
4The 2840m-high Villarrica is an active volcano with a lava lake in its crater.
5It is a popular destination for hikers.
6Hundreds climb the peak each summer to peer inside the crater.
7According to Chile's Ministry of Mining, its last major eruption was in 1985 and there have been smaller eruptions since.
8More than 100 people are thought to have died in mudflows on the slopes of the volcano during the 20th Century.
9Chilean police said the towns of Pucon and Conaripe had been evacuated as a precaution.
10Pucon Mayor Carlos Parra said that after 20 minutes, the volcano's activity seemed to have calmed down again.
11"There's no ash, no lava flow, the volcano is totally passive at the moment," he said.
