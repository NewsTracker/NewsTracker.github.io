Google says 21 percent of tech hires in 2014 were women

1An employee answers phone calls at the switchboard of the Google office in Zurich August 18, 2009.

3The company recently began disclosing the makeup of its workforce after admitting that it was "wrong, and that it's time to be candid about the issues."
4Google said Monday that the increase in Black and Hispanic employees outpaced the company's overall hiring growth, but made up for only 2 percent and 3 percent of total workforce, respectively.
