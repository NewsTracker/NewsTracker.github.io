African American Festival taking place in Baltimore

1BALTIMORE (AP) - Officials are expecting up to 350,000 people at the African American Festival this weekend in Baltimore.
2The festival is taking place Saturday and Sunday at the Camden Yards Sports Complex.
3The free event will include music, cultural exhibits, contests, health screenings and children's activities including inflatable obstacle courses and a rock climbing wall.
4Organizers say there will be more than 150 vending booths and attractions and two entertainment stages.
5The festival will take place from noon to 10 p.m. on Saturday and from noon to 8 p.m. on Sunday.
6The Maryland Transit Administration is urging people attending the festival to take public transit to avoid traffic and parking.
