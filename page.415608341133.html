10In a wide-ranging interview Friday with his manager Ted Chung in Austin, Texas, 43-year-old Snoop discussed topics from getting high with Willie Nelson to meeting Dr. Dre.

18WHO NEEDS A GRAMMY?
19Though Snoop has had platinum-plus albums and hit songs, he's never won a Grammy.
20But he says he's not worried about winning awards, thanks to his youth football league.
21He said he's proud he has been able to send young men to Division I schools to play sports and gain an education.
22"That's better than a Grammy right there," he said.
23He also said his proudest moment was seeing his son, who earned a football scholarship to UCLA, go to college.
24"He's first person in my family to actually go to college, so how do you think I feel?" he asked.
25Snoop admits he hung up on Dr. Dre when the rapper-producer reached out to him.
26Snoop said Dre heard some of his music at a party and then called the young rapper to collaborate.
27"Dr. Dre was like, 'Who was that rapping?" he recalled.
28"From that day he called me and I didn't believe it, I was like, 'Man, quit playing.' I hung up on him."
29Dre called Snoop back and they went on to produce rap classics, from "Gin and Juice" to "What's My Name?"
30"It was like magic," Snoop said.
31"We built a bond. It was a brotherhood."
32Snoop said when he burst on the music scene, he had many enemies.
33"They went to Congress, Dionne Warwick and a bunch of people had my record on display talking about, 'He's the worst thing in the world,' and they were really out to get me," he recalled.
34"And I had to get past that because you have to understand, a lot of these people I looked up to as kids and they were hating on me and didn't know me."
35Snoop said he didn't let the backlash derail him and his mission as an artist.
36"It rubbed me the wrong way and I was young and ambitious and gangster ... but as a man now, I don't do that. I look at what they did, how they rubbed me the wrong way and I try not to rub my young homies the wrong way. I try to be for them and about them, and understand them and talk to them and communicate with them."
