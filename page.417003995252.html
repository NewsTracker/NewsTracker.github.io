Nepal army finds wreckage of missing US chopper

1A US marine corps helicopter carrying out earthquake relief in Nepal was reported missing on May 12 with eight personnel on board.
2KATHMANDU: The wreckage of a US military helicopter that went missing while delivering aid in quake-hit Nepal has been found near the Chinese border, the Nepalese army said Friday.
3"We don't know if there are any survivors, we have yet to confirm that. We have sighted the wreckage from the air, we are now trying to land in the area and get more information," Major General Binoj Basnet told AFP by telephone.
