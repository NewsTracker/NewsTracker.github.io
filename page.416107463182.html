Apple Watch options: 54 combinations of case, band, size

1Apple Watch comes with a choice of watch case, band and size - there are 54 possible configurations in all.
2Most models come in two sizes, 38 millimeters and 42 millimeters, as measured by the height of the watch face.
3The larger one costs $50 more in most cases.
4For a few of the bands, only one size is available.
5- The cheapest is a "Sport" version with an aluminum case.
6You can get it with a silver case and choose a white, blue, green or pink band made of synthetic rubber.
7Or get a gray case with a black band.
8The smaller version costs $349, and the large one is $399.
9That's 10 configurations - five bands, each in two sizes.
10- The regular version has a stainless steel case.
11The cheapest has the same band as the Sport version.
12You choose white or black and pay $549 for the smaller model and $599 for large.
13Fancier bands are available - three different leather bands, a link bracelet or a Milanese loop.
14Prices go as high as $1,099 for a large watch with a stainless steel link bracelet.
15There are 20 official configurations, but once you factor in some leather bands coming in two or three sizes, you have 32 options to choose from.
16- Edition is the luxury line with cases made of 18-karat gold alloy.
17It's $10,000 for a small version with the white or black band that comes with Sport models.
18The large one costs $12,000.
19You can get a leather band for $15,000 or $17,000, depending on the size.
20There are officially eight luxury configurations, or 12 if you count band sizes as choices.
21Apple is also selling bands separately for those who like to swap designs.
22Prices range from $49 for the basic sport band to $449 for a link bracelet.
23To help you choose, Apple has plenty of videos on its website and Apple Store and Apple Watch apps.
24The Apple Store app also has sizing guides, images of the watch cases in actual size and animation of buckles and clasps, which you can open and close by pinching in and out.
