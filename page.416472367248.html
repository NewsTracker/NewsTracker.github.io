1MILWAUKEE (AP) -- Derrick Rose scored 34 points, Jimmy Butler added 24 and the Chicago Bulls beat the Milwaukee Bucks 113-106 in double overtime on Thursday night to take a 3-0 lead in their Eastern Conference playoff series.

3It set the tone for an 8-0 run that gave Chicago a 109-101 lead with 2:44 left to finally shake the pesky Bucks.
4Rose atoned for a missed free throw with 4 seconds left in regulation that left the game tied.
5He scored six points in the second overtime, including a layup off an offensive rebound with 2:15 left for a 10-point lead.

7The Bulls will go for the sweep on Saturday in Game 4 in Milwaukee.
