Tigers closer Nathan out for year with torn elbow ligament (Yahoo Sports)

1Detroit Tigers relief pitcher Joe Nathan throws during the sixth inning of a spring training exhibition baseball game against the Miami Marlins in Lakeland, Fla., Wednesday, March 25, 2015.
2DETROIT (AP) -- Tigers closer Joe Nathan will miss the rest of the season with a torn ligament in his pitching elbow.
3Nathan tore his ulnar collateral ligament on Wednesday while pitching on a minor-league rehabilitation assignment with Triple-A Toledo.
4Hoping to show he was ready to come off the disabled list, he tore both his previously strained flexor pronator and his UCL.
5Detroit manager Brad Ausmus made the announcement Thursday, and Nathan was expected to talk to reporters later in the day.
