Google unveils buying button in mobile shopping ads

1SAN FRANCISCO - Google is introducing a way for shoppers browsing on their smartphones to buy merchandise by tapping on a button included in the ads appearing in its search results.
2The feature, called "Purchases on Google," completes sales using financial information that already has been stored with Google.
3Participating merchants can still gather information about customers using Google's buying button.
4Facebook also has been testing a similar buying button in ads appearing in its social network.
5About a dozen retailers are initially using Google's buying button.
6Google is hoping to increase the number of merchants before the end of this year.
