Egypt's Mubarak to be retried over killing of protesters

1CAIRO (AP) - Egypt's highest appeals court has ordered the retrial of ousted President Hosni Mubarak on charges that he failed to stop the killing of hundreds of protesters during the 2011 uprising that ended his 29-year rule.
2Mubarak, who is 86 and in failing health, has since his arrest in April 2011 been held in detention in a number of hospitals.
3He now resides at a Nile-side military hospital in a leafy suburb just south of Cairo.
