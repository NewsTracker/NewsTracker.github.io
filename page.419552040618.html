Apple Recalls Some iPhone 6 Plus Models Over Blurry Photos

1(Reuters) - Apple Inc is recalling a limited number iPhone 6 Plus phones due to faulty back cameras that take blurry photos.
2The affected phones were mostly sold in a 4-month period between September 2014 and January 2015, Apple said on its website.
3The company, whose shares were set to open at their lowest this year on Monday, said a small component in the affected 6 Plus's iSight back camera may fail.
4Apple said it would replace the phone free of charge if the iSight camera takes blurry photos and falls into a particular serial number range.
5Eligible serial numbers can be checked on Apple's website.
