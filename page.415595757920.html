Miley Cyrus surprises crowd at SXSW event starring rappers

1Cyrus appeared to be the only woman on the stage, which was packed with nearly 20 people jumping up and down, left and right.
2Rappers including Future, Riff Raff, the duo Rae Sremmurd and the group Two-9 - who all also performed - were onstage at The Fader Fort alongside Cyrus.
3Others ranged from hype men to cameramen to people shooting toy guns full of water and confetti into the audience.
4Cyrus sang - well, rapped - her portion on the song "23," Mike WiLL Made-It's 2013 hit that also starred Wiz Khalifa and Juicy J. She also performed her anthemic "We Can't Stop," which Mike WiLL Made-It produced.
5She also recited some of Nicki Minaj's part on Rae Sremmurd's song "Throw Sum Mo."
6Since Cyrus marked a breakthrough with the hits "We Can't Stop" and "Wrecking Ball," she has been supported by rappers.
7The Grammy-nominated "Bangerz" featured collaborations with Pharrell, Big Sean, Nelly, Future and French Montana; and she also appeared on albums by Snoop Dogg and will.i.am.
8Mike WiLL Made-It, who has worked with Rihanna and 2 Chainz and executive produced Cyrus' "Bangerz," was headlining the show.
9He was excited throughout the set, and before he performed "23" - a song that references Air Jordan sneakers - he said he would give sneakers away to the "most pumped person in the crowd."
10Then he threw blue ones into the audience.
