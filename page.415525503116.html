11What the fans want to know, of course, is how "Community" will be different on Yahoo, if at all?
12I had sworn up and down that it wasn't going to change very much at all, that my goal was going to be to keep it the same.

16The cast is going to be different too.
17Joel McHale is coming back as sleazy lawyer Jeff Winger, but a lot of other fan favorites, such as Donald Glover and John Oliver, won't be in the Yahoo episodes.

31You and the show became famous for having a tortured relationship with the executives at NBC.

35Oh, God.
36I grew up on NBC sitcoms, and I wanted to make a classic NBC sitcom.
37"Community" was designed from the ground up to be a kind of valentine to that golden age of television.

39There was only one regime that had actually bought the show and was proud of it, and that was [former programming chief] Ben Silverman's, who was then quickly out.
40Then began the age of people coming into NBC who would regard "Community" as this problem that needed to be solved.
41All television networks are slowly losing ratings, but to the people at NBC we were this show that for some mysterious reason, year after year, on Thursday night at 8 o'clock against "Big Bang Theory," was being viewed by less and less people.
42Obviously, when Yahoo buys a show, knowing everything that they already know about it, that's immediately out the window.
