Ukraine's ex-PM sets up 'Ukraine salvation committee'

1MOSCOW (AP) " Ukraine's former prime minister, in exile in Moscow, has announced the creation of a 'Ukraine salvation committee' and pushed for early presidential and parliamentary elections.
2Mykola Azarov fled Ukraine after ex-president Viktor Yanukovych was ousted in February 2014 following months of protests.
3He said Monday that the committee aims to "take responsibility for the situation in the country."
4The committee will not include Yanukovych.
5Azarov has criticized Yanukovych for losing control over the country, and Azarov ally Igor Markov said the ex-president should be prosecuted for it.
6Azarov said the committee will back ex-lawmaker Vladimir Oleinik as a presidential candidate if early elections are held.
7There was no immediate comment from Ukrainian officials.
8Russian President Vladimir Putin's spokesman, Dmitry Peskov, said the Kremlin had nothing to do with Azarov's move.
