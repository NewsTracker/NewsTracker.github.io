US cousins appear in court in Islamic State case

1CHICAGO (AP) " Prosecutors have told a federal judge in Chicago they'll soon start turning over evidence to lawyers for two suburban cousins accused of plotting terrorist attacks to support the Islamic State group.
2Twenty-two-year-old Hasan Edmonds and his 29-year-old cousin, Jonas Edmonds, appeared in court Wednesday but neither spoke.
3Prosecutor Barry Jonas told Judge John Lee that prosecutors should be able to hand over most of the evidence within two months.
4A complaint says Jonas planned to attack an Illinois military facility while Hasan, who belonged to the Illinois Army National Guard, would join Islamic State fighters.
5Agents arrested the men, both from Aurora, in March.
6Hasan was arrested at a Chicago airport, Jonas was at his home.
7Both have pleaded not guilty.
