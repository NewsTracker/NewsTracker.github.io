Steven Gerrard scores in MLS debut, Galaxy rout Quakes 5-2 (Yahoo Sports)

1CARSON, Calif.
2(AP) -- Steven Gerrard pounded home his first MLS goal and sprinted to the corner, sliding on his knees and spreading his arms in triumph while the LA Galaxy's sellout crowd roared.
3The Liverpool icon already looks quite comfortable on the West Coast, and that could be bad news for the rest of this league.
4Gerrard scored in the 37th minute of his official MLS debut before assisting on Robbie Keane's tiebreaking goal in the Galaxy's 5-2 victory over the San Jose Earthquakes on Friday night.
5''I felt like a kid out there again,'' the 35-year-old Gerrard said after playing 87 minutes and leaving to a standing ovation.
6''I didn't want to come off there.
7I want to play as much football as I can.
8... I'm really happy sitting here.
9I'm happy with the level.
10I feel very confident and happy with my teammates.
11We're only going to get stronger.
12Keane also scored twice on penalty kicks for his second straight hat trick in league play.
13His new partnership with Gerrard is off to a tantalizing start for the Galaxy, who roared back from a two-goal deficit for their fourth win in five games on the strength of their stars.
14''As the game went on, we were getting better and better,'' said Keane, who shook off a serious pregame illness just to suit up.
15Two of Keane's goals were created by Gerrard in his official debut game in the top North American league.
16The longtime Liverpool and England captain played briefly in two non-league Galaxy games this month, but the Southern California crowd properly welcomed Gerrard in a dynamic victory for the defending MLS champions.

21''When you play with good players, it's easy to connect.

31Gerrard and Keane got several huge ovations from the StubHub Center fans anticipating a thrilling stretch run in an up-and-down season for the Galaxy, who won their third MLS Cup title in four seasons last year.

33Gerrard's debut was watched by Landon Donovan and David Beckham, his illustrious predecessors in the Galaxy midfield.

35The Galaxy are moving on with Keane and Gerrard, who has been welcomed to town with great fanfare.
