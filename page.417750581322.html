England beats Mexico 2-0 at Women's World Cup

1Fran Kirby and Karen Carney scored in the second half, and England beat Mexico 2-1 Saturday at the Women's World Cup in Moncton, New Brunswick.
2The English moved into a tie with France for second in Group F with three points and a game to go.
3Colombia is the surprise leader of the group after a 2-0 upset of France.
4The 21-year-old Kirby got the first goal of the World Cup for England when she used a toe poke to beat Cecilia Santiago in the 71st minute.
5Carney scored on a header in the 82nd minute.
6Fabiola Ibarra scored in stoppage time for Mexico.
7Mexico, last in its group, plays Wednesday in Ottawa against the French, while England will take on the upstart Colombians.
