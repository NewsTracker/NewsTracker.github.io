Now you can find your lost phone with a Google search

1Have a crazy night?
2Kids steal your Android phone to play Angry Birds?
3Are you reading this from a tablet as you reach desperately under your bed?
4Well, search no longer, Android users.
5Google has introduced something even simpler than using Apple's "Find My iPhone" app.
6All you need to do is type "find my phone" into Google, and it will bring up a map with your phone's location on it and give you the ability to set off the ringtone.
7It takes minimal effort to set up.
8Your phone has to have the latest Google app installed on it, plus your phone and desktop browser need to be signed into the same Google account.
9But after that, finding your phone is as easy as Googling your next online date.
