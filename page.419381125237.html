5Marshall says Smith didn't stick a finger in Enemkpali's face, as some reports suggested.
6The receiver roomed with the quarterback in the offseason.
7Smith had surgery Thursday to insert screws and metal plates in his jaw.
8He is expected to be out six to 10 weeks.
