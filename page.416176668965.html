Blast in Egypt's North Sinai kills five soldiers: security source

1North Sinai is the epicenter of an insurgency that has killed hundreds of members of the security services since mid-2013, when then army chief Abdel Fattah al-Sisi ousted Islamist president Mohamed Mursi after mass protests against his rule.
2Two of the soldiers killed in the blast were officers, the sources said.
