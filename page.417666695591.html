Swiss Justice Dept: 1 of 7 arrested in FIFA case seeks bail

1Switzerland's Justice Department says one of the seven soccer officials arrested in Zurich appealed for bail during the extradition process.
2The justice office says Switzerland's "Federal Criminal Court will rule on whether or not he will have to remain in custody for the entire length of the extradition proceeding."
3The man who appealed was not identified.
4U.S. authorities have until July 3 to formally request extradition.
5The seven men face 20 years in prison on racketeering charges.
6They were arrested in early-morning raids on luxury hotels in Zurich on May 27.
