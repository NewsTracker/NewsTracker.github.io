Ricciardo in love with Montreal's F1 track

1Daniel Ricciardo is in love.
2He can't get enough of the curves, kisses and screams.
3Of Montreal's Circuit Gilles Villeneuve, that is.
4The track was the site of his maiden Formula One victory and is the venue for this weekend's Canadian Grand Prix.
5"Winning has made me love it a little bit more, but really I've loved it since my first lap back when I was driving for Toro Rosso," the Australian said in his race preview.
6"I remember coming back into the pits and saying `what a circuit'. I was amazed at how good it was - and I'd only done an install (lap)."
7The ability to jump across the kerbs and bounce the car around makes it a "ridiculous" amount of fun, Ricciardo says, which reminds him of his go-kart days.
8"And I love that," he said.
9"You can get aggressive with it, and aim to just brush the wall.
10"Some guys will play it safe and sacrifice half a 10th (of a second) to get through there cleanly; others will take a risk and go flat out trying to find a little bit.
11"The nearer you are, the faster you'll go. Give the wall a kiss and you feel pretty good; kiss it too hard and that's it.
12It's unlikely Ricciardo will be in contention for a second straight win at the venue, given Red Bull's form in 2015.
