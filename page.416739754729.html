Reports: Dave Goldberg, husband of Facebook COO Sheryl Sandberg, died after exercising

1Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Facebook, and her husband Dave Goldberg, CEO of SurveyMonkey, arrive for morning session of the Allen & Co. annual conference at the Sun Valley Resort on July 10, 2013 in Sun Valley, Idaho.
2Dave Goldberg, the 47-year-old SurveyMonkey CEO and husband of Facebook executive Sheryl Sandberg was exercising at a gym at a resort in Mexico on Friday when he collapsed and then died, media reports say.
3A person close to the family who spoke on the condition of anonymity told The New York Times and the Associated Press that efforts to revive Goldberg at the gym and hospital were unsucessful.
4Goldberg's death stunned Silicon Valley over the weekend and people flocked to social media to express their condolences and grief.
5Goldberg's brother Robert announced his death through a Facebook post on Saturday morning.
6David Goldberg, chief executive officer of SurveyMonkey, is seen in this undated courtesy photo obtained from the company on Saturday, May 2, 2015, in San Jose, Calif.
7Goldberg, 47, was the husband of Sheryl Sandberg, chief operation officer of Facebook.
8"In this time of sorrow, we mourn his passing and remember what an amazing husband, father, brother, son and friend he was. No words can express the depth of loss we feel, but we want his children to learn how much he meant to all of you," Robert Goldberg wrote in the post.
9Dave Goldberg founded the online music and media outfit Launch Media in 1994 before it was sold to Yahoo in 2001.
10Since 2009, he was CEO of SurveyMonkey, an online survey company based in Palo Alto.
11He's been married to Sandberg since 2004.
12Goldberg's cause of death has not been announced by family members.
