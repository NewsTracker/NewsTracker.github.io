Mom, 33, arrested on terrorism charges and banned from social media for Facebook rant that called for 'Death to all white cops nationwide' as Baltimore protests erupted into riots

1A 33-year-old mother has been charged with making 'terroristic threats' after she called for 'death to all white cops nationwide' in a post on Facebook.
2Ebony Dickens of East Point, Georgia, posted the rant under the alias Tiffany Milan on Monday as protests in Baltimore about the death of Freddie Gray descended into rioting.
3Atlanta police were alerted to the post and informed local police in East Point, six miles southwest of the city once they had correctly identified her.
4The FBI, Department of Homeland Security, the District Attorney's office and even the New York Police Department assisted East Point police Channel 2 reported.
5She was taken into custody at 9:30 p.m. on Tuesday evening and charged with dissemination of information related to terroristic threats.
6Officers confiscated three computers and a loaded gun from the property.
7Some hours before her arrest Dickens had deleted her Facebook account.
8'I condone black on white killings.
9'I've thought about shooting every white cop I see in the head until I'm either caught by the police or killed by them.
10Ha!!!!
11I think I can pull it off.
12Might kill at least fifteen tomorrow.
13'I'm plotting now.
14They reading this sh** too right now.
15Freedom of speech tho.
16So when you can absolutely show me the 1st amendment where it explicitly says you can't say "kill all cops," then I'll delete my status.
17Her words suggested imminent lawless action and that spurred officers into action.
18During her bond hearing at East Point Magistrate Court on Wednesday it was revealed that Dickens had a Masters degree in Legal Services.
19City Solicitor Antavious Weemes argued that Dickens should have therefore known better, Channel 11 reported.
20In Dickens' defense, public defender Tracey Moran told the court: 'My client was simply venting... it was a horrible mistake.
21Her online boast: 'show me the 1st amendment where it explicitly says you can't say 'kill all cops,' then I'll delete my status,' did not sit well with the Judge.
22She disagreed saying that the first amendment to the Constitution does not give you the right to threaten and terrorize others.
23A $10,000 bond was set and Dickens was banned from all social media.
24Dickens will appear before a judge on Thursday at Fulton County Jail, where she is being held.
