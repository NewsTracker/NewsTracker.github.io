'Volcano of Fire' spews colossal column of ash over Mexican city

1Photo: Ash erupts from the Colima volcano in Mexico.
2Mexico's Colima volcano has spewed a giant 3.5-kilometre-high column of ash that rained down on a nearby city, authorities say.
3The Jalisco state civil protection agency said a "moderate" quantity of ash fell on Ciudad Guzman, a town near the western state of Colima, where the volcano is located.
4A civil protection official said there were no reports of damage or injuries in the city of 100,000 people.
5Officials urged the population to use masks if they venture out of their homes, remove excess ash from rooftops so they do not collapse and cover water drains.
6The 3,860-metre mountain, also known as the Volcano of Fire, is among the most active in the country.
