Grandfather could testify in Alabama girl's running death

1(AP) - The husband of an Alabama woman charged in her granddaughter's running death could be a witness in her upcoming capital murder trial.
2Court documents show prosecutors have subpoenaed Johnny Garrard in the trial of his wife, 59-year-old Joyce Hardin Garrard of Boaz.
3The woman is accused of making granddaughter Savannah Hardin run until she collapsed and died in 2012.
4Initial jury qualification is done, and final selection begins next week.
5Documents filed Friday don't indicate why prosecutors might seek testimony from Garrard's husband.
6But arguments at a past hearing suggested he was at the scene at least briefly on the day authorities say Savannah collapsed after running around the yard for hours.
7The defense says Garrard is innocent.
8It blames the child's death on other medical problems and treatment decisions.
