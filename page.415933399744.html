RCR appeals penalty against Newman's team for tire bleeding (Yahoo Sports)

1NASCAR this week said tires taken from Newman after the March 22 race in California failed an independent inspection and had been altered to intentionally release air pressure during the race.
2Crew chief Luke Lambert was fined $125,000 and suspended for the next six races.
3The suspension also includes the All-Star race in May. Lambert was also placed on probation through the end of the year.
4Tire technician James Bender and engineer Philip Surgen were also suspended six races and placed on probation through Dec. 31.
5Newman and car owner Childress were docked 75 points each.
