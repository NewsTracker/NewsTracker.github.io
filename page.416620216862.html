10In upcoming promos, the studio will be including information on how to help the relief efforts in Nepal and also how to prepare for natural disasters.
11had already planned public service announcements about preparedness around the marketing of the film starring Dwayne Johnson and Carla Gugino but adjusted and accelerated those efforts after Nepal.
