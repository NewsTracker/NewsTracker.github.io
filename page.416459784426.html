Migrant dies trying to enter Greece by land during floods

1THESSALONIKI, Greece (AP) " Greek authorities say they have found the body of a migrant who apparently drowned during floods as he tried to illegally enter the country from Turkey.
2The unidentified young man's body was found Thursday near the banks of the Evros River on Greece's northeastern border with Turkey.
3Police believe he died about a month ago.
4The number of people " mostly Syrian war refugees but also migrants from many Middle Eastern and African countries " entering Greece illegally has nearly tripled this year.
5Most make the perilous crossing by sea, paying smuggling gangs to ferry them over from Turkey in unseaworthy boats.
6During the past 24 hours, authorities safely picked up 262 migrants in two separate incidents in the Aegean Sea.
