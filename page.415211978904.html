10The families were picked up by consular vehicles on the tarmac and whisked away to Cilacap, the seaport town near Nusakambangan.
11They will be permitted to visit the Bali nine pair, organisers of a heroin smuggling syndicate, on Nusakambangan, where the men are being held in isolation cells.
