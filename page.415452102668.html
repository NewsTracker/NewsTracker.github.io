Russell Brand skips SXSW premiere of 'raw' documentary on his life

1The comedian pulled out of attending the world premiere of 'Brand: A Second Coming,' directed by Ondi Timoner, and issued a statement instead.
2The film features details about his relationships with women including his ex-wife Katy Perry and premiered at SXSW Festival in Austin, Texas on Friday night.
3He said in a statement: "I let go of my mad ambition to direct and star in what had become a shambles and handed the reins over to Ondi, who wanted creative control and to make a documentary about me and my transition from a relatively conventional celebrity to whatever the hell it is I am now.
4"Ondi is a very beautiful person and a director of peerless integrity, I suppose what I didn't consider was that in letting go of the film, I was agreeing to be the subject of a biography.
5"You'd think a narcissist would like nothing more than talking about themselves and their 'rags to riches,' 'hard luck' story but actually, it felt like, to me, my life was hard enough the first time round and going through it again was painful and sad.
6"I know Ondi is an artist and I'm told the film is good but for me, watching it was very uncomfortable."
7Admitting Brand was unhappy with how "raw" the final version of the documentary is, Timoner confessed she was shocked by his reaction to the film.
8According to The Sun newspaper, she said: "I really wanted him to be at peace with this film, but I don't know if he'll ever really be at peace with it. It's so raw and so personal and he's never let anything like this out before.
9"I mean this is a guy who has written 'My Booky Wook' and makes a lot of his stand up about his personal life and his foibles and his history, but somehow, on film, it's a different story for him.
10"He said to me a couple of times during filming, 'I went through it already. It was hard enough to live the first time."
11Brand -- who is now sober after struggling with drug and alcohol addiction in the past -- married 'Dark Horse' hitmaker Katy Perry in 2010 but they got divorced two years later.
