Official: German killed by shark on Egypt's Red Sea Coast

1CAIRO (AP) - An Egyptian security official says a shark killed a German citizen on Egypt's Red Sea Coast.
2The official says the 52-year old man's body had been moved to a local hospital after the attack Saturday and that officials were conducting an investigation.
3He says the shark bit off the man's leg at the knee.
4An official from the German Embassy in Cairo says they have received reports that a German citizen died in the area but are not aware of the cause of death.
5All officials spoke Sunday on condition of anonymity because they are not authorized to brief reporters.
6Egypt is one of the world's top diving destinations.
7Similar attacks have happened in the past -- in 2010, a shark killed a 70-year-old German tourist while she was snorkeling.
