Android peeing on Apple logo spotted on Google Maps

1Rivalry between Google and Apple has always been fierce, but now Google Maps appears to have been emblazoned with the image of the Android mascot urinating on an Apple logo.
2The origin of the image is unclear.
3Some have suggested it could be Google’s idea of an Easter Egg (hidden joke), while others claim it was added by a user.
4The image is most likely a result of a user abusing Map Maker, the tool that allows users to contribute to Google Maps.
5One Hacker News user claims to have tracked down the culprit – a user called nitricboy.
6"We've terminated the Android figure involved in this incident, and he'll be disappearing from Google Maps shortly," a Google spokesperson said.
7Meanwhile, another Hacker News user has spotted an image in the Takht Pari Forest, just a few miles east of the first.
8Other images include a smiley face to the east of Islamabad and a Skype logo to the north of Lahore.
