ACLU: Squalid conditions at troubled Baltimore jail

1BALTIMORE (AP) - The ACLU and Public Justice Center are asking a federal judge to reopen a lawsuit against the state of Maryland, saying there are squalid conditions at a troubled Baltimore jail.
2Attorneys filed a motion Tuesday, saying the conditions endanger the health and safety of inmates.
3They say moldy showers are often infested with vermin and medical care is poor or absent.
4The attorneys say the lack of medical care may be implicated in the deaths of at least seven inmates.
5They say the terms of a 2009 agreement with the Justice Department are not being met.
