Potential Dem rivals to Hillary Clinton plug away in Iowa

1DES MOINES, Iowa (AP) - The Hillary Rodham Clinton juggernaut is coming to Iowa.
2Martin O'Malley already has beaten her to the early-voting state.
3For months, former Maryland Gov. O'Malley has largely had Iowa to himself as Clinton slow-played her entry into the Democratic race for president in 2016.
4O'Malley's Iowa advantage - if there was one at all - should be coming to an end Sunday with Clinton's much-anticipated announcement she's making a second run for the White House.
5O'Malley has done the most to potentially challenge Clinton on the Democratic side.
6He's visited Iowa six times since the start of last year.
