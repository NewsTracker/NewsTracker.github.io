Judge orders Facebook and Zuckerberg to turn over documents

1Manhattan federal Judge Vernon Broderick issued an order Friday against Facebook and Mark Zuckerberg.
2He gave them until Monday to relinquish documents requested by Paul Ceglia's (SEHG'-lee-uhz) lawyer.
3The judge rejected a request documents not be turned over until Ceglia is caught.
4Documents requested include all electronic communications Zuckerberg had about a Ceglia contract in an 18-month stretch beginning in 2003.
5Ceglia cut off his electronic ankle bracelet last month and fled.
6His wife, two children and dog also are missing.
7The family lived in Wellsville, 70 miles southeast of Buffalo.
