Greece reiterates it has responsibility to make debt payments

1ATHENS Greece has the responsibility to pay its obligations but needs a deal with lenders as quickly as possible because of its cash crunch, the government spokesman said on Monday in response to questions about whether it can make a debt payment on June 5.
2Asked about comments by officials including the interior minister that Greece does not have money to make that payment, Gabriel Sakellaridis reiterated the government's official stance that the government would do its best but needed fresh aid soon.
3"Based on the liquidity problems that we have, there is an imperative need for us and the euro zone to have a deal as soon as possible," he told a news conference.
4"To the degree to which we are able to pay our obligations, we will pay our obligations. It's the government's responsibility to be in a position to pay all of these obligations."
5He also voiced optimism of a deal in a short period of time, saying one could be found by early June.
