One dead after patrol boat blows up and sinks off Ukraine's Mariupol

1KIEV A search operation was under way on Sunday for a border guard official missing after a patrol boat sank off Ukraine's port city of Mariupol following an explosion, the border guard said.
2The cause of the explosion was not immediately clear, authorities said.
3Mariupol is on the edge of the conflict between government forces and Russian-backed separatists and has been under threat from rebel attacks for months.
4"Today at 14.20 hours (1120 GMT) a patrol boat exploded as it came out of its mooring station in Mariupol," a state border guard statement said.
