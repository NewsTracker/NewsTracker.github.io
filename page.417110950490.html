CANNES WATCH: DiCaprio buys $13,600 bag for charity

1FILE - This May 15, 2013 file photo shows actor Leonardo DiCaprio poses for photographers during a photo call for "The Great Gatsby" at the 66th international film festival, in Cannes, southern France.
2Actor Leonardo DiCaprio bids on Chanel bags at the Heart Fund Gala in Cannes.
3Who knew Leonardo DiCaprio had an eye for Chanel bags?
4The actor plunked down 10,000 euros ($13,620) for a blue Chanel Neoprene flap bag embroidered with flowers on the Croisette at Cannes on Monday night.
5But it was all for charity, part of an auction at the Heart Fund Gala, also attended by Paris Hilton, actor Gary Dourdan and featuring a performance by Yasiin Bey (formerly known as Mos Def).
6DiCaprio outbid several suitors for the purse, but was himself outbid on two other items, including a vacation at one of the luxury resorts of Nikki Beach Worldwide.
7Bey performed a brief set, during which he shouted out to DiCaprio.
8The Heart Fund offers cardiovascular care across the globe for impoverished children.
9Heart surgeons have donated their services to perform costly operations.
10Last year, with the support of Nikki Beach owners Jack and Lucia Penrod, the organization purchased a bus to act as a mobile hospital to screen and treat children; it was unveiled at this year's gala.
