Mexico mining groups says 3 kidnapped miners were slain

1MEXICO CITY (AP) - A Mexican mining industry group says three miners were killed earlier this month in the southern state of Guerrero.
2The three employees of the Los Filos gold mine in the mountains of Guerrero had been reported kidnapped.
3The Mexican Mining Chamber said Thursday that the killings "disturb the healthy development of the mining industry in Guerrero."
4An official in the Guerrero state government confirmed the bodies of the three miners were found dumped in a sparsely populated area about a week after they were abducted.
5The official was not authorized to be quoted by name.
6The mine is operated by the Canadian mining company GoldCorp.
7The company has said the victims were employees but were not working or travelling to the mine when they were kidnapped.
