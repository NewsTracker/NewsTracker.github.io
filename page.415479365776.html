Iraqi militia loyal to radical cleric joins Tikrit offensive

1BAGHDAD: Dozens of fighters with the militia loyal to radical Shia cleric Muqtada al-Sadr left Iraq's capital on Sunday to take part in an offensive to capture Tikrit from the Islamic State group.
2Men from Iraq's Peace Brigade left to the Shia holy city of Samarra, the launching point for operations to recapture Saddam Hussein's hometown.
3About a dozen heavily armed trucks packed with fighters left Baghdad earlier Sunday.
4"We are traveling to help the community of Muslims and to help the people of Iraq," said Ali al-Mousawi, a spokesman for the brigade.
