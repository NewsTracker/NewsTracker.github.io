Ferguson Horror: Men in truck 'randomly fire into crowd of protesters in Baltimore'

1A MAN has been shot in the face and two others injured amid a random shooting spree targeting protesters in Ferguson, according to reports.
2The first shooting happened earlier when a man was hit in the leg during a demonstration against police violence.
3A suspect was quickly taken into custody at the late-night protest.
4Then witnesses reported seeing "guys in a random truck" firing at protesters.
5It is unclear if the shootings were linked to the demonstration, where some 50 people gathered and intermittently blocked traffic near where white Ferguson officer Darren Wilson fatally shot Brown in August.

7Brown's shooting, and subsequent police killings of unarmed black men, sparked months of sometimes destructive protests against police violence.
