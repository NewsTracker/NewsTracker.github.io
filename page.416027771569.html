Google Capital Leads $60 Million Funding Round For ZenPayroll

1NEW YORK - Online payroll software company ZenPayroll said Monday that Google Capital is leading a $60 million round of funding, bringing its total funding to date to $86.1 million.
2Other participants in the funding round include Emergence Capital Partners and Ribbit Capital, as well as existing investors General Catalyst, Kleiner Perkins Caulfield & Byers and Google Ventures.
3ZenPayroll was set up in 2012 to service companies in California.
