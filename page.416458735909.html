Spurs' Kawhi Leonard named NBA defensive player of year (Yahoo Sports)

1SAN ANTONIO (AP) -- San Antonio Spurs forward Kawhi Leonard has been named the NBA defensive player of the year.
2The league announced the award on Thursday.
3Injuries limited Leonard to just 64 games this season.
4But he still managed to make an enormous impact, especially on the defensive end of the floor.
5Leonard is just the sixth forward to lead the league in steals per game since the NBA started tracking the statistic 42 years ago.
6He averaged 2.3 steals per game and helped the Spurs finish third in defensive rating with 99.6 points allowed per 100 possessions.
7Leonard garnered 333 total points in the voting, including 37 first-place votes.
8Golden State forward Draymond Green finished a close second with 317 points, including 45 first-place votes.
