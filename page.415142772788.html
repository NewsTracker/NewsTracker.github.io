Iraqi forces move on Tikrit: TV report

1Al-Iraqiya television said on Monday that the forces were backed by artillery and airstrikes from Iraqi fighter jets.
2It said the militants were dislodged from some areas outside the city but gave no details.
3Hours ahead of the operation, Prime Minister Haider al-Abadi called on Sunni tribal fighters to abandon the extremist group, promising them a pardon.
4Tikrit, about 130 kilometres north of Baghdad, fell into the hands of IS last northern summer along with the country's second-largest city of Mosul and other areas in the Sunni heartland.
