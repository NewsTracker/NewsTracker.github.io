Apple, IBM, Japan Post team up to improve elderly care

1NEW YORK (AP) - Apple, IBM and Japanese insurance and bank holding company Japan Post have formed a partnership to improve the lives of elderly people in the country.
2Apple CEO Tim Cook, IBM CEO Ginni Rometty and Japan Post CEO Taizo Nishimuro announced the deal in New York on Thursday.
3The program will provide iPads with apps designed to help seniors manage day-to-day lives and keep in touch with family members.
4IBM will provide cloud services and help create apps to improve seniors' quality of life.
5Apple and IBM formed a partnership last year to create mobile apps for a broad range of industries.
6This has led to the creation of 22 apps so far.
