R&B singer The Weeknd, in sultry mood, performs during SXSW

1The R&B singer performed Thursday on the second night of the "YouTube at SXSW" event at the Coppertank Events Center.
2His set was smoky - at times it was hard to clearly see his face - as he sang tunes that ranged from sensual to energetic.
3"Can I ask you something, Austin, Texas? I want to ask you a question, SXSW. Can I get sexy for you, SXSW?" he asked the feverish crowd of a few hundred.
4The Weeknd performed his recent Top 10 hits, "Earned It" from the "50 Shades of Grey" soundtrack and "Love Me Harder," his mid-tempo duet with Ariana Grande.
5The War on Drugs headlined YouTube's concert on Wednesday.
6Other performers during the three-day event included iLoveMakonnen, Future Islands, and Marina and the Diamonds.
7Snoop Dogg will headline the third night on Friday.
8"YouTube at SXSW" is a pre-party for Monday's YouTube Music Awards.
9YouTube painted the front of the Coppertank Events Center red to emulate its logo.
