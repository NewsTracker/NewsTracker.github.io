The Latest: France's Sapin says committed to Greece in euro

1French Finance Minister Michel Sapin says Europe remains committed to avoiding "catastrophe" for Greece and keeping it in the eurozone.
2Sapin said on France's iTele television Thursday that "the exit of Greece from the eurozone is not desirable, nor envisaged."

4Sapin said that regardless of what happens Sunday, France's own economy will be able to bear the outcome, in contrast to the situation five years ago when the Greek debt crisis first exploded.
5"There are no immediate consequences for France. France is stronger than in 2010."
6"We are committed to avoid a catastrophe for Greece and difficulties for Europe and France."
