Report: Ukraine renews license for under-fire TV station

1KIEV, Ukraine (AP) " Ukraine's government broadcasting authority has extended the operating licensing for a popular television station that has been accused of a pro-Russia stance.
2The broadcasting authority said in a statement that its executive board voted Thursday to renew Inter's license for seven years.
3Prominent members of parliament had demand action against Inter for adopting an editorial policy allegedly detrimental to the country.
4Inter is also facing unrelated criminal investigations.
5The broadcasting authority says there were no legal grounds to revoke Inter's license.
6Inter says it is victim of a political witch-hunt and says media freedoms are being curbed in Ukraine.
7Any perceived sympathy for Russia has become acutely suspect in Ukraine, which is battling separatists that the government says are being directed by Moscow.
8Russia denies those claims.
