Ukraine govt: No links between reported suicides of 6 officials with former Yanukovych regime

1Interior Minister Arsen Avakov said Friday that any suggestion that the deaths were connected was "speculation."
2In the most prominent case, a leading official with Yanukovych's now-defunct former ruling Party of Region, Mikhail Chechetov, in late February fell to his death from his 17-story apartment in Kiev.
3Police said they found a suicide note.
4Chechetov was facing criminal investigation for abuse of office.
5Five other former Ukrainian government officials in the past two months have died in various circumstances that police have said were most likely suicides.
6Yanukovych was ousted in February 2014 after months of sometimes violent anti-government street protests.
