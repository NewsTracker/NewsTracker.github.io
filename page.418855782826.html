Mexico national team coach allegedly hits TV journalist

1MEXICO CITY (AP) " The coach of Mexico's national soccer team is known for his histrionic displays of emotion during games.
2Now Miguel Herrera's off-field composure is being questioned after a television sports anchor claimed the coach punched him at the Philadelphia airport following Mexico's 3-1 victory over Jamaica to take the Gold Cup.
3Television Azteca sports anchor Christian Martinoli described the incident Monday on a Television Azteca program.
4He said Herrera apparently wanted to hit him again but fellow sports commentator Luis Garcia got between them.
5Martinoli has criticized Herrera's performance as coach as have many Mexicans.
6The Mexican Soccer Federation did not immediately confirm the Sunday incident.
7The short, stocky Herrera gained notoriety for his effusive celebrations at the 2014 World Cup in Brazil.
