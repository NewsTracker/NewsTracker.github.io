10"But we have a mandate to build. To better the lives of people in need. To conquer hunger, disease and illiteracy. To promote respect for people's rights and freedoms."
11The U.N. leader also is scheduled to meet the Bring Back Our Girls group, which demands government action to free 219 abducted schoolgirls held by Boko Haram for nearly 500 days.
12His visit comes as a regional army is preparing a long-delayed offensive against Nigeria's Islamic extremists.
13The 6-year-old uprising has killed 20,000 people, including more than 1,000 this year.
