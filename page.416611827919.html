Sergeant is 1st witness to identify James Holmes as gunman

1CENTENNIAL, Colo.
2(AP) - A police sergeant who responded to the Colorado theater shooting says he saw a suspect lying on the ground in the parking lot as two officers arrested him and stripped off his helmet and body armor.
3Sgt. Gerald Jonsgaard identified James Holmes as that person in court on Wednesday.
4It was the first time in the trial that a witness described Holmes as the gunman.
5Previous witnesses either didn't see Holmes at all or only saw a masked gunman.
