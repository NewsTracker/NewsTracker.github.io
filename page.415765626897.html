Robinson says Ferguson's ready for Raiders

1Sydney Roosters ace Blake Ferguson is set for the extra attention when he faces his old Canberra teammates for the first time since being sacked by the Raiders.
2Sydney Roosters coach Trent Robinson believes Blake Ferguson is mature enough to handle any extra attention that inevitably comes his way in Sunday's NRL showdown with Canberra.
3Fresh from his two-try heroics against Penrith on Monday night, Ferguson confronts his past when he faces the Raiders for the first time since being sacked by the club in 2013.
4"It's the same prep for him and he's started the week very well," Robinson said on Friday.
5"There is no reason I should say anything different to what I say every week about how to prepare for a game because we're about trying to get Blake right to play, not about who the opposition is and how he should react to that."
