Fin24.com | Apple takes on Google Street View

1Apple Mac.
2San Francisco - Apple said on Wednesday that it is cruising streets in specially equipped cars to gather pictures and other data for its free online mapping service.
3For years, a Street View team has been ranging the world for Google Maps.
4Following in its tracks, Apple promised to safeguard privacy while gathering mapping data on the road.
5"For example, we will blur faces and license plates on collected images prior to publication," Apple said in an online post.
6Google took to blurring faces and car license plate numbers in Street View imagery at its mapping service due to privacy concerns.
7The Street View team has collected pictures in cities around the world as well as in remote locales such as the Canadian Arctic and Brazilian rain forests.
8Apple's move comes as people increasingly use smartphones for navigation as well as to find nearby offerings such as shops, restaurants or tourist attractions.
9Referring customers to businesses provides money-making opportunities for mapping services.
10Senior vice president of software engineering Craig Federighi said at the company's annual gathering of software developers this week that Apple is investing heavily in maps and that usage is soaring.
11He also showed off a version of Apple Maps devoted to getting around using public transit and enhanced with Siri virtual assistant technology so people can get directions by simply asking.
12"We taught Siri all about transit," Federighi said while highlighting map service improvements that will be included in the coming version of software powering Apple mobile devices.
13Apple Maps will also locate nearby merchants, and display whether they handle transactions using the Apple Pay digital wallet.
14Apple has been improving its map service since it launched in 2012 with so many glitches that CEO Tim Cook apologised publicly.
