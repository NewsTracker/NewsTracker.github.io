Rain postpones NASCAR race at Richmond until Sunday (Yahoo Sports)

1RICHMOND, Va.
2(AP) -- Weather disrupted NASCAR's top series for a second consecutive week when Saturday night's Sprint Cup Series race was postponed because of persistent rain at Richmond International Raceway.
3It's the first time this season the series has been unable to race because of rain, but completing last Sunday's race at Bristol Motor Speedway was a challenge.
4Rain caused nearly a 90-minute delay Sunday at Bristol Motor Speedway, and the race was stopped for a four-hour rain delay not long after it finally began.
5In all, the Bristol event took nearly nine hours to complete from start to finish.
6Joey Logano will start from the pole when the Richmond race begins.
7Denny Hamlin, who grew up 20 minutes from the speedway in Chesterfield, will start second in his attempt to complete a nearly perfect weekend.
8Hamlin won the pole for Friday night's Xfinity Series race and then led all but two laps in winning the race.
9The victory, the 100th in the Xfinity Series for manufacturer Toyota, came as Hamlin returned to competition after getting out of his car at Bristol with neck spasms.
10He said the spasms began around Lap 12, and after receiving treatment during the long rain delay, the decision was made for him not to resume the race.
11Joe Gibbs Racing development driver Erik Jones replaced Hamlin in the car.
12Following Friday night's victory, Hamlin said he made the right call not to race anymore at Bristol and look ahead to the Richmond races.
13''We knew we had a big week ahead of us coming up here at Richmond and I needed to be 100 percent for that,'' Hamlin said.
