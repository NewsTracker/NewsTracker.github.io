Israel election: 'We achieved a great victory against all odds', says Benjamin Netanyahu

1The election was widely seen as a referendum on Netanyahu, who has governed Israel for the past six years.
2Recent opinion polls indicated he was in trouble, giving chief rival Isaac Herzog of the opposition Zionist Union a slight lead.
3Exit polls Tuesday showed the two sides deadlocked but once the actual results came pouring in early Wednesday, Likud soared forward.
4Zionist Union wound up with just 24 seats.
5Even before the final results were known, Netanyahu declared victory and pledged to form a new government quickly.
6"Against all odds, we achieved a great victory for the Likud," Netanyahu told supporters at his election night headquarters.
