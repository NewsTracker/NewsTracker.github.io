1The father of a fugitive accused of falsely claiming he's owed a majority ownership in Facebook says his son doesn't trust the criminal justice system.

3The younger Ceglia disappeared with his wife, children and the family dog this month.

5That lets the government pursue a half dozen properties Ceglia's family posted to cover $250,000 bail.
