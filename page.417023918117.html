Recap: Baltimore vs. LA Angels

1Baltimore, MD (SportsNetwork.com) - Jered Weaver pitched into the eighth inning to help the LA Angels of Anaheim to a 3-1 win over the Baltimore Orioles in the opener of a three-game set.
2Weaver (2-4) won his second straight start as he allowed just a run on three hits with a walk and three strikeouts over 7 1/3 innings for the Angels, who have won four straight.
3Albert Pujols hit a solo home run while Johnny Giavotella and Carlos Perez each drove in a run.
4J.J. Hardy hit a solo homer for the Orioles, who have lost seven of their last 10.
5Wei-Yin Chen (1-2) gave up two runs on five hits while walking two and striking out seven in seven-plus innings.
6The Angels took a 1-0 lead in the first when Pujols stroked a two-out, solo homer to left, his sixth of the year.
7In the second, the Angels added to their lead as Erick Aybar led off with a double, took third on a groundout from Giavotella and came home when Perez followed with a sacrifice fly for a 2-0 lead.
8Baltimore didn't get a runner to second base until the fifth when Hardy hit a soft roller towards first that Weaver misplayed and threw away, leaving Hardy at second.
9Weaver, though, got Rey Navarro to fly out and Caleb Joseph to strike out to end the frame.
10The Orioles finally got on the board in the eighth when Hardy led off the frame with his second homer of the year.
11Manny Machado hit a two-out single off Joe Smith later in the inning, but Alejandro De Aza flied out.
12Giavotella's sac fly in the ninth gave the Angels an insurance run, and Huston Street worked a scoreless home ninth to pick up his 12th save of the season.
