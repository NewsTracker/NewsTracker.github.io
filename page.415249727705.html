17She testified that she laughed with him in the bathroom, poured him a glass of whiskey and then, when she heard the shower turn on, grabbed her dog and fled the apartment.
18Nissenbaum allegedly cut himself with a razor in the bathtub and then set fire to the woman's bedding in the bathroom, hoping to kill himself, according to testimony.
19Detective Mark DiMeo, who interviewed him in the hospital after the attack, said Nissenbaum admitted slapping the woman several times to get her to stop screaming and admitted he knew she had an active order of protection against him.
20He also admitted dying his beard black by using mascara, DiMeo testified.
