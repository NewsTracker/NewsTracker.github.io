Suge Knight Fires His Robbery Case Lawyer

1He is charged with murder in a separate case, in which prosecutors said he ran down two men with a pickup truck at a Los Angeles area burger stand on 29 January, killing one of them.
2Comedian Katt Williams, 43, is also charged in the robbery case.
3During a hearing in Los Angeles Superior Court, Knight did not give a reason for making his request to replace his lawyer, David Kenner, when Judge Ronald Coen asked him if that was what he wanted to do.
4"Is Kenner fired?"
5Judge Coen later asked.
6"Kenner is fired," Knight said.
7Mr Kenner, who has a long history of working with Knight, was not present during the court hearing.
8Knight has until 27 May to find a new lawyer.
9The 49-year-old has complained about his health in previous court hearings and was rolled into Wednesday's hearing in a so-called safety seat which he was not happy about.
10He complained to the judge and said he could walk.
11"If you can walk, you can walk. It won't happen again," Judge Coen said.
12He added that Knight had previously complained of paralysis on one side.
13Outside of court, lawyer Matthew Fletcher, who consulted with Knight during the hearing on Wednesday and represents him in the murder case, said the safety chair was unnecessary.
14"Come on, this is getting to the point of abject humiliation," Mr Fletcher told reporters after the hearing.
15He said he did not expect to represent Knight in the robbery case.
