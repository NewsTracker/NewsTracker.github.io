Nintendo President Satoru Iwata Dies of Cancer

1TOKYO-Nintendo Co. President Satoru Iwata has died of a tumor, the company said Monday, in a sudden announcement that comes just months after Mr. Iwata announced the videogame maker's entry into mobile gaming.
2Mr. Iwata, 55 years old, underwent surgery in June 2014, and his condition worsened rapidly in recent days, the company said.
3It said he died on Saturday and the cause of death was a bile duct tumor.
