15The Sunni city of Tikrit lies about 130 km (80 miles) north of Baghdad.
16It is one of the largest cities held by the Islamic State group and lies on the road connecting Baghdad to militant-held Mosul, the country's second-largest city.

19The U.S. and other coalition members have been providing weapons and training to Iraqi and Kurdish forces in the north in an effort to prepare them for the battle ahead.
20The Tikrit operation is the largest to date and requires careful air and ground coordination in order to minimize civilian casualties and damage to the city's infrastructure.
21Iraqi security forces and allied militias surrounded Tikrit and entered some northern and southern neighborhoods earlier this month.
