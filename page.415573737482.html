8The report said that ISIL's Islamic sharia courts in Mosul had also meted out cruel punishments including stoning and amputation.

10The UN investigators said it was "widely alleged" that Iraqi government forces had used barrel bombs, an indiscriminate weapon banned by international law, but this required further investigation.
