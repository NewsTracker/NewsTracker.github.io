4Kenyan President Uhuru Kenyatta vowed a sharp response to last week's attack at the university.
5Kenyan warplanes attacked suspected militant positions in Somalia, and the government said Wednesday that it was freezing accounts of organizations and individuals suspected of financing Islamic extremists.
6Al-Shabab militants have vowed retribution on Kenya for deploying troops to Somalia in October 2011 to fight the militant group blamed for cross-border attacks.
7The April 2 Garissa University College attack is one of the worst attacks extremists have carried out in the country, second only to the 1998 bombing of the U.S. embassy in Nairobi.
8The opposition has called for the withdrawal of Kenyan troops from Somalia, a call that the government has dismissed.
