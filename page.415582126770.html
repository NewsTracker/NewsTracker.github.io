Closing arguments set in trial over girl's running death

1GADSDEN, Ala.
2(AP) - Closing arguments are set in the capital murder trial of an Alabama woman accused of making her granddaughter run until she died.
3A judge told jurors to return to court Friday morning so prosecutors and defense lawyers could sum up the case of 49-year-old Joyce Hardin Garrard.
4Garrard was the last witness, denying she meant to harm 9-year-old Savannah Hardin.
5Garrard says she made the girl pick up sticks after telling a lie, and she claims she was coaching the girl on running to improve her performance in races at school.
6But prosecutors say Garrard actually made the girl run for hours as punishment.
7They cite a surveillance video that captured Garrard saying she would run the girl and teach her a lesson.
8Jury deliberations will start after closing arguments.
