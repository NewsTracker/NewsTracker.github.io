Militant attacks kill 10 soldiers in Egypt

1CAIRO - Egyptian security officials say two militant ambushes in the restive northern Sinai Peninsula have killed 10 soldiers.
2The two attacks, which also wounded 11 soldiers and several civilians, come as Egypt battles a growing insurgency in the northern Sinai, which borders Israel and the Gaza Strip.
3Attacks mainly targeting Egyptian security forces have spiked since the 2013 military overthrow of Islamist President Mohammed Morsi following massive protests against his divisive rule.
4Most of the biggest attacks have been claimed by a Sinai-based outfit that pledged allegiance to the Islamic State group last year.
5The security officials spoke on condition of anonymity because they were not authorized to brief reporters.
