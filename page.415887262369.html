Death toll rises to 14 in Mexico gas tanker explosion

1The Tabasco state prosecutor's office said through its official Twitter account that two more people had died.
2The Civil Defense agency says the 15,800-gallon (60, 000-liter) tanker crashed on the road between Villahermosa and Coatzacoalcos in southeastern Mexico on Thursday.
3Area residents ignored authorities' warnings to stay away and instead tried to take gasoline from the damaged tanker and many suffered serious burns when it exploded.
4In addition to those killed, more than 20 people were injured.
5Authorities had not been able to interview the injured.
