Denver Nuggets player Ty Lawson arrested on DUI allegations (Yahoo Sports)

1DENVER, CO - APRIL 12: Ty Lawson #3 of the Denver Nuggets handles the ball against the Sacramento Kings on April 12, 2015 at the Pepsi Center in Denver, Colorado.
2LOS ANGELES (AP) -- Denver Nuggets point guard Ty Lawson has been arrested in Los Angeles on suspicion of driving under the influence of alcohol - his second DUI arrest this year.
3California Highway Patrol Officer Jennifer Cassidy says Lawson was driving at a high rate of speed before being pulled over around 2 a.m. Tuesday on state Route 101 in Hollywood.
4Cassidy says Lawson displayed several signs of intoxication.
5Jail records show he was released on $5,000 bail.
6The Denver Nuggets said the team was aware of the arrest and would have no further comment.
7A message seeking comment from Lawson's agent, Happy Walters, was not immediately returned Tuesday.
8Lawson was arrested on suspicion of DUI last January in Denver.
9He is expected in court in that case on Friday.
