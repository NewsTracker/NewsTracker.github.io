US prosecutors in New York City charge 5th man in Islamic State group recruitment plot case

1NEW YORK - Another man is facing charges in New York City that he was involved in a plot to recruit U.S. fighters to join the Islamic State group.
2Akmal Zakirov becomes the fifth defendant charged in an indictment filed in federal court in Brooklyn.
3The name of his attorney wasn't immediately available Thursday.
4Prosecutors accused Zakirov of plotting to pay for one of his co-defendants to fly to the Middle East to join the Islamic State group.
5The man was intercepted at Kennedy airport in February before he could board a flight to Turkey.
