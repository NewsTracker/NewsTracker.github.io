7For real, it's that easy?

11It was scored a ''bad-hop single'' that drove home the first two runs off Jeff Samardzija (4-3).

26Beltre was put on the disabled list, effective Monday, after getting hurt on a slide into second base Sunday.

28Gallo, chosen 39th overall as a supplemental pick in the June 2012 amateur draft, was hitting .314 with nine homers and 31 RBIs in 34 games for Double-A Frisco.
