Earthquake of magnitude 6.1 sends Greek islanders rushing into street

1Earthquake of magnitude 6.1 sends Greek islanders rushing into street Add to...
2An earthquake of magnitude 6.1 struck around 50 km off the eastern tip of the Greek Mediterranean island of Crete, the U.S. Geological Survey said.
3USGS located the quake 152 km east-southeast of the island's main town, Heraklion.
4It revised the magnitude from a previous 6.3.
5Greek fire brigade officials in Athens said the quake had been felt on the island but that there were no immediate reports of damage of casualties.
6A local police official in Crete said the quake had been strong enough to send some of the island's residents rushing into the streets.
