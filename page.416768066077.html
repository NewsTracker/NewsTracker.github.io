Mexico City probes metro collision; worker dies in aftermath

1MEXICO CITY (AP) " Mexico City authorities are investigating whether bad weather or excessive speed may have played a part in a two-train metro crash that injured 12 people.
2Metro transportation general director Joel Ortega Cuevas says a worker died in the aftermath of the accident.
3Ortega says the worker fell and hit his head while one of the trains was being towed away from the above-ground station where the crash occurred.
4Witnesses said the accident happened Monday evening during a heavy hail storm along Line 5 of the metro system near Mexico City's international airport.
5Six people remain hospitalized including the drivers of both trains.
6Service on Line 5 was restored late Tuesday morning.
