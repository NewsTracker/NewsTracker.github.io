Shootout, 3 bodies found raise fears in Mexico City

1MEXICO CITY (AP) " A violent attempted jailbreak and the discovery of two hacked-up bodies is once again raising fears that provincial drug cartel violence is finding its way into Mexico City.
2Mexico City Mayor Miguel Angel Mancera said Monday three men sneaked into a police station posing as relatives and lawyer of a detainee over the weekend.
3The gang then opened fire inside the police station and fled with the suspect, who had been caught with an assault rifle.
4One of the assailants was killed in the Sunday shootout at the station.
5The suspect and one of the shooters were quickly recaptured.
6Also over the weekend, employees at a freight train station in Mexico City discovered two hacked-up bodies in 11 bags on an incoming train.
