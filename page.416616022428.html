Apple finds defects with Watch: WSJ

1An Apple Watch logo is seen on a display at an Apple Store in Berlin April 10, 2015.
2(Reuters) - Apple Inc (AAPL.O) has found defects with the Apple watch, the Wall Street Journal reported, citing sources.
3A Chinese-made component of the Apple Watch was found to be defective, causing the company to limit availability of the watch, the Journal said, citing people familiar with the matter.
4Apple was not immediately available for comment.
