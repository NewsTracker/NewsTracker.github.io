US seeks extradition of 7 FIFA officials

1Swiss justice officials say they have received a formal request from the United States to extradite seven FIFA officials arrested in May in Zurich.
2The Federal Office of Justice said on Thursday the requests were received on Wednesday, within the deadline required by a bilateral treaty.
3They are based on a US probe into alleged bribery worth more than $US100 million ($A131 million) involving high-ranking officials at soccer's world governing body.
