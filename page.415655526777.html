Morocco dismantles Islamic State-linked network planning attacks, Interior Ministry says

1RABAT, Morocco?
2Moroccan authorities have announced the dismantling of a militant network linked to the Islamic State group across nine cities that had been planning attacks.
3The statement by the Interior Ministry Sunday didn't specify how many were arrested, but said the network was present in several cities, including Agadir, Tangiers, Taroudant and Marrakech.
4In the southern coastal city of Agadir, police seized firearms and a large quantity of ammunition that was to be used in attacks against military and political figures, it said.
