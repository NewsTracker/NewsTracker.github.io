Boko Haram again attacks northeast Nigerian city Maiduguri,

1MAIDUGURI, Nigeria - Witnesses say Boko Haram is again attacking the northeastern city of Maiduguri where explosions, heavy gunfire and the screech of a jet fighter were heard around midnight.
2The insurgents have been targeting Maiduguri since newly installed President Muhammadu Buhari announced Friday he is moving the military headquarters of the war from Abuja, the capital in central Nigeria, to Maiduguri.
3The city is the insurgents' birthplace and the biggest city in northeast Nigeria.
4There was no word if there were any casualties in this latest attack.
