6.1 magnitude earthquake strikes near Greek island

1ATHENS, Greece (AP) - A magnitude 6.1 earthquake struck near the Greek island of Crete late Thursday, but there were no immediate reports of injuries or serious damage.
2The Athens Geodynamic Institute said the undersea quake occurred at 9:07 p.m. (1807GMT).
3No tsunami warning was issued.
4The institute's seismologist Efthymios Lekkas said the quake was felt on the islands of Crete, Karpathos and Rhodes.
5"I would recommend that people in the affected regions remain calm," Lekkas told state television.
6Earthquakes are common in Greece and neighboring Turkey.
7Lekkas said Thursday's event occurred along a fault system that frequently produces earthquakes.
