Apple Watch sales plummet

1Sales of the Apple Watch have fallen by 90 per cent since the gadget was released in April, research has shown.
2While Apple has not yet released official figures, a report by market research firm Slice Intelligence claims sales of the iWatch, which starts at 299, dipped to 4,000 a day last month.
3This is far less than the 200,000 a day sold in its first week - and the 10million iPhone 6 handsets snapped up during the weekend after they were released.
