14The Apple Watch isn't the only gadget available this week.
15Here are five other new devices and services targeting your wallet.
16Apple's new laptop, simply called MacBook, debuts Friday.
17It's the company's lightest and thinnest laptop so far, making the ultra-thin MacBook Air appear bulky by comparison.
18Apple borrowed many design techniques from the iPad, including eliminating the fan.
19The new MacBook comes in silver, gold or gray - the first time there has been a color choice since Apple ditched plastic for metal on its laptops.
20The base model is $1,299.
21You get double the storage and a faster processor for $1,599.
22Samsung's Galaxy S6 phones and the HTC One M9 make their general U.S. debut in retail stores.
23(Online orders have been accepted for a few weeks).
24Samsung swapped its plastic back cover for more stylish metal and glass to better compete with the iPhone.
25It also improved the camera and software interface.
26HTC also improved its camera and has added home-screen personalization based on location.
27Prices vary by carrier.
28No-contract versions generally start at about $650.
29In time for the return of "Game of Thrones" this Sunday, HBO launched its stand-alone streaming service, HBO Now, priced at $15 a month.
30Initially, it's available only to owners of an Apple TV, iPhone or iPad, or to Cablevision's Internet-access subscribers.
31HBO Now offers the same content as HBO Go, the companion app to HBO's cable channels.
32The difference is you don't need a cable or satellite TV subscription.
33You could also pay $15 a month to watch HBO shows online through Dish's Sling TV service.
34That's on top of Sling TV's $20 monthly fee for nearly two dozen other cable channels, including ESPN and CNN.
35Sling TV made HBO available Thursday.
36SPEAKING OF STREAMING.
37CBS' $6-a-month streaming service, All Access, is now available on Roku - the first streaming TV device for the network.
38The subscription offers live feeds of CBS stations in some markets - 14 with CBS-owned stations, plus 56 represented by affiliates under deals announced Thursday.
39All Access also offers a bigger library of on-demand content than what's available for free.
40Meanwhile, the Roku 3 player now has a new remote control with a voice search button that customers can use to see what's available across multiple streaming services such as Netflix.
41Another feature tracks when a movie you're interested in becomes available for streaming or gets a price cut.
