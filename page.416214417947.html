Nicaragua asks US to remove embassy worker over Ebola worry

1MANAGUA, Nicaragua (AP) " Nicaragua has asked the United States to remove an embassy worker who was in Liberia because it fears the person may have been exposed to the Ebola virus that has devastated several West African countries.
2Carlos Saenz, an official at Nicaragua's health ministry, told reporters Monday the U.S. was asked to help transport the unnamed 51-year-old worker to the United States.
3Saenz says the embassy worker was quarantined.
4The government is examining people who had contact with the worker.
5The U.S. embassy in Managua said in a statement that the employee was in Liberia, but denied there had been any contact with Ebola patients.
6It said the worker had been examined at the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention before traveling to Nicaragua.
