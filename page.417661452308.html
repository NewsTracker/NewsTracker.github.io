Suicide bomber targets ancient Egyptian temple in Luxor

1LUXOR, Egypt: Egyptian officials say a suicide bomber has blown himself up at the ancient temple of Karnak in Luxor, a southern city frequented by millions of foreign and Egyptian tourists every year.
2They say there is no immediate word on casualties from Wednesday's late morning attack.
3They say police have foiled two other suicide attacks also targeting the Nile-side temple, one of the country's main tourist attractions, rivaling the pyramids at Giza.
4The officials spoke on condition of anonymity because they were not authorized to speak to the media.
