Police in Spain arrest 10 people for suspected links to Islamic State

1Police in northeastern Spain say they have arrested 10 people for suspected links to jihadi terror activity and the Islamic State armed group.
2A Catalan regional police statement said the arrests took place Wednesday in five northeastern towns, including the regional capital of Catalonia, Barcelona.
3It said some 360 Mossos d'Esquadra regional police officers took part in 13 raids in one of the region's biggest operations against jihadi activity.
4The operation was co-ordinated by the National Court in Madrid.
5Spain has arrested dozens of suspected jihadi militants and recruiters in recent years.
6Last week, police in Catalonia arrested four members of a family, including 16-year-old twin boys who were allegedly about to travel to Syria to become jihadi fighters.
