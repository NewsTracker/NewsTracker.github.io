Wisconsin Man Accused of Trying to Join Islamic State Group

1A Wisconsin man is accused of traveling to the Middle East in an attempt to join the Islamic State group.
2Thirty-four-year-old Joshua Van Haften was arrested Wednesday night at O'Hare International Airport in Chicago.
3A criminal complaint charges him with attempting to provide material support to a foreign terrorist organization.
4Van Haften is scheduled to appear Thursday morning in federal court in Madison.
5The complaint alleges Van Haften, of Madison, had talked to people about his plan before leaving the United States.
6It says he left the United States in August for Istanbul.
7The complaint cites Facebook posts from Van Haften that said he was unable to cross the border from Turkey into Syria.
8He had just returned on a flight from Turkey when he was arrested.
