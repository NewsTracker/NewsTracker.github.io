Mexico won't send contestant to Miss Universe

1Mexico's national beauty pageant director confirms the country won't send a contestant to the Miss Universe contest because of insulting remarks by Donald Trump, an owner of the pageant.
2Lupita Jones says via her official Twitter account that she was offended and angry "like everyone else" with Trump over his comments referring to Mexican immigrants as criminals and rapists.
3Jones was Miss Universe 1991 and runs the Mexico competition.
4She said Trump damaged the pageant and its tradition of convening countries for an event that showcases "friendship, unity and breaking down cultural barriers."
5She lauded Mexico's media giant Televisa for deciding not to air the pageant in announcing that Mexico would not participate.
