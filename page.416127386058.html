Lebanese police kill prominent Islamic militant in shootout

1BEIRUT (AP) - Lebanese police say they have killed a prominent Islamic militant who was wanted on terrorism-related charges in a shootout in the country's north.
2A statement issued by police on Friday says Osama Mansour was shot dead along with an accomplice in the city of Tripoli during a security operation in the area the previous night.
3The operation sought to arrest a radical cleric, Sheikh Khaled Hoblos.
4The statement says Mansour opened fire on the police trying to arrest Hoblos and wounded two policemen.
5Police then returned the fire, killing the two men.
6Mansour is wanted in Lebanon on several charges including plotting attacks against the Lebanese army and is an associate of Lebanon's top fugitive Shadi Mawlawi.
7The police said he was wearing an explosives belt when he was shot.
