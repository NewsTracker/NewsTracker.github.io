4 dead, 16 injured in Gulf of Mexico oil rig fire

1The Abkatun Permanente platform, which sits in the middle of Campeche Bay, erupted in fire in the early morning and forced the evacuation of nearly 300 workers, Pemex said.
2Two people were in serious condition as the injured were treated at a hospital in Ciudad del Carmen.
3A city emergency services spokesman contradicted Pemex's account and said at least 45 people were injured, RT News reported.
4It wasn't immediately clear how the blaze started as eight firefighting boats tried to extinguish it.
5Pemex representatives did not immediately respond to the Daily News' request for comment.
6General Director Emilio Lozoya Austin is overseeing the accident response team from an emergency center in Paraiso, Pemex said.
7In 2011, another Campeche Bay Pemek platform sank without causing injuries.
8Campeche Bay is the highest oil-producing region in Mexico.
