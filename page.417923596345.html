3 New Mexico teen boys charged with cyberattack on Enfamil baby formula website

1ALBUQUERQUE, N.M. - Authorities say three Rio Rancho, New Mexico, teenage boys orchestrated a cyberattack on the Enfamil baby formula website, attracting the attention of the FBI and Secret Service.
2The Albuquerque Journal reports that Rio Rancho police have charge the boys with felony computer abuse and conspiracy.
3Police say the boys, all juveniles, used a school computer at V.
4Sue Cleveland High School shortly before the beginning of summer break.
5The high school began investigating on May 20 after learning of the cyberattack from the Secret Service.
6The school's report says the three students sent vulgar messages on the Enfamil site during a robotics class.
7When Enfamil blocked them, they asked other hackers to bombard the site with messages.
8The families of the boys either would not comment or could not be reached.
