Mexico bans poultry, egg imports from bird flu-hit Iowa

1MEXICO CITY (Reuters) - Mexico, the biggest buyer of U.S. chicken, has halted imports of live birds and eggs from the U.S. state of Iowa due to an outbreak of deadly bird flu there, the Mexican government said on Tuesday.
2Mexico and other major countries last month imposed new export restrictions on poultry products from various U.S. states, but the outbreak has since spread to top U.S. egg-producing state Iowa.
