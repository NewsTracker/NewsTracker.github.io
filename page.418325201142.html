Mexico rescues 12 Cubans from makeshift raft

1MEXICO CITY (AP) - The Mexican Navy says it has rescued 12 Cuban men who were found aboard a makeshift raft off the coast of the Yucatan Peninsula.
2Photos supplied by the Navy on Monday showed a raft apparently made of polystyrene foam blocks tied to a framework of wooden boards.
3The Navy said the rescue occurred Sunday, after a fishing boat spotted the raft adrift.
4It had no apparent means of propulsion.
