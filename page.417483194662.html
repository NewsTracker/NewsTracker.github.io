2 Civilians Killed in Surge of Shelling in East Ukraine

1Authorities in east Ukraine say two civilians in a government-controlled area have been killed amid a surge of shelling as combat flares between the armed forces and separatist rebels.
2Luhansk region governor Hennadiy Moskal said in a statement on his official website that an elderly couple died when their car was struck by mortar fire Wednesday morning.
3The statement said the strike occurred 20 kilometers (12 miles) into government-held territory.
4As Ukraine's conflict shows little sign of abating, talks on implementing a shaky February cease-fire agreement have foundered.
5Negotiators from the warring sides met in Belarus on Tuesday, but the session concluded without obvious progress.
