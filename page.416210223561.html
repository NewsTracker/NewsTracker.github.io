Microsoft hires back Kurt DelBene for corporate strategy job

1Microsoft said Monday that it has hired back Kurt DelBene to head its corporate strategy and planning.
2DelBene was president of Microsoft's Office unit when he left the company in 2013.
3After Microsoft, he was put in charge of HealthCare.org, the website used as an insurance portal for President Barack Obama's health care law.
4Most recently, he was a partner and venture capital firm at Madrona Venture Group, which invests and advises technology startups.
5In his new position, DelBene will report to Microsoft Corp. CEO Satya Nadella.
