Google to include tweets in search results

1Google and Twitter have announced a partnership to display tweets in search results, renewing a tie-up that ended in 2011.
2The move will allow Google to get more real-time results in its search queries, and help Twitter boost engagement after a period of sluggish user growth which has weighed on its stock price.
3"We're excited to team up with Google to bring Twitter's unique, real-time content to Google's search results," Twitter vice president Jana Messerschmidt said on Tuesday.
4The deal will start with search results within the Google app and mobile Web, with a desktop version coming, said Messerschmidt.
5"For example, if you're interested in hearing more from Taylor Swift, a quick search on Google will pull up her most recent tweets," she said in a blog post.
6"Or, if you're a TV buff, a search for #Madmen will bring up the most relevant news and Tweets about Sunday's series finale."
7The two firms had a similar arrangement dating back to 2009, but tweets disappeared from Google search results in 2011.
8"It's a great way to get real-time info when something is happening," Google product manager Ardan Arac said in a separate post.
9"And it's another way for organisations and people on Twitter to reach a global audience at the most relevant moments."
10Microsoft's Bing search engine has a similar function of integrating Twitter messages.
