Nepal earthquake: US Marine helicopter reported "missing" with eight crew and emergency supplies

1A US Marine Corps helicopter carrying out earthquake relief in Nepal was reported missing on Tuesday with eight personnel on board, officials have said.
2"The incident occurred near Charikot, Nepal while the aircraft was conducting humanitarian assistance and disaster relief operations," said Major Dave Eastburn, spokesman for US Pacific Command.
3About 300 American personnel are on the ground in Nepal.
4The cause of the disappearance was not immediately clear and the incident is under investigation, said CNN.
