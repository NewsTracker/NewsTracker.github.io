1The party has issued a pre-action letter threatening a judicial review of the decision by the broadcaster.

10Viewers would have the opportunity to see Ukip perform head to head with the main parties but not the DUP.
11This would mislead people on the respective importance of these parties "in terms of seats in Parliament and therefore influence on the political landscape", the letter said.

13The BBC said it was aware of the DUP letter, but is not commenting on it.
14But BBC chief adviser Ric Bailey has already argued that the parties in NI are in competition for votes with each other rather than the national parties.
