CANNES WATCH: Longoria a success behind the camera

1Eva Longoria poses for a portrait photograph at the 68th international film festival, Cannes, southern France, Thursday, May 21, 2015.
2There's been much talk at the Cannes Film Festival about how to boost the ranks of women directors and producers.
3Eva Longoria is a good example of a success story.
4Though she gained fame for "Desperate Housewives," the actress has turned into a successful producer and director.
5She executive produces the TV series "Devious Maids" among other series, and has directed as well.
6"I love producing, I love directing. I like being behind the camera because you have more control over the final product and the entire project and so, for me, I will always continue to produce," she said.
7Longoria, who is in Cannes in part as an ambassador for L'Oreal, said the new digital age of TV, and various streaming services are helping to improve diversity in Hollywood.
