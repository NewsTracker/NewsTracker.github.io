Hernandez goal puts Real Madrid into Champions League semis (Yahoo Sports)

1MADRID (AP) -- A late goal by Javier ''Chicharito'' Hernandez gave Real Madrid a tense 1-0 win over 10-man Atletico Madrid and a place in the Champions League semifinals on Wednesday.
2After the first leg of their quarterfinal had finished 0-0 last week, Hernandez missed three good chances to break the deadlock for the holders before finding the net in the 88th minute from close range following a pass by Cristiano Ronaldo.
3By then, Atletico was down to 10 men after midfielder Arda Turan was sent off for a second yellow card following a rash challenge on Madrid's Sergio Ramos in the 76th minute.
4As in the first leg of the derby between last season's finalists, Atletico goalkeeper Jan Oblak kept his team in the tie by saving a series of shots on goal by Madrid's forwards.
5In the night's other match, Juventus drew 0-0 at Monaco to reach the semifinals with a 1-0 victory on aggregate.
6At the Santiago Bernabeu, Madrid began with four changes to its side from last week's first leg.
7Coach Carlo Ancelotti was missing suspended Marcelo in defense, while Luka Modric in midfield, and forwards Gareth Bale and Karim Benzema were injured.
8Atletico was without the suspended Mario Suarez in midfield as the teams met for the eighth time this season - with Atletico unbeaten in the previous seven meetings.
9In a rarely-seen tactical strategy, Ancelotti moved Ramos up to midfield, with Pepe behind in central defense.
10Hernandez was given Benzema's role as sole striker.
11Madrid made a bright start and Ronaldo missed a good chance in the 13th minute when he shot just wide of the near post.
12Atletico's Jesus Gamez conceded a free kick in the 27th after a clumsy foul on Ronaldo but James Rodriguez could only send his shot over the crossbar.
13Madrid goalkeeper Iker Casillas made a good save from Gamez in the 29th in Atletico's first effort on goal, while Hernandez headed over the bar at the other end two minute later from a precise cross by Rodriguez.
14Oblak made yet another excellent save to deny Ronaldo just before the break.
15Madrid kept up the pressure after the restart and Hernandez missed a chance when, having controlled the ball with his back to goal, he spun around and unleashed a shot that flew just wide of the far post.
16Raphael Varane cleared what could have been a good chance for Atletico in the 59th and Madrid quickly responded with a header by Ramos which Oblak was able to smother.
17Casillas was forced to make a save in the 69th from midfielder Jorge ''Koke'' Resurreccion's header, while Hernandez missed his third chance on goal four minutes later as he shot across the goalmouth.
18However, the Mexican made no mistake in the 88th when he steered home Ronaldo's pass from the right side of the area.
