NDP candidate resigns after Tories share Israel Facebook comments

1HALIFAX - An NDP candidate in Nova Scotia has resigned after comments he allegedly made in a Facebook discussion emerged on a Conservative website.
2Morgan Wheeldon was the party's candidate in the Annapolis Valley riding of Kings-Hants, but NDP campaign advisor Brad Lavigne said Monday that is no longer the case.
3A party news release didn't provide details of what Lavigne said on Facebook, and the post has been deleted.
4However, the Conservatives are circulating a Facebook screen grab from 2014 where Wheeldon allegedly comments, "one could argue that Israel's intention was always to ethnically cleanse the region."
5Lavigne said the party is aware of information that hadn't been previously disclosed and the candidate's position was "not in line" with the NDP position.
6He said when the party approached Wheeldon with this information, he submitted his resignation.
