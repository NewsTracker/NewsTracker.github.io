Mexico seizes quarter-ton of opium paste in Guerrero state

1The army said Tuesday that the opium paste would have produced about 20 kilograms of processed heroin.
2It said the seizure was made 10 days earlier in a mountainous region of Guerrero state known for opium growing.
3Troops found buried buckets and bags of the gummy paste extracted from poppy flower bulbs.
4Three rifles and almost 300 rounds of ammunition were found nearby.
5An increase in opium growing and trafficking has triggered a rise in violence in Guerrero state, where a drug gang is alleged to have killed and incinerated 43 college students in September.
