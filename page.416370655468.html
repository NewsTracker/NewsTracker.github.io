In lieu of flowers, 'don't vote for Hillary Clinton'

1Hilary Clinton.
2The family of deceased Larry Upright added a line to his obituary they thought he would appreciate -- "do not vote for Hillary Clinton."
3Upright, 81, of Cabarrus County, N.C., died last Monday and when his family was writing his obituary at the funeral home, they decided to use the opportunity to pass along a message from their dear departed diehard Republican about the upcoming 2016 presidential election.
4The family asked that in lieu of flowers, a donation to the Shriners Hospital for Children would be appreciated.
5"Also, the family respectfully asks that you do not vote for Hillary Clinton in 2016. R.I.P. Grandaddy," the obituary read.
6"He was very passionate about politics and probably passed a little of that on, so it was natural for me to think about that," daughter Jill McLain said of adding the anti-Clinton sentence to Upright's obituary, wsotv.com reported.
7"We did this for him," son Mike Upright said.
8"He had nothing to do with it other than voicing his opinion time after time after time.
