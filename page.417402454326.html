Woman tosses rare Apple computer worth $200k

1A US woman has thrown out an original 1976 Apple 1 computer that turned out to be a piece of history worth $US200,000 ($A261,470).
2A recycling centre in the Silicon Valley is looking for a woman who dropped off an old Apple computer that turned out to be a collectible item worth $US200,000 ($A261,470).
3Victor Gichun of Clean Bay Area says the woman dropped off boxes of electronics that she had cleaned out from her garage after her husband died.
4It wasn't until a few weeks later that workers at the recycling centre opened the boxes to discover an Apple I (one) computer inside.
5The San Jose Mercury News reports (http://bayareane.ws/1QiwrkE) it was one of only about 200 first-generation Apple computers made in 1976.
6Gichun sold the computer to a private collection, and he wants to split the proceeds with the mystery donor.
