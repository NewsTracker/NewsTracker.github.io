Former FIFA, UEFA official Mayer-Vorfelder dies at 82

1Gerhard Mayer-Vorfelder, a former FIFA and UEFA official who also served as the president of the German soccer federation, has died.
2He was 82.
3The German federation said Mayer-Vorfelder died in a Stuttgart hospital on Monday.
4The DFB gave no cause of death.
5Mayer-Vorfelder headed the DFB from 2001-06 and oversaw the organization of the 2006 World Cup in Germany before leaving the organization.
6He also served two terms on the FIFA execitive committee and was UEFA vice president from 2007-09.
7Mayer-Vorfelder was also president of Bundesliga club Stuttgart from 1975-2000.
8Stuttgart won two Bundesliga titles and one German Cup during that time.
9Mayer-Vorfelder is survived by his wife Margit and their four children.
