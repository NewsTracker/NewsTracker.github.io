School that lost students in Germanwings crash inaugurates memorial with plaque, cherry trees

1BERLIN - A high school that lost 16 students and two teachers in the Germanwings plane crash in March is inaugurating a memorial to the victims that features 18 cherry trees.
2The memorial being unveiled Friday at the Joseph Koenig high school in the German town of Haltern includes a steel plaque with the 18 victims' names, as well as the trees.
