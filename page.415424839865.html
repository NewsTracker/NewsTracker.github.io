Bowler throws grenade instead of ball in east Ukraine; authorities urge care with explosives

1A separatist-run news agency cited emergency services official Sergei Ivanushkin as saying Friday that the separatist-held parts of Luhansk region have seen a spate of accidents recently because of careless use of explosives.
2Ivanushkin said a man and woman were hospitalized after Thursday's incident.
3The blast occurred at a "cheburechnaya" - a restaurant serving fried pastries - that also offered duckpin bowling, which uses a ball without holes on a relatively short lane.
4East Ukraine has been wracked by war for almost a year, during which time the region has become flooded with small arms and grenades.
