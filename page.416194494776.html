Hillary Clinton Takes Road Trip To Iowa For First Campaign Event

1Clinton left Sunday on a road trip from her home in Chappaqua, New York, in a van headed for Iowa.
2The former secretary of state announced her Democratic presidential bid Sunday and is appearing at her first event in eastern Iowa on Tuesday.
3Longtime aide Huma Abedin tells Clinton alumni during a Sunday night conference call that it was Clinton's idea to make the roughly 1,000-mile trip to Iowa by van.
4She says the van stopped at a gas station in Pennsylvania, where Clinton met a family from Michigan.
5Abedin says "it's safe to say she surprised quite a few people" who had stopped at the gas station.
