Obama: Soccer fans want FIFA to operate with integrity

1ELMAU, Germany (AP) - President Barack Obama says soccer fans around the world want to ensure FIFA operates with integrity.
2Obama weighed in on the FIFA corruption scandal during a visit to Germany.
3He says in conversations with people in Europe, he's heard concerns about the importance of FIFA operating with transparency and accountability.
4Obama says he can't comment on the ongoing investigation by the U.S. Justice Department.
5He says soccer is not only a game but a massive business.
6He says it's also a source of incredible national pride.
7Obama says the U.S. - not traditionally a soccer powerhouse - has been getting better and better at each World Cup.
8He says Americans want to make sure that a sport gaining popularity in the U.S. is conducted in an upright manner.
