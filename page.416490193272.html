Amazon, Microsoft lead gains in big technology stocks

1NEW YORK (AP) - Big gains by Amazon, Microsoft and Google sending technology stocks to a higher close.
2The broader market also rose Friday, but not as much.
3The Nasdaq composite rose again, a day after beating its record-high close from the peak of the dot-com boom 15 years ago.
4Amazon soared 14 percent after revealing details about its cloud computing service for the first time.
5Microsoft and Google also rose after reporting their latest quarterly results.
6The Nasdaq composite gained 36 points, or 0.7 percent, to 5,092.
7The Dow Jones industrial average rose 21 points, or 0.1 percent, to 18,080.
8The Standard & Poor's 500 rose four points, or 0.2 percent, to 2,117.
9The S&P 500 also closed at a record high, beating its mark from March 2 by less than a point.
