FIFA's Valcke cancels trip to Women's World Cup in Canada

1ZURICH (AP) " FIFA Secretary General Jerome Valcke has canceled his trip to Canada for the opening of the Women's World Cup in the wake of an ongoing investigation into soccer corruption.
2FIFA spokeswoman Delia Fischer said Monday that "due to the current situation," Valcke will not attend this weekend's opening game "as previously scheduled."
3Fischer said "it is important that he attend to matters at FIFA's headquarters in Zurich."
4She added that FIFA President Sepp Blatter still intends to travel to the CONCACAF Gold Cup, which is hosted by the United States and Canada in July.
5Several high-ranking FIFA officials were arrested in Zurich last week in a U.S.-led probe into corruption.
6The women's World Cup starts Saturday with Canada playing China in Edmonton.
