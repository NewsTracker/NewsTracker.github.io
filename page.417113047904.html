3NEW YORK -- Apple is introducing an updated MacBook Pro and iMac and lowering the price for its top-end iMac.
4The updated 15-inch MacBook Pro laptop has a new style of trackpad, faster flash storage and a longer battery life and starts at $1,999.
5The new $1,999 iMac desktop computer has a higher-resolution 27-inch screen and faster processor.
6Apple's top-end iMac will now sell for $2,299.
7It has been selling for $2,499 at some retail outlets.
8The news comes ahead of Apple's worldwide developers conference June 8.
