15The Durst family owns a sprawling real estate company, The Durst Organization, that was founded in 1915 by Joseph Durst, Robert Durst's grandfather.
16Leadership of the company passed on from Joseph to his son Seymour Durst, who expanded the company.
17The multimillion dollar company currently owns several skyscrapers, including the Bank of America Tower in Manhattan.
18Both Robert and his brother Douglas joined the company, though Douglas was later chosen to head the company as chairman.
19After Robert's arrest, Douglas released a statement expressing gratitude to all who assisted in Durst's arrest and hoping that Robert "will finally be held accountable for all he has done."
20How did his grandfather start the family business?
21The Durst Organization had humble beginnings in 1902 when, according to the corporation's website, Joseph Durst, a tailor from Gorlice, Austria (present-day Poland), arrived in the United States with $3 to his name.
22After a stint in the dressmaking business, he bought his first building in 1915.
23The firm expanded quickly in the 1960s under his son Seymour, building a series of towers on Manhattan's Avenue of the Americas and creating the first building in the city that had a fully integrated heating and cooling system.
24What is his relationship with his immediate family?
25Durst has been at odds with his family for years.
26According to the New York Post, Durst has been permanently ostracized from his wealthy family - last year, he was arrested for trespassing on his brother Douglas' property on the West Side of Manhattan - and more than a dozen family members have taken out restraining orders against him.
27Durst was arrested Saturday night for the 15-year-old murder of writer and Durst family friend Berman.
28Durst's case gained additional attention after the HBO documentary focusing on his life, The Jinx: The Life and Deaths of Robert Durst, aired its sixth and final episode Sunday, ending its run with an over two-year- old clip.
29Durst can be heard mumbling to himself in a bathroom "What the hell did I do? Killed them all of course."
