Backpack meth lab discovered in Indiana Walmart bathroom

1A restroom at a Walmart in eastern Indiana has been closed indefinitely after an employee discovered a working meth lab inside.
2State police say a Walmart employee alerted police after seeing a man he described as suspicious enter the restroom about 11:30 p.m. local time Thursday with a backpack and leave without it.
3The Star Press reports that members of a state police meth suppression team removed the dangerous chemicals.
4Delaware County Health Department inspectors closed the restroom and a nearby women's restroom until they could be "decontaminated" by a professional cleaning company.
5State police say people who make methamphetamine are leaving "the deadly explosive chemicals in public places to return later to get the finished product," rather than risk explosions and contamination at their own homes.
