Facebook, 9 Media Outlets Launch 'Instant Articles' Platform To Directly Publish News On Mobile Feeds

1(Reuters) - Facebook Inc tied up with nine news publishers to launch "Instant Articles" that will let them publish articles directly to the social network's mobile news feeds.
2Instant Articles will let stories load more than 10 times faster than standard mobile web articles and will include content from publishers such as the New York Times, BuzzFeed and National Geographic, Facebook said in a blog post on its website.
3"Instant Articles lets them deliver fast, interactive articles while maintaining control of their content and business models," said Facebook Chief Product Officer Chris Cox.
4The news publishers can either sell and embed advertisements in the articles and keep all of the revenue, or allow Facebook to sell ads.
5The Internet social networking company will also let the news companies track data and traffic through comScore and other analytics tools.
6The other launch partners for Instant Articles are NBC, The Atlantic, The Guardian, BBC News, Spiegel and Bild, Facebook said.
