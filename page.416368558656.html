3PHILADELPHIA (AP) -- It's Tebow Time in the City of Brotherly Love.
4Tim Tebow is expected to sign a one-year contract with the Eagles on Monday, according to three people familiar with the deal.
5The people spoke to The Associated Press on condition of anonymity Sunday because the contract hasn't been finalized.
6Tebow hasn't played in the NFL since 2012 with the New York Jets.
7He was released by the New England Patriots before the 2013 season and spent last year working in television as an analyst for the SEC Network and ESPN.
8Despite being out of the league, the 27-year-old Tebow remained one of the most popular players around.
9He has a legion of fans who follow him because of his strong Christian beliefs.
10The former Heisman Trophy winner led Florida to two national titles and was a first-round pick by Denver in 2010.
11He started 16 games during two seasons with the Broncos, including a playoff victory over Pittsburgh in January 2012.
12Tebow was traded to the Jets after Denver signed Peyton Manning.
13The 27-year-old Tebow joins quarterbacks Sam Bradford, Mark Sanchez, Matt Barkley and G.J. Kinne on Philadelphia's roster.
14The addition of Tebow would be the latest move in a stunning offseason for coach Chip Kelly.
15He traded Nick Foles, LeSean McCoy and allowed Jeremy Maclin to leave in free agency.
16Tebow had some success in Denver, but his inaccurate passing and lack of pocket presence was an issue.
17His strength has been running the ball or improvising.
18Tebow has completed just 47.9 percent of his passes for 2,422 yards, 17 touchdowns and nine interceptions.
19He has 989 yards rushing, a 5.0 average yards per carry and 12 TDs.
20Tebow worked with former major league pitcher Tom House on improving his mechanics.
21He joins the Eagles in time to start their offseason program this week.
