Soldiers Kill 6 Presumed Criminals in Northern Mexico

1Authorities in the northern Mexican state of Tamaulipas say soldiers have killed six presumed criminals who shot at them near the border with the United States.
2The Tamaulipas state government says in a statement released Saturday that gunmen traveling in two trucks opened fire on soldiers patrolling a rural area of the community of Matamoros across the border from Brownsville, Texas.
3The statement says the soldiers returned fire, killing six of the gunmen.
4None of the soldiers were reported wounded or killed.
5Authorities have not released the identities of the suspected criminals who died, nor have they said whether they were believed to be members of a known criminal group.
6Tamaulipas state has been plagued by cartel violence in recent years.
