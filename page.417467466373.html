British actor fighting IS in video

1A British actor who has had minor roles in Hollywood films has joined Kurdish fighters battling the Islamic State group in Syria.
2Michael Enright, who played a deckhand in Pirates Of The Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest, appears in a video released by the Kurdish People's Protection Units, known as the YPG.
3It shows him in a trench with other fighters firing an assault rifle.
4In the video, posted on the YPG's Facebook page, Mr Enright says "Isis are dangerous to every human being alive."
5He calls for weapons and medical aid for the Kurdish fighters, describing them as "my havals", the Kurdish word for comrades.
6The actor also played minor roles in Knight And Day and Old Dogs, according to the IMDb online entertainment database.
