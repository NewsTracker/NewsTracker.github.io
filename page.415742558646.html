4 killed when Ukraine bus hits land mine

1The Ukrainian Interior Ministry's branch for the Donetsk region said the explosion occurred when a bus traveling from Artemivsk to Horlivka took a turn to bypass a checkpoint and hit the mine on a dirt road.
2It posted photos of the bus badly disfigured by the blast, its side panel torn off.
3The regional administration appointed by the Ukrainian government said on Twitter that the wounded have been taken to a hospital in Artemivsk.
4Large areas in eastern Ukraine have been covered with land mines following fierce battles between Russian-backed separatists and Ukrainian government forces.
