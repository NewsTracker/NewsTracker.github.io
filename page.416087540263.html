Bearing down: Jack Nicklaus makes hole-in-one at Par-3 (Yahoo Sports)

1(AP) -- Jack Nicklaus thrilled the crowd Wednesday at the annual Masters par-3 contest, making a hole-in-one on the fourth hole.
2The six-time Masters champion, playing in the exhibition at age 75, spun an iron shot back into the hole for the ace.
3After his ball bounced twice then took a third hop before spinning back into the hole, Nicklaus was congratulated by Ben Crenshaw and Gary Player.
4Nicklaus acknowledged the ovation from the crowd, raising his arms in the air with a double fist-pump.
5The 63-year-old Crenshaw will tee off for his 44th and final Masters on Thursday.
