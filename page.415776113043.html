Boston woman charged with fraudulently accepting benefits for marathon bombing injuries

1Joanna Leigh was indicted on charges of larceny and making a false claim to a government agency.
2Prosecutors say she received $8,000 from The One Fund, a charity established to help bombing victims; $18,000 from a state victims' compensation fund; $9,000 from an online fundraiser; and $1,700 raised by students at a Boston school.
3Authorities say she was at the marathon in April 2013, but was not injured.
4The 41-year-old Leigh says she suffered traumatic brain injuries, and the charges are retaliation for her criticism of The One Fund for not benefiting people with such injuries.
