Ex-CIA man convicted of Iran leak seeks lenient sentence

1ALEXANDRIA, Virginia (AP) " Lawyers for a former CIA officer convicted of leaking details of a secret mission to derail Iran's nuclear ambitions say he deserves a light sentence similar to that just handed down to ex-CIA director David Petraeus.
2Jeffrey Sterling will be sentenced next month in federal court in Alexandria.
3On Friday, his lawyers urged a lenient sentence.
4They said a recommendation under federal sentencing guidelines for a term of 20 years or more is excessive.
5The lawyers say similar leaks have resulted in far lighter sentences.
6Petraeus was sentenced Thursday to two years' probation for giving classified material to his biographer and former mistress.
7Former CIA veteran John Kiriakou received a sentence of 2 1/2 years for leaking a covert officer's identity.
8Prosecutors are urging a severe sentence.
