3 Greek soccer players slightly hurt in Hungary taxi crash

1The Greek embassy in Budapest says three national team soccer players were slightly injured when their taxi crashed on its way to the airport.
2Press counselor Nikolaos Vlahakis says Vangelis Moras, Panagiotis Tachtsidis and Ioannis Fetfatzidis were injured Monday morning in a collision with another car.
3Moras and Tachtsidis have already been released from a Budapest hospital while Fetfatzidis was expected to be released later Monday.
4Vlahakis says the crash was "quite hard" but the players' injuries were not serious.
5Police say one person in the other car died at the scene of the accident.
6Moras and Tachtsidis play for Hellas Verona in Italy, while Fetfatzidis plays for Serie A rival Chievo Verona.
