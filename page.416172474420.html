2Until now, the former first lady has offered only hints of what would drive her if she were to make a second bid to become the first female president.
3Clinton, who lost the 2008 nomination to President Barack Obama, will skip a flashy kickoff rally in favor of conversations with voters about the economic needs of middle class families and the next generation.
4Clinton will enter the race as the overwhelming favorite for her party's nomination.
5Still, her team has said her early strategy is designed to avoid appearing to take that nomination for granted.
6She will contend with Republicans in Congress who are investigating her use of a private email account and server during her four-year tenure as secretary of state.

15Clinton also intends to sell herself as being able to work with Congress, businesses and world leaders.

18The Clinton advisers spoke on condition of anonymity in order to discuss her plans ahead of Sunday's announcement.
19People familiar with the plans say Clinton will travel to Iowa and other key states to hold small events with residents in the days after the video's release.
20By campaigning heavily in the early-voting states, which influence the rest of the state-by-state battle for party nominations, Clinton hopes to avoid making the same stumbles she did in 2008, when she entered the race as a U.S. senator and a heavy favorite only to be upset by Obama in Iowa's lead-off caucuses.
21In New York on Saturday, at the final event put on by "Ready for Hillary," a group not connected with her campaign that's worked for the past few years to stoke excitement for it, enthusiastic supporters joined elected officials and local party leaders to celebrate the launch to come.
22As her official announcement loomed, the Republican National Committee linked Clinton to Obama, a regular focus of criticism from Republicans.

25Clinton is not expected to roll out detailed policy positions in the first weeks of her campaign.

27It's not yet clear whether that will include a noticeable break with Obama on economic policy.
