Woman pleads not guilty to fatally stabbing beau over Xbox

1A New Jersey woman accused of fatally stabbing her boyfriend during a dispute over their Xbox console has pleaded not guilty to aggravated manslaughter.
2NJ.com (http://bit.ly/1JCeizA) reports that 28-year-old Aisha Barr entered her plea Tuesday during a brief appearance in state Superior Court in Essex County.
3She remains jailed on $500,000 bail and wasn't available for comment.
4Authorities say Barr stabbed her 26-year-old boyfriend, Christopher Greene, at their Newark home May 31.
5According to police reports, the couple began arguing over Xbox and the dispute turned physical.
6Authorities say Barr grabbed a kitchen knife and stabbed Greene in the chest.
7He was taken to a hospital, where he died.
