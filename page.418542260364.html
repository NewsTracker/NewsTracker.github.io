Hurricane Dolores strengthens to Category 4 storm in Pacific

1MIAMI (AP) " Hurricane Dolores has rapidly strengthened to a powerful Category 4 storm in the Pacific off Mexico's coast.
2The storm's maximum sustained winds early Wednesday are near 130 mph (215 kph) with some additional strengthening forecast during the next day.
3But the U.S. National Hurricane Center says the hurricane will begin weakening Thursday.
4Dolores is centered about 320 miles (510 kilometers) south of Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, and is moving west-northwest near 7 mph (11 kph).
5Swells generated by the hurricane are affecting parts of Mexico's southwestern coast along with the southern coast of the Baja California peninsula.
6The swells could cause life-threatening surf and rip current conditions.
