Ferguson police involved in a standoff after reports of shooting

1FERGUSON, Mo.
2- Tactical officers have surrounded a home in the St. Louis County town of Ferguson following reports of a shooting apparently involving two brothers.
3Officers responded to the home Tuesday afternoon.
4Ferguson police say a man shot his brother in the head and barricaded himself inside a home.
5Police say the victim was alert when he was taken by ambulance to a hospital.
6Officers in tactical gear were called to the home, prompting a standoff.
7The home is in a residential area of Ferguson.
8The St. Louis suburb has been at the center of attention since August, when 18-year-old Michael Brown, who was black, was fatally shot by a white Ferguson police officer.
9A middle school and an elementary school near the home were on lockdown during the standoff.
