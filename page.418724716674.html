Egypt court sentences Mubarak-era premier to 5 years in prison in corruption case retrial

1CAIRO - Egypt's official news agency is reporting that a Cairo court has convicted a former prime minister who served under deposed autocrat Hosni Mubarak of illegally amassing wealth while in office, sentencing him to five years in prison in a retrial.
2Wednesday's verdict follows an appeal from Ahmed Nazif of an earlier three-year sentence.
3He was accused of illegally amassing $8.2 million while in office.
4Nazif was not in court.
5He still can appeal the verdict.
6Nazif had been acquitted in a separate retrial of charges of wrongfully awarding contracts for supplying new car license plates.
