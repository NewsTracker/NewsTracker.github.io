Islamic State blows up Syrian church - state news agency

1SANA, which did not report any casualties, said the militants had planted explosives inside the Church of the Virgin Mary in Tel Nasri, an Assyrian village in an area where Christian and Kurdish militia have been battling Islamic State.
2Islamic State controls the village, the news agency said on Monday.
3The militant group, which controls swathes of Syria and Iraq, espouses a fiercely purist school of Sunni Islam, deeming many other Muslims to be heretics.
4Its fighters have destroyed Shi'ite and Sufi religious sites and also attacked churches.
