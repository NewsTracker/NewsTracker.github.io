16Islamic State captors also had a big problem with the hostages' smell, telling them "you smell like pigs", to which Mr Espinosa thought: "Of course, we haven't showered for three months."
17Because of their stench the captives were left in a freezing cell in winter.
18The hostages were split up at different stages but were eventually put together again where they had 'conferences' led by Mr Foley to keep them busy.
19They asked new British hostage and taxi driver Alan Henning, 47, about news from the outside world and he told them about Nelson Mandela's death and how football teams were doing - talking about anything but their own situations.
20Mr Henning was beheaded in October 2014.
