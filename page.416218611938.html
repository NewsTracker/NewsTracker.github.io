Toyota to invest $1 billion for new Mexico plant, create 2,400 jobs-source

1The 200,000-cars-a-year factory will create about 2,400 direct jobs, one of the people told Reuters, declining to be identified because the plan is not yet public.
2Reuters reported earlier that the factory - Toyota's first to build passenger cars in Mexico - would produce the Corolla compact car from the summer of 2019, ending a self-imposed three-year freeze on new investments.
3President Enrique Pena Nieto will attend the announcement to be held in Mexico on Wednesday, the people said.
4A Toyota spokeswoman in Tokyo said nothing had been decided.
