Faeroe Islands beat Greece again in Euro 2016 qualifying

1FRANKFURT, Germany (AP) " The Faeroe Islands proved the first time wasn't a fluke when it beat Greece again 2-1 in European Championship qualifying on Saturday.
2Meanwhile, Poland and Germany moved closer to clinching spots in next year's tournament in France.
3Brandur Olsen secured the Faeroes' victory in the second half, with Hallur Hansson scoring before the break.
4Sokratis Papastathopoulos scored too late for Greece.
5The Faeroes also won 1-0 in Greece in November for its first competitive away win since 2001, and it cost the job of Greece coach Claudio Ranieri.
6Elsewhere, Poland beat Georgia 4-0 behind a hat trick by striker Robert Lewandowski in the last four minutes, and Andre Schuerrle scored three as Germany routed Gibraltar 7-0.
7Cristiano Ronaldo also had a hat trick as 10-man Portugal rallied for a 3-2 win in Armenia.
8Georgia kept it close and hit the post before Lewandowski made up for missing three big chances in the first half by scoring his quick hat trick.

10Arkadiusz Milik scored Poland's first and set up two of Lewandowski's goals.
11"It's crazy," Lewandowski said.
12Schuerrle also had a hat trick as World Cup champion Germany stayed second in the group by routing Gibraltar 7-0 in Faro, Portugal.
13In Dublin, Scotland rallied to draw with Ireland 1-1.
14Jon Walters scored for Ireland just before halftime, then Shaun Maloney's strike was deflected in by John O'Shea just after the break.
15Poland has 14 points, one more than Germany.
16Scotland has 11, Ireland nine, Georgia three, and Gibraltar none.
17Max Kruse added two goals for Germany, which labored in the first half and wasted an early penalty missed by Bastian Schweinsteiger.
