Nigerian military evacuating, evaluating 293 girls and women rescued from Boko Haram forest

1LAGOS, Nigeria - Nigeria's military says it is moving 200 girls and 93 women from a northeastern forest where they were rescued from Boko Haram extremists.
2Army spokesman Col.
3Sani Usman says many are traumatized.
4He says the military on Wednesday is flying in medical and intelligence teams to establish their psychological and physical health.
5He says they started evacuating them from the Sambisa Forest on Tuesday but would not say to where.
6Military operations continue in the forest where it was announced Tuesday the women and girls were rescued while troops were destroying four Boko Haram camps.
7An intelligence officer and a soldier say Boko Haram used some of the women as armed human shields, a first line of defense who fired at troops.
8They demanded anonymity because the issue is sensitive.
