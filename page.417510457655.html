Hurricane Blanca weakens slightly to Category 3 storm

1MIAMI (AP) - Hurricane Blanca has weakened slightly to a Category 3 storm as it swirls in the eastern Pacific off Mexico's coast.
2The hurricane's maximum sustained winds decreased to near 125 mph (205 kph) early Thursday but the U.S. National Hurricane Center says some re-strengthening is expected during the next day or so.
3Blanca is centered about 490 miles (790 kilometers) south of Manzanillo, Mexico, and has been drifting southwest.
4But it's expected to begin moving northwestward later in the day.
5Swells from the hurricane will begin affecting Mexico's southwestern coast and are likely to cause life-threatening surf and rip current conditions.
6Meanwhile, farther out over the Pacific, Tropical Storm Andres is quickly losing strength and is expected to become a remnant low by Thursday night or early Friday.
