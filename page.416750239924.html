9 police officers kidnapped in northern Mexico; 1 dead

1CIUDAD VICTORIA, Mexico (AP) " An official says nine police officers were kidnapped by a criminal gang in northern Mexico and later rescued, though one was killed in the confrontation.
2The officers from Nuevo Leon state were abducted when they pursued suspects into neighboring Tamaulipas, on the Texas border, Sunday night.
3They were following a vehicle carrying armed men.
4On a highway they encountered two other vehicles full of gunmen.
5One officer was killed, and the others kidnapped.
6A Tamaulipas security official who was not authorized to discuss the incident publicly and spoke on condition of anonymity said Monday that the events took place in Ciudad Mier, near the border.
7The officers were later found by soldiers and state police.
