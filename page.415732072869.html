One Direction's Zayn Malik quits band

1The news was announced on One Direction's official website, with a statement from the band that read in part, "We're really sad to see Zayn go, but we totally respect his decision and send him all our love for the future."
2Malik issued his own statement saying, "My life with One Direction has been more than I could ever have imagined. But, after five years, I feel like it is now the right time for me to leave the band. I'd like to apologise to the fans if I've let anyone down, but I have to do what feels right in my heart. I am leaving because I want to be a normal 22-year-old who is able to relax and have some private time out of the spotlight. I know I have four friends for life in Louis, Liam, Harry and Niall. I know they will continue to be the best band in the world."
3Malik, who is engaged to Little Mix star Perrie Edwards, quit the One Direction tour last week due to stress just days after pictures emerged showing him partying with a young woman in Thailand.
4In a show of support for Malik, his bandmates avoided introducing themselves as One Direction at a show in the Philippines last Saturday and simply used their first names to thank the crowd.
5Former American Idol judge Simon Cowell - who discovered the band - also weighed in on Malik's departure.
6"I would like to say thank you to Zayn for everything he has done for One Direction. Since I first met Zayn in 2010, I have grown very, very fond - and immensely proud - of him. I have seen him grow in confidence and I am truly sorry to see him leave. As for One Direction, fans can rest assured that Niall, Liam, Harry and Louis are hugely excited about the future of the band."
7One Direction's most recent album - FOUR - was released in 2014.
8The band play six Canadian concerts - including Toronto's Rogers Centre - this summer.
