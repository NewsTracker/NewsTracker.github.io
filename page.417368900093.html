1A man has been fined for using his Apple Watch while driving, in what is believed to be the first case of its kind since the smartwatch was released last month.
2Mr Macesin, who got his Apple Watch on April 24, the day it launched, was prosecuted under Section 439.1 of the Quebec Highway Safety Code, which states that "no person may, while driving a road vehicle, use a hand-held device that includes a telephone function".
