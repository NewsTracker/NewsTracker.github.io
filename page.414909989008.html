8The bidding process also came under fire and is being investigated by the FBI.
9Last year, an oversight committee that reviews the district's use of bond proceeds rejected the plan, saying the school district had not proved that it was needed.
10In a statement emailed to reporters on Friday, Cortines said the district "does not have sufficient funds to purchase and maintain technology" under the program.
11Cortines will urge the Board of Education to hold off purchasing any more of the equipment for the foreseeable future, a spokesman said.
