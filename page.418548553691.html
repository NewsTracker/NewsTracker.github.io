Apple Updates iPod Touch

1This composite product image provided by Apple shows varieties of the new iPod Touch.
2Apple Inc. AAPL 0.92 % introduced its updated iPod Touch that features the same chip used in the iPhone 6 and an 8-megapixel iSight camera.
3The iPod was a breakthrough portable music player when it was introduced in 2001, but it has been displaced by smartphones in recent years.
4Starting at $199, Apple said the iPod Touch has 10-times faster graphics performance and improved fitness tracking.
5The company said the upgraded iPod also offers three-times faster Wi-Fi connectivity.
6Apple's streaming-music service will be available through the Music app on a free-trial basis for three months.
7Afterward, customers who choose to keep the streaming-music service would be charged a $9.99 a month for a membership, with an additional option for a family plan that covers up to six family members for $14.99 a month.
8The new iPod lineup will be offered in a new array of colors, with the 16-gigabyte version priced at $199.
9At the higher end, Apple is offering for the first time a 128-gigabyte model for $399.
