CANNES WATCH: DiCaprio buys $13,600 bag for charity

1CANNES, France (AP) - Who knew Leonardo DiCaprio had an eye for Chanel bags?
2The actor plunked down 10,000 euros ($13,620 for a blue Chanel Neoprene flap bag, embroidered with flowers, on the Croisette on Monday night. But it was all for charity, as part of an auction at the annual Heart Fund Gala, also attended by Paris Hilton, actor Gary Dourdan and featuring a performance by Yasiin Bey (formerly known as Mos Def).
3DiCaprio outbid several suitors for the purse, but was himself outbid on two other items, including a vacation at one of the luxury resorts of Nikki Beach Worldwide.
4Bey performed a brief set, during which he shouted out to friend DiCaprio.
5The Heart Fund offers cardiovascular care across the globe for impoverished children.
6Heart surgeons have donated their services to perform costly operations.
7Last year, with the support of Nikki Beach owners Jack and Lucia Penrod, the organization was able to purchase a bus that would act as a mobile hospital to screen and treat children; it was unveiled at this year's gala.
8This year's goal is a plane outfitted with medical equipment to perform surgeries, and it was achieved as one benefactor donated 5 million euros ($6.8 million).
