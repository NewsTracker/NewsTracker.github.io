'Call of Duty: Black Ops 3' coming to PS3, Xbox 360

1"Call of Duty: Black Ops III."
2Activision has announced that the upcoming Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 will be available for Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3 and not only for Xbox One, PlayStation 4 and PC as previously announced.
3Campaign, multiplayer and zombies gameplay will be available to Xbox 360 and PS3 players, although they won't be able to take advantage of the four-player cooperative campaign, nor the next-gen multiplayer beta, being planned for the other consoles.
4Activision notes that no Nintendo Wii U version is planned.
5Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 launches on Nov. 6.
6More information about the game is coming during next week's E3.
