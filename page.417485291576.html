Platini Cancels UEFA Meeting on Anti-Blatter Tactics

1UEFA has canceled a meeting in Berlin this weekend where member federations were to discuss ways of opposing a Sepp Blatter-led FIFA.
2UEFA President Michel Platini says it is better to await developments after Blatter announced plans to resign.
3Platini also noted the "unpredictable nature" of a U.S. federal investigation into corruption in world soccer.
4The 54 UEFA member federations were expected to meet on the sidelines of this weekend's Champions League final.
5At least 10 UEFA members are thought to have voted for Blatter despite Platini urging the FIFA president to resign before the election last Friday.
