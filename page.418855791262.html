MH17 fallout: Russia considers ban on import of Dutch flowers

1Moscow: Russia says it is considering banning imports of cut flowers from The Netherlands, a move that would aggravate tensions between the two countries already at odds over the downing of flight MH17.
2National veterinary agency Rosselkhoznadzor said on Monday it has urged the European Union's organisation for plant protection to review certification for Dutch flower exports to Russia.
3Rosselkhoznadzor official Alexei Alexeenko denied earlier reports that a ban is already in place, state news agency RIA Novosti said.
4The reports said the agency is concerned about quarantined organisms.
5Russia has often been accused of using sanitary issues as a pretext for politically motivated bans.
6Russia opposes a Dutch-supported call for a criminal tribune on the 2014 downing of a Malaysia Airlines flight over eastern Ukraine territory controlled by Russia-backed rebels.
