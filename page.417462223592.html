Greece submits 'realistic' plan to creditors as it races to strike debt deal

1Greece says it has sent creditors a "comprehensive" and "realistic" package of reforms, as it races to reach a last minute debt deal.
2Prime Minister Alexis Tsipras is calling on Europe's leaders to accept the plan to end what he describes as "divisions."
3"We have made concessions, because a compromise demands concessions," said Tsipras.
4"We know these concessions will be difficult but we have submitted a realistic plan for Greece to exit the crisis."
5It is believed the plan was submitted in time for late night talks in Berlin - involving Angela Merkel, Francois Hollande and finance officials.
