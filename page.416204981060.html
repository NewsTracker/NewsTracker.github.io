Islamic State militants target Moroccan Embassy in Libya

1Security spokesman Essam al-Naas says the bomb went off early Monday.
2Morocco condemned the attack and called for an investigation.
3Libya's branch of the Islamic State group said in a statement on its Twitter account that "soldiers of the caliphate" targeted the building but gave no motive.
4It also posted several photographs of the embassy, including one of an empty street at night and another showing flames caused by the explosion.
5The same group claimed responsibility for an attack on the South Korean Embassy in Tripoli early on Sunday, which killed one guard and one passer-by.
