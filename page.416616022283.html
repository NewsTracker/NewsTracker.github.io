Aurora Shooting Massacre's Youngest Victim Described in Court

1A veteran police officer got choked up on the stand in Colorado today as he testified about the moment he carried a 6-year-old victim out of the Aurora movie theater after the July 2012 shooting.
2Sgt. Michael Hawkins was one of the 10 witnesses who testified in court today as part of the trial against shooter James Holmes, who faces the death penalty for killing 12, the youngest of whom was Veronica Moser-Sullivan.
3"At the point that I was running out with her, I became aware of her bleeding all over me ... and I looked down at her and I realized that she was probably gone," Hawkins said, pausing because he was overcome with emotion.
4The court also heard the 911 call made by Kaylin Bailey, who was 13 at the time of the shooting and had babysat Veronica earlier in the day.
5Bailey is heard crying as the operator tries to walk her through CPR, but she told the court that she wasn't able to get to Veronica because the girl's pregnant mother was covering the child.
6Veronica Moser-Sullivan died as a result of the shooting, and her mother Ashley Moser was paralyzed from the waist down and miscarried.
