Ukraine's justice ministry bars Communists from elections

1KIEV, Ukraine (AP) " Ukraine's justice ministry has barred three Communist parties from running in the upcoming local elections, citing recent legislation.
2Ukrainian news agencies on Friday quoted Justice Minister Pavlo Petrenko as saying that the three parties will be barred from the October elections.
3The minister also pledged to file a lawsuit to ban the three organizations.
4The Communist party has been an important force in Ukraine, polling 13 percent in the 2012 parliamentary election, but its popularity plummeted over its support for ex-President Viktor Yunukovych.
5In last year's parliamentary election the Communist Party of Ukraine garnered less than 4 percent of the vote.
6Ukraine passed several laws in April banning the use of symbols from the Soviet years and denouncing Communist ideology.
