Obama: Hillary Clinton would be an 'excellent president'

1PANAMA CITY (AP) - President Barack Obama says he thinks Hillary Rodham Clinton would be "an excellent president."
2Clinton is expected to launch her long-anticipated second run for president on Sunday with an online video.
3Obama says Clinton was a formidable candidate against him when they competed for the Democratic nomination in 2008.
4Says Obama: "I think she would be an excellent president."
5He adds that Clinton will have strong messages to deliver if she does decide to run.
