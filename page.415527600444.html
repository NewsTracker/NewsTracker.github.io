4A prosecution statement said some suspects confessed during the investigation, adding that only 12 out of the 16 defendants are in custody and belong to the Brotherhood and the Ultras.
5The suspects face several charges including murder, thuggery and vandalism.
6The prosecution had said 22 people died in the violence, but the health ministry said they miscounted and the correct toll was 19.
7Egypt's hard-core football fans have often clashed with police, including in political unrest that has seen two presidents toppled in four years.
8The government has launched a brutal crackdown against supporters of president Mohamed Morsi, who was ousted by the army in July 3, 2013.
9The crackdown has mainly targeted Morsi's Muslim Brotherhood, and hundreds of mostly Islamist protesters have been killed in clashes.
10The government suspended the premier football league again after the latest stadium deaths, but later said that the most watched league will return after a mourning period.
