Mexico recovers stolen radioactive material

1MEXICO CITY (AP) - Mexican authorities say they have recovered hazardous radioactive material stolen last week from the Gulf Coast state of Tabasco.
2The Interior Ministry says the material, Iridium 192, used for industrial radiography, was found sealed in its container on a pedestrian walkway in Cardenas, the same town where it was stolen.
3The ministry said in a statement Wednesday that the container was not "opened or manipulated" and posed no threat.
4Robbers last Thursday stole a truck containing the material, which authorities said could cause grave injury to anyone handling it even for a short time.
5It was the fifth theft of radioactive material in Mexico in little over a year.
6The hazardous material has been recovered in all cases.
7The theft last week prompted an alert in five states.
