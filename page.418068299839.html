Gunmen open fire at Sousse, Tunisia, hotel; 37 dead

1Officials said tourists were among those killed at the Imperial Marhaba hotel.
2At least one of the gunmen was also killed and a second was being pursued, the BBC reported.
3Sources told Al Jazeera the gunmen exchanged fire with security forces before one man fled.
4British tourist Gary Pine told Sky News he thought at first that "fire crackers" were going off at the hotel.
5"There was a mass exodus off the beach," he said.
6"My son was in the sea at the time and myself and my wife were shouting at him to get out and as he ran up he said 'I've just saw someone get shot.'"
7Hotel officials said 13 people were injured, including nine guests and four hotel employees.
8No group has claimed responsibility for the attack, though sources said the gunmen may have intentionally targeted tourists at the hotel and possibly two other hotels.
