1A U.S. Border Patrol agent who killed a teenager when he fired across the border from Texas into Mexico cannot be sued in U.S. courts by the Mexican teen's family, a federal appeals court ruled Friday.
2The unanimous ruling was issued by the full 5th U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals and reverses most of an earlier 2-1 ruling by a three-judge panel of the court.
