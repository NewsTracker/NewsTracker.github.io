Baltimore boy struck by police car in serious condition

1A Baltimore police cruiser slammed into a 12-year-old boy on Thursday, causing serious injuries, authorities said.
2The patrolman was headed to a call about 4:15 p.m. when the boy stepped in front of the car, police Lt. Sarah Connolly told the Daily News.
3The child was rushed to St. Mary's Hospital where he was in serious condition.
4Photos from WMAR show the cruiser surrounded by police tape in the 3800 block of Falls Rd.
