FBI chief: Islamic State group bigger threat than al-Qaida

1ASPEN, Colorado (AP) " The FBI director says the Islamic State group's effort to inspire troubled Americans to kill at home has become more of a terror threat to the U.S. than an external attack by al-Qaida.
2FBI Director James Comey told an audience at the Aspen Security Forum that the Islamic State group has seen success in recent months after a yearlong social media campaign to radicalize disaffected Americans and Europeans.
3He said the FBI had made a number of arrests over the last eight weeks of people who had been radicalized and has hundreds of investigations pending.
4Comey's remarks signal a deepening concern about the impact of the Islamic State's effort to inspire terrorist violence.
