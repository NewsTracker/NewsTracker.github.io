Jury to resume deliberations in Aaron Hernandez murder trial (Yahoo Sports)

1(AP) -- Twelve jurors working to decide whether to convict former New England Patriots player Aaron Hernandez in the death of another man are set to resume deliberations.
2The jurors spent nine hours deliberating on Tuesday and Wednesday.
3Hernandez is charged with the June 2013 shooting death of Odin Lloyd, who was dating his fiancee's sister.
4Lloyd was shot six times in an industrial park less than a mile from Hernandez's home.
5On Wednesday, jurors asked questions to clarify two weapon and ammunition possession charges Hernandez faces in addition to the murder charge.
6Jurors must deliberate every day from 9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., and may go later if they wish.
