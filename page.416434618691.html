Trader Joe's Sweet Apple Chicken Sausage recalled over fears product contains bits of plastic

1MONROVIA, Calif.
2- Trader Joe's customers who bought the chain's Sweet Apple Chicken Sausage are urged to throw it out amid concerns plastic may have made its way into the packages.
3The U.S. Department of Agriculture announced this week that Massachusetts-based sausage maker Kayem Foods is recalling more than 59,000 pounds of the product.
4The recall is for 8-ounce sausage packages sold at Trader Joe's stores nationwide and marked with expiration dates of April 22, 25 and 29.
5Customers can return the product for a full refund at any Trader Joe's.
