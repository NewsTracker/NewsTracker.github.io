Islamic State video shows killing of teen accused as Israeli spy

1BEIRUT (Reuters) - A video posted online by Islamic State militants on Tuesday showed a boy fatally shooting Muhammad Musallam, an Israeli Arab accused by the group of having signed up as a jihadi to spy for Israel's intelligence service.
2The video, published by the group's Furqan media outlet, showed Musallam, 19, sitting in a room wearing an orange jumpsuit, talking about how he had been recruited and trained by Mossad.
3He said his father and elder brother had encouraged him.
4After that, it showed Musallam being escorted to a field and then being shot in the head by a boy, described by an older, French-speaking fighter as one of the "cubs of the caliphate".
5Reuters could not immediately verify the authenticity of the footage, which also appeared on Twitter feeds used by Islamic State supporters.
6Israeli security officials said they were aware of the video but could not confirm that it was authentic.
7The video, about 13 minutes long, showed Musallam on his knees when the boy armed with a pistol and wearing a military uniform fires one bullet into Musallam's forehead.
8The boy then fired three more shots into his body.
9In the video, Musallam said he had joined Islamic State to report to the Israelis on weapons caches, bases and Palestinian recruits.
10Israel and his family denied that he was an Israeli spy.
11An Israeli security official had said Musallam went to Syria to fight for Islamic State in October last year.
12Musallam's father has denied his son was a spy, saying a month ago that his son had gone missing while on a tourist trip to Turkey.
13Said Musallam and the youth's mother, Um Ahmad, wept at their home in East Jerusalem when they learned of his death.
14Clutching her son's photograph, Um Ahmad told reporters: "A spy of 19? How is that possible? Why would he have gone there if he were a spy?"
15Said Musallam said his son had been trying to return home when he was captured.
16He accused the militants of forcing his son to say he was a spy before killing him just for show.
17"Mohammad was told by them to say about himself that he works for the Israelis," Said Musallam said.
18"They took him as a victim, only to show the world, so the world would be afraid of them."
