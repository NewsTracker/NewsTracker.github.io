Microsoft Confirms Acquisition of Wunderlist App

1Microsoft Corp. confirmed it bought the German startup behind a task-management mobile app called Wunderlist, as the software firm continues to acquire app developers to fill gaps in its consumer-product lineup.
2The Wall Street Journal earlier had reported Microsoft agreed to acquire the app maker, 6Wunderkinder GmbH, for between $100 million and $200 million.
3Microsoft announced the purchase Tuesday in a blog post.
