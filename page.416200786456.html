Fighting picks up in war-torn eastern Ukraine

1DONETSK, Ukraine (AP) - Observers says fighting has picked up in eastern Ukraine, after more than a month of relative calm.
2The military conflict between Russia-backed rebels and government forces has killed more than 6,000 but has largely subsided since the cease-fire was announced in February and at least some heavy weaponry withdrawn.
3Observers from the Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe said Sunday that they heard intense mortar fire outside the village of Shyrokyne for about three hours as well as machine gun engagement.
4Mortar fire was also heard at night and in the morning on Monday in central Donetsk.
5Heavy fighting was also reported at the destroyed Donetsk airport where journalists have not been allowed since Sunday because of the security situation.
