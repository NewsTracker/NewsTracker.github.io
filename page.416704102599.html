IS group claims Iraq car bomb attack that killed 19

1BAGHDAD (AP) " The extremist Islamic State group has claimed responsibility for a late-night car bomb attack in Baghdad that killed at least 19 people, saying it was targeting a Shiite militia.
2The two car bombs went off about 10 minutes apart late Saturday in the Karrada district, known for its restaurants, cafes and ice cream parlors.
3Police said the dead and wounded were mainly shoppers and people commemorating the death of Imam Ali, a key figure in Shiite Islam.
4Two traffic policemen were among those killed.
5Sunday's claim of responsibility by the IS group is similar to previous claims this past week, in which the militants characterized the attacks as a response to fighting in the western Anbar province.
6Baghdad has seen a spike in car bombs in the last week.
