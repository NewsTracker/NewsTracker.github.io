Apple to join Dow Jones stock index

1The switch will take place after the close of trading on Wednesday March 18, S&P Dow Jones Indices said.
2Apple will start trading as part of the 30-stock Dow at the opening of trading the next day.
3S&P Dow Jones Indices said it is making the move in response to a planned stock split for Visa, another member of the 119-year-old barometer of the stock market.
4After its four-to-one stock split, Visa will wind up with a lower price.
5S&P said that would reduce the weight of the information technology sector in the Dow.
6Adding Apple will help balance out this reduction.
7Unlike other stock market measures, the Dow weighs members by their prices, so a large change in the price of one stock can have a big effect on the overall index.
8S&P said swapping Apple for AT&T will not alter the Dow's level.
