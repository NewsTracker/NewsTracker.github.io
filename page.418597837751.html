US stocks mixed open mixed as investors assess earnings

1NEW YORK (AP) - U.S. stocks indexes were mixed in midday trading as investors assess the latest batch of earnings news.
2The Dow Jones industrial average was down 71 points, or 0.4 percent, at 18,048 as of 11:51 a.m. Eastern time Friday.
3The Standard & Poor's 500 index dropped one point, or 0.1 percent, to 2,123.
4The Nasdaq composite climbed 33 points, or 0.7 percent, to 5,197, putting the technology-heavy index at an all-time high.
5Google surged after reporting earnings that exceeded the expectations of Wall Street analysts.
6Bond prices didn't move much.
7The yield on the 10-year Treasury note held at 2.35 percent, unchanged from a day earlier.
