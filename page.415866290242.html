Canadian Parliament approves extension and expansion of mission against the Islamic State

1Prime Minister Stephen Harper announced the extension and expansion earlier this month and Parliament voted 142 to 129 on Monday night.
2Passage was all but assured as Harper's Conservative government has the majority of seats.
3Canada will be the first NATO country, other than the United States, to conduct airstrikes in Syria.
4Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, Jordan and the United Arab Emirates have also done so.
5Canadian airstrikes have been limited to IS targets in Iraq thus far.
6Canada also has 69 special forces soldiers training Kurdish peshmerga fighters in northern Iraq.
7The IS group controls a wide swath of territory across the Syrian and Iraqi border.
