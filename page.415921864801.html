Masked gunmen storm Kenya university

1Nairobi, Kenya: Gunmen stormed a Kenyan college campus in the north-eastern town of Garissa in the early hours of Thursday morning, setting off explosions and exchanging gunfire with security services for several hours, Kenyan media and the Red Cross said.
2Heavy gunfire and explosions were heard and the stand-off was ongoing at the Garissa University College, the Kenya's Daily Nation newspaper and Capital FM radio station said.
