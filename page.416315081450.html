Bryant 0 for 4 with 3 Ks in debut as Cubs lose to Padres 5-4 (Yahoo Sports)

1CHICAGO (AP) -- Kris Bryant had such a Chicago Cubs-like start to his major league career.
2Called up one day after the team ensured he can't be eligible for free agency until 2021, the prized prospect struck out in his first three at-bats against James Shields and went 0 for 4 in a 5-4 loss to San Diego Padres on Friday.
3Seeking their first World Series title in 107 years, the Cubs selected Bryant's contract from Triple-A Iowa and inserted him into their starting lineup as the cleanup hitter and third baseman.
4Bryant cleanly handled Derek Norris' sharp first-inning grounder, starting a 5-4-3 double play.
5Following a two-out walk to Anthony Rizzo in the bottom half, Bryant was given a standing ovation as many in the crowd took photos.
6With fans still on their feet, Bryant swung through three pitches for an inning-ending strikeout.
