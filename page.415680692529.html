Jordan says it will take part in training Syrian rebels to fight against Islamic State group

1The training is part of a regional effort also involving Turkey, Saudi Arabia and Qatar as hosts of training sites.
2The U.S. Congress has passed legislation providing $500 million for training about 5,000 rebels over the next year.
3Jordanian government spokesman Mohammed al-Momani said Monday that Jordan will train Syrians to "defeat the terrorism that has been killing their sons and daughters."
