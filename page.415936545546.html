Great-grandma featured in Facebook photo with newborn dies

1Sarah Hamm says her grandmother, Rosa Camfield, died in her sleep late Monday night.
2Hamm took a picture of her newborn daughter with Camfield on March 17 and posted it to her Facebook page.
3Hamm says Life of Dad, an entertainment website, put the photo on its own Facebook page.
4That post has since garnered more than 2.5 million "likes" and has been shared nearly 79,000 times.
5Hamm says Camfield would have been very happy but very shocked that one photo could create such a stir.
