CANNES WATCH: Andie MacDowell stuns in coral gown

1CANNES (AP) - Proving that age is no barrier to beauty, a radiant Andie MacDowell went for some old-fashioned va-va-voom in an Elie Saab gown in coral on the Cannes red carpet.
2The 57-year-old "Four Weddings and a Funeral" star wore a cinched column look for the Monday evening premiere of Pixar's latest offering, "Inside Out."
3The setting sun glistened warmly off the gown's sparkling embellishments, and a daring deep V-neckline oozed sensuality and confidence.
4MacDowell outshone many actresses half her age - quite the accomplishment, given the star wattage at this film festival on the French Riviera.
