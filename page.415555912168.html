4The shutdown is expected to take place March 27 through 29, according to Patrick Fatoma, another official with the Ebola response center.
5The government has done this before, and some experts said it was unexpectedly effective in providing information about to control the disease.

7There are currently about 30 cases in treatment centers across the country, Fatoma said, a far cry from the height of the epidemic when hundreds of people were falling sick each week in Sierra Leone.
