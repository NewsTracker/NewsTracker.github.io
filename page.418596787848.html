25165 -- The number of criminal counts of which Holmes was convicted.

27Holmes acknowledged that he planned and carried out the attack but pleaded not guilty by reason of insanity.
28256 -- The number of witnesses during the grueling trial, according to the Denver Post, which has kept a running tally.

3213 -- The number of psychiatrists and psychologists who testified, four on behalf of the prosecution and nine on behalf of the defense.
