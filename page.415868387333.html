DeRozan scores 42, Raptors beat Rockets 99-96 (Yahoo Sports)

1DeRozan scored a career-high 42 points and tied his career high with 11 rebounds as his Raptors beat Harden's Rockets 99-96 on Monday night, Toronto's eighth straight home victory over Houston.
2''It's definitely cool because we've been playing against each other since we were kids,'' DeRozan said of Harden, who also grew up in the Los Angeles area.
3''That's one of my close friends in the league to this day.
4''I'm close to his family, he's close to my family, so it's always cool,'' DeRozan added.
5DeRozan made a go-ahead turnaround bank shot with 1:27 left, the 11th lead change of the final quarter, then sealed it with a jumper over Harden with 18 seconds to go.
6He made 14 of 27 attempts from the floor and hit 12 of 17 at the free throw line.
7''The most important thing, not only the points, was his defense,'' Raptors coach Dwane Casey said of DeRozan, who guarded Harden down the stretch.
8Jonas Valanciunas scored 15 for Toronto, while Lou Williams had 13.
9Harden scored 31 for the Rockets, whose four-game winning streak was snapped.
10Houston has not won north of the border since a 114-100 victory on March 16, 2007.
11''We had our opportunities, we just didn't get the job done,'' Rockets coach Kevin McHale said.
12Trevor Ariza had 15 and Corey Brewer scored 13 for the Rockets.
13DeRozan and Harden, teammates on the USA squad that won gold at last summer's World Cup, first squared off when they were around 12 years old, DeRozan said.
14''He didn't have no beard back then,'' DeRozan joked.
15Houston's Dwight Howard was rested after finishing with 11 points and 10 rebounds in 19 minutes of Sunday's 99-91 win at Washington, his third game in five nights since returning from a right knee injury.
16''Dwight is going to have limited minutes for a while,'' McHale said before the game.
17The Raptors snapped a streak of 10 straight losses against opponents with winning records.
18They hadn't beaten a team above .500 since a 105-80 victory at Atlanta on Feb. 20.
19Ariza's 3-pointer gave Houston a 96-95 lead with 1:44 remaining before DeRozan answered with his basket.
20With a chance to put Toronto up three, DeRozan missed a pair of free throws with 45 seconds left.
21A tie up on the second miss led to a jump ball, which Terrence Ross won for the Raptors.
22After a pair of Toronto timeouts, DeRozan pulled up for his jumper over Harden, making it 99-96 with 18 seconds remaining.
23''He had it going,'' Harden said.
24Ariza got two chances to shoot for the tying basket in the final seconds, but missed both attempts.
25''They made plays to win and we didn't,'' Ariza said.
26Houston led 52-48 at halftime.
27DeRozan scored 13 in the third but Harden had 15, including the final five, as the Rockets took a 76-74 lead into the fourth.
28Rockets: Rookie C Clint Capela scored the first basket of his NBA career on an alley-oop from Harden in the first quarter.
29He finished with eight points on four eye-popping dunks.

31Beverley was injured in a March 23 win over Indiana.

33''He's having trouble cutting, going either way,'' Casey said.
34''He probably wouldn't go if (tonight) was a playoff game.'' There's no timetable for Lowry's return.
