FIFA postpones 2026 World Cup bidding amid turmoil

1SAMARA, RUSSIA - FIFA has suspended the 2026 World Cup bidding process amid a widening corruption scandal implicating previous bid contests.
2FIFA secretary general Jerome Valcke says it would be "nonsense" to begin the process now.
3FIFA planned to write to its 209 member federations this week to explain the bidding timetable and rules.
4The 2026 host is expected to be chosen by the 209 members at their May 2017 meeting in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
5Valcke spoke at a news conference with Russian organizers of the 2018 World Cup, and defended his role in alleged bribes paid by South Africa during the 2010 bidding contest.
