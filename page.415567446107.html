Obama, Merkel say no easing of Russia sanctions over Ukraine

1WASHINGTON: US President Barack Obama and German chancellor Angela Merkel spoke by telephone on Thursday on Ukraine, stressing they would keep up sanctions on Russia over its role in the crisis.
2"There will be no easing of sanctions imposed on Russia until it has fulfilled all of its Minsk commitments," the White House said, referring to a recent deal to defuse tensions in eastern Ukraine.
3The two leaders said the agreement - which sketched out a ceasefire, called for the withdrawal of weapons from the frontline and opened the door to political dialogue - was necessary for a "lasting and peaceful resolution to the conflict."
