Fraser-Holmes succumbs to swim pressure

1Gutted former world No.1 Thomas Fraser-Holmes admits he couldn't handle the expectation at the national swimming championships in Sydney after a breakthrough 2014.
2Fraser-Holmes has one last chance to impress in the 400m individual medley final on Friday night - the eight day titles' last evening of action.
3Fraser-Holmes on Friday clocked four minutes, 23.49 seconds to be fifth fastest for the final - well behind top qualifier Travis Mahoney (4:20.76).
4Gold Coast's Fraser-Holmes, 23, arrived at the nationals as one of the swimmers to watch.
5He was 200m freestyle world No.1 last year, claiming the Commonwealth Games and Pan Pacs titles.
6But alarm bells were ringing when Fraser-Holmes remarkably failed to earn a 200m world titles berth after finishing a distant third in the national final.
7He also came third in the 200m IM final.
8All Fraser-Holmes has to show for the titles to date is a 4x200m freestyle relay berth for July's world titles in Kazan, Russia.
9"Things didn't go how I wanted them on my first day and in swimming it's pretty easy for things to snowball," a shattered Fraser-Holmes said.
10"Maybe the pressure got to me, being No.1 last year and coming back here with that expectation.
11Fraser-Holmes was still getting his head around missing out on the 200m at the world titles where he was tipped to win gold based on 2014 form.
12Besides the 4x200m relay spot, selectors' discretionary powers may yet grant Fraser-Holmes a nod in the 200m IM and 400m IM events at Kazan.
13"I have to accept I am not swimming the 200m at worlds," he said.
14Meanwhile, Keryn McMaster (4:44.99) was fastest qualifier for Friday night's women's 400m IM final.
15Mack Horton is tipped to shatter Grant Hackett's 16-year-old 18 age group record in the 1500m freestyle final on Friday night.
16In the other finals on the last night are Cate Campbell (50m freestyle), Sally Hunter (50m breaststroke) and Ben Treffers (50m backstroke).
17Australia's 2015 world titles team - renamed the Dolphins - will be confirmed on Friday night.
