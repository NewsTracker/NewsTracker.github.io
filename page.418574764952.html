'Wire' cast members to reunite for Baltimore performance

1Cast members from HBO's "The Wire" are reuniting for a special performance to honor four of Baltimore's "unsung heroes."
2Rewired for Change, the nonprofit founded by actress Sonja Sohn, says the free event is set for Saturday afternoon at Lyric Opera House and will feature a special musical guest.
3The nonprofit will present the Wired Up!
4Awards and will host a fundraiser for its community grant program.
5Participating cast members are expected to include: Dominic West, Michael Kenneth Williams, Wendell Pierce, Seth Gilliam, Chad L. Coleman, Larry Gilliard, Jr, Andre Royo, Gbenga Akinnagbe, Jaime Hector, Tristan "Mack" Wilds and Felicia "Snoop" Pearson.
6The cast issued a statement saying Baltimore's uprising was caused by people in marginalized communities being ignored by the establishment.
