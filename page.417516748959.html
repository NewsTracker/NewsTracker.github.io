Regina woman, 18, accused of urging young boys to fight in Facebook video: Cops

1An 18-year-old Regina woman has been charged with assault after she was allegedly heard off-camera urging two boys, ages seven and nine, to fight in a clip posted on Facebook last month, police say.
2On May 9, police began investigating a video that showed the two boys fighting in an alley in Regina.
3Police say they identified the suspect who allegedly instigated the fight and the two boys involved.
4Daralee Klyne, 18, has been released with conditions and is scheduled to appear in provincial court July 22.
5Police said the boys were not seriously injured.
