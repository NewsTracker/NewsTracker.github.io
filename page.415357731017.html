US stocks sink as investors worry about higher rates

1NEW YORK (AP) " U.S. stock indexes fell sharply as fears of over possible interest rate increases by the Federal Reserve roiled financial markets around the globe.
2The Dow Jones industrial average dropped 332 points, or 1.9 percent, to 17,662 Tuesday.
3The Standard & Poor's 500 fell 35 points, or 1.7 percent, to 2,044.
4The Nasdaq composite lost 82 points, or 1.7 percent, to 4,859.
5The prospect of higher U.S. rates helped send the euro sharply lower against the dollar.
6The euro fell to its lowest level in 12 years.
7Barnes & Noble plunged 10 percent after reporting earnings that fell far short of analysts' forecasts.
8Oil fell $1.71 to $48.29 a barrel in New York.
