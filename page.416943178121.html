Man arrested after 'liking' mug shot on Facebook posts bond

3GREAT FALLS, Mont.
4(AP) - A Montana man who was arrested after "liking" his mug shot on a Crimestoppers Facebook page has posted bond in a felony forgery case.
5Levi Charles Reardon of Great Falls was arrested on April 24, three weeks after an acquaintance pointed out his picture on the Great Falls/Cascade County Crimestoppers Facebook page and Reardon liked it.
6Reardon told the Great Falls Tribune (http://gftrib.com/1L0leUL ) on Monday that he was surprised at the level of attention he received after liking the post listing him as one of the area's most wanted.
7Reardon pleaded not guilty on May 7.
8Public defender Alex Spayd said Reardon posted a $2,500 bond the next day.
9His trial is set for Aug. 31.
