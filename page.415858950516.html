Charging wild rhino runs riot in Nepalese city (+ video)

1A wild rhino charged into a city in southern Nepal, killing a woman, injuring several others and chasing other panicked residents through the main market and a hospital, an official said.
2Attempts to force the rhino out of Hetauda city by honking car horns and beating drums were unsuccessful, with the animal running from one area to another, government administrator Ram Prasad Thapaliya said.
3Thapaliya said the rhino fatally gored a 61-year-old woman on the outskirts of Hetauda, a city of about 135,000 people that borders a forested area.
4It then chased people around the main downtown market and the city bus station.
5An unknown number of people were injured, he said.
6"The whole town is terrorised by this animal. It even entered the local hospital, forcing terrified people to jump over the fences."
7Hetauda is about 80 kilometres south of Kathmandu, Nepal's capital.
