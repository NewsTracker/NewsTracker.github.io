2 Men Accused of Bomb Plot During Ferguson Protests

1A federal indictment released Thursday charges Olajuwon Davis and Brandon Orlando Baldwin with several counts, including conspiracy to use explosives and making false written statements while buying firearms.
2Both men are 22.
3Baldwin's attorney declined comment.
4Davis' attorney didn't immediately return messages seeking comment.
5The indictment says the men were arrested in November, about a week before prosecutors announced they wouldn't charge the white Ferguson police officer who last summer fatally shot 18-year-old Michael Brown, who was black and unarmed.
6The announcement, like Brown's death, sparked widespread protests.
7The alleged bombing plot resulted in no explosions.
8The indictment doesn't indicate any intended targets.
9A spokesman for the Justice Department declined comment.
