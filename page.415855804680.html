American who caught Ebola improves to fair condition

1(AP) - Officials say an American health care worker who contracted Ebola while volunteering in Sierra Leone has improved to fair condition at a federal hospital.
2The National Institutes of Health announced Monday that the patient had been upgraded from serious condition.
3He was in critical condition earlier.
4He is being treated at the NIH hospital in Bethesda, Maryland, where he arrived March 13.
5The patient's name and age have not been released.
6He was working with Partners in Health, a Boston-based nonprofit organization treating patients in Liberia and Sierra Leone since November.
7Health officials are monitoring about 40 other Americans who may have been in contact with the infected man.
8None tested positive as of late last week.
9The 21-day monitoring period for all of them ends this week.
