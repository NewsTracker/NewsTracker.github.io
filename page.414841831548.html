9Aroui said the attack must be in revenge for a series of police operations against jihadi "sleeper cells" that have resulted in the arrest of dozens of suspected militant supporters.
10In one sweep earlier this month, 32 suspects were arrested for planning attacks on the Interior Ministry and National Guard bases.
