New Mexico AG pursues case against major nursing home chain

1New Mexico's top prosecutor is following through with a lawsuit against one of the nation's largest nursing home chains over inadequate care.
2The state initially sued in December, targeting several nursing homes run by Preferred Care Partners Management Group.
3The company operates in at least 10 states.
4The suit claimed thin staffing made it impossible to address residents' needs.
5As a result, the state and federal government were improperly billed.
6The case was highlighted in a New York Times story that revealed a private law firm urged the previous attorney general to sue.
7Current Attorney General Hector Balderas says it was important to review the merits of the case to ensure it was in the best interest of New Mexicans.
8An amended complaint includes accounts of neglect and poor care from dozens of former employees and residents' families.
