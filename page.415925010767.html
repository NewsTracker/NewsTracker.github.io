Doctors in Nepal end strike, return to work

1KATHMANDU, Nepal (AP) - Thousands of doctors in Nepal have returned to work ending a strike after the government bowed to their demands.
2Government chief Secretary Leela Mani Paudyal said Thursday a written agreement was signed between him and Dr. Govinda K.C., who has been on a hunger strike for 11 days demanding reforms in medical education and services.
3Dr. Nirmal Rimal of the Nepal Medical Association says doctors are returning back to work Thursday.
