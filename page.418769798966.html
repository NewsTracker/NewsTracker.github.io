FIFA seeds European World Cup qualifying groups by ranking

1ST. PETERSBURG, Russia (AP) European teams in the World Cup draw will be seeded according to the latest world rankings.
2FIFA's decision Friday means Romania and Wales are among the nine top-seeded nations despite not playing at a World Cup since 1998 and 1958, respectively.
3Former World Cup winners Italy and France are in the pot of second-seeded teams who risk being drawn with either Germany or Spain, the past two winners.
4Only the nine group winners will automatically advance to the tournament in Russia.
5The eight best runners-up go to a playoff round to decide the final four qualifying slots.
6FIFA says Armenia and Azerbaijan will again be separated in Saturday's draw for political reasons.
