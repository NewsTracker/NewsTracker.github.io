Jordan hosts international competition of anti-terror squads

1AMMAN, Jordan (AP)?
2Jordan is hosting a competition of elite anti-terrorism squads from 18 countries, including fellow members of the military coalitions fighting rebels in Yemen and Islamic State extremists in Iraq and Syria.
3Spokesman Col.
4Khaled Abu Hamad says the 7th Annual Warrior Competition offers a chance for team leaders to "meet and to know each other, as the terrorists have become international."
5The competition opened Sunday with a drill by Jordanian special forces rescuing hostages from a plane and rappelling from a helicopter.
6Teams compete in 10 events.
7China won the 2014 competition.
8Among those participating are the U.S., Russia, China, Saudi Arabia and Afghanistan.
9Jordan is a member of a U.S.-led military coalition against Islamic State militants.
10It also supports a Saudi-led campaign against rebels in Yemen.
