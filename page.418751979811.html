Bill Clinton: Hillary once thought she was 'too aggressive'

1WASHINGTON - Hillary Clinton once thought she was "too aggressive" to be a politician, according to her husband.
2Early in their relationship, he encouraged her to move to a big city and run for office rather than marry him, former President Bill Clinton told Time Magazine.
3"I asked her to marry me three times before she said yes... and the first time I said, 'I want you to marry me but you shouldn't do it,'" he said before encouraging her to move to New York City or Chicago and run for office.
4Hillary Clinton's reply showed her lack of confidence.
5"Oh my God | I'll never run for office. I'm too aggressive, and nobody will ever vote for me," she said, according to her now-husband.
6The former President says he's keeping a low profile around Hillary's campaign these days.
7President George W. Bush, in the joint interview, said he encouraged his brother to run because the likelihood of Hillary as the nominee meant the problem of a dynastic candidate wasn't as problematic.
8"What difference does it make," he said at the time, "if the order is Bush/Clinton/Bush/Obama/Clinton or it is Bush/Clinton/Bush/Obama/Bush?"
