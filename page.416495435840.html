Hillary Clinton plans campaign trip to Nevada

1LAS VEGAS (AP) - Hillary Rodham Clinton is planning to visit Nevada on the third trip of her fledgling presidential campaign.
2A Clinton campaign official confirmed the former secretary of state will come to Nevada on May 5.
3The official said the events will be similar to the small-scale question-and-answer sessions Clinton held in her two other campaign trips.
4The official did not provide additional details about the events.
5Clinton has traveled to the early-voting states of Iowa and New Hampshire since officially launching her campaign earlier this month.
6Nevada is a swing state with caucuses scheduled early in the presidential nominating process.
7It is traditionally the fourth state to weigh in on nominations.
8Clinton is the favorite to win the Democratic presidential nomination.
