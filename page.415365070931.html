'Israeli spy' Musallam executed by child, according to Islamic State video

1Video posted online by Islamic State shows a boy killing an Israeli Arab accused by IS as working for Mossad.
2Beirut: A video posted online by militants of the so-called Islamic State on Tuesday purportedly shows a boy killing Muhammad Musallam, an Arab citizen of Israel accused by the group of having signed up as a jihadist to spy for Mossad, with a bullet to the head.
3The video, published by the group's Furqan media outlet on Tuesday, showed Musallam sitting in a room wearing an orange jumpsuit, talking about how he had been recruited and trained by the Israeli intelligence service.
4He said his father and elder brother had encouraged him.
5After that, it showed Musallam being escorted to a field and executed by a boy, a child described by an older, French-speaking fighter as one of the "cubs of the caliphate".
6The mother and father of Muhammad Musallam, an Israeli Arab held by Islamic State in Syria as an alleged spy, hold pictures of him in their Jerusalem home in February.
7The authenticity of the footage, which also appeared on Twitter feeds used by IS supporters, could not immediately be verified.
8Israeli security officials said they were aware of the video but could not confirm that it was authentic.
9The video, which was about 13 minutes long, showed Musallam on his knees as he listened to the older fighter pronouncing the verdict in French.
10Then the boy, wearing a military uniform and armed with a pistol, stands face to face with Musallam and fires one bullet into his forehead.
11Musallam slumps, then the child shoots him three more times and chants "Allahu Akbar!"
12(God is Greatest).
13An image from a video reportedly released by the Islamic State through Al-Furqan, one of the Jihadist platforms used by the militant organisation on the web, shows a youth identifying himself as 19-year-old Mohammed Said Ismail Musallam from Jerusalem.
14In the video, Musallam repeated what he said last month in an interview published by the group's English-language magazine Dabiq, in which he said he had joined IS to report to the Israelis on weapons caches, bases and Palestinian recruits.
15Israel and his family denied that he was an Israeli spy.
16"I tell my father and my son: Repent to God. I say to the spies who spy on Islamic State: You will not be successful, they will expose you," Musallam said in the latest video, in Arabic.
17Musallam's family have said he went missing while on a tourist trip to Turkey and an Israeli security official has said he went to Syria to fight for IS in October last year.
18The mother of Muhammad Musallam with her son's photo in February.
19The execution video comes amid reports that a 13-year-old French boy has become the youngest known foreigner to die fighting for IS.
20Abu Bakr al-Faransi ("Abu Bakr the French") was reportedly killed two months ago defending a frontier post against the Syrian army in the region of Homs, Radio France International said.
21His two brothers are also thought to have been killed in Syria.
22The boy, who was pictured on social media brandishing an AK-47 and holding up his index finger in an Islamist salute, was originally from the troubled Strasbourg district of Ampere, according to Europe 1.
23He arrived in Syria in August 2014 with his parents, his brother Nadir and sisters.
24The family had reportedly travelled there in a camper van to recover the body of Mohamed, Abu Bakr's other brother, who had left in March that year but was dead four months later.
25By October 2014, Nadir, aged in his 20s, had also died.
26The fate of the sisters and parents is unknown.
