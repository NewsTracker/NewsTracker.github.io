Arson Reportedly Suspected As Cause Of Fire At Houston Islamic Center

1As inspectors continue to probe the cause of a fire at an Islamic center in Houston, reports have emerged that an accelerant was used in the blaze.
2The fire in one of the buildings of the Quba Islamic Institute erupted on Friday at 5 a.m., according to KTRK.
3The Houston Fire Department told KTRK an accelerant was used, which usually means the fire was intentionally set.
4Ahsan Zahid, assistant Imam at the center, told the Houston Chronicle that investigators have two working theories for why and how the fire was started.
5It was either sparked by someone who wanted to damage the facility, or it was started by a homeless person who broke in to the building to escape the cold.
6Officials from the Council on American-Islamic Relations told KTRK the incident should be investigated as a hate crime.
7The destroyed building was used as a storage facility and no one was hurt in the blaze.
