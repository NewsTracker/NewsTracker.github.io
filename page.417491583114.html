Apple recalls Beats Pill speakers because they may overheat

1NEW YORK (AP) " Apple said it is recalling its Beats Pill XL speakers after a customer reported getting burned when it overheated.
2There were eight reports of the speaker's battery overheating, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission said Wednesday.
3One person's finger was burned and another reported damage to a desk.
4Apple said customers can apply for a $325 refund on its website.
5They can also elect to receive Apple Store credit for the same amount.
6Apple, based in Cupertino, California, said it will send customers boxes for free to return the speakers.
7About 222,000 of the speakers are in the U.S., and 11,000 are in Canada, the CPSC said.
8They were sold at Apple's stores and websites starting in January 2014.
9It was also sold at other retailers.
10Beats, which also makes headphones and has a streaming music service, was bought last year by Apple Inc. for $3 billion.
