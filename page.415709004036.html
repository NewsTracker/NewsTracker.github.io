Ex-cyclone to cause wild weather in NT

1More than 400 residents of the remote Goulburn Island, about 300km east of Darwin, will be returned home on Wednesday after assessment teams concluded there was minimal damage from the cyclone that menaced the region earlier this week.
2Early on Wednesday morning, the Bureau of Meteorology warned of damaging winds and heavy rain in the Arnhem and Daly districts as the ex-cyclone moved west.
3"The remnant tropical low is expected to continue to track towards the west and remains below tropical cyclone strength," BoM said in a statement.
4"Thunderstorms with peak gusts around 90 km/h and severe thunderstorms and heavy rain which may lead to flash flooding are forecast for the southern Arnhem forecast district and parts of the Daly forecast district."
5BoM said locations that may be affected include Dorisvale, Douglas River, Nauiyu, Barunga, Beswick, Bulman, Central Arnhem Plateau, Ngukurr, Numbulwar and Litchfield National Park.
