Wanted man 'likes' own mugshot on Facebook, gets arrested

1A Montana man was arrested after he "liked" his mugshot on Facebook.
2Levi Charles Reardon, 23, was one of 19 "most wanted" criminals posted April 3 on the Great Falls/Cascade County Facebook page.
3A day later, a screengrab posted to the page showed Reardon had "liked" his wanted poster.
4He was arrested without incident April 24.
5Reardon is suspected of stealing a wallet and personal cheques, according to the Great Falls Tribune.
6He allegedly cashed four of the missing cheques - one of them after police had been in contact with him about the theft.
7Police didn't say whether Reardon's location services were enabled or how they found him.
