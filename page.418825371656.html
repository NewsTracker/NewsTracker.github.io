Obama opens first visit to Ethiopia by US president; faces calls to address human rights

1ADDIS ABABA, Ethiopia - President Barack Obama is making the first-ever visit by an American president to Ethiopia.
2Obama arrived in the capital of Addis Ababa from Kenya, where he made a historic return to the country of his father's birth.
3He'll meet with Ethiopian leaders and speak at the African Union.
4The president has faced criticism for visiting Ethiopia from human rights groups that accuse the government of cracking down on dissent by arresting journalists, opposition party supporters and others.
5The White House says Obama will raise human rights while in Ethiopia.
6Officials have defended the decision to travel to there, noting U.S. counterterrorism cooperation with the East African nation.
7While in Ethiopia, Obama will also meet with regional leaders to discuss the crisis in South Sudan.
