Mexico to meet Costa Rica at Citrus Bowl in CONCACAF tuneup

1The June 27 match with Costa Rica at Orlando's Citrus Bowl Stadium is a partnership with the Mexican national team, Soccer United Marketing, Orlando City Soccer, Orlando Venues, Florida Citrus Sports and the Central Florida Sports Commission.
2The appearance is Mexico's first at the Citrus Bowl since the 1994 FIFA World Cup.
3It is all part of Mexico's 2015 U.S. Tour, which also includes a March 28 match with Ecuador in Los Angeles, March 31 match with Paraguay in Kansas City, Missouri, and July 1 match with Honduras in Houston.
