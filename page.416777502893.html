Prince to Play "Rally 4 Peace" Concert in Balitmore

1Music legend Prince announced Tuesday that he will be headlining a special "Rally 4 Peace" concert on Mother's Day in Baltimore, Maryland.
2Sunday's concert, which will reportedly feature a number of surprise special guest stars, is slated to be held at the Royal Farms Arena, according to the concert's promoter LiveNation.
3Prince's funk-rock band, 3RDEYEGIRL, will also be performing.
4Attendees will be encouraged to show symbolic solidarity by wearing grey as a tribute "to all those recently lost in the violence."
5"In a spirit of healing, the event is meant to be a catalyst for pause and reflection following the outpouring of violence that has gripped Baltimore and areas throughout the U.S.," the press release stated.
6The city of Baltimore has recently been rocked by violent protests and riots in the wake of the death of Freddie Gray, who died after suffering a spinal injury while in police custody.
7The six officer's involved in the arrest of Gray have since been charged with homicide, one with second-degree murder.
8Prince recently composed a yet-to-be-released tribute song to the people of Baltimore that, according to the singer's publicist, also addresses "the socio/political issues around the country in the wake of a slew of killings of young black men."
9Tickets go on sale at LiveNation.com Wednesday, May 6 at 5 p.m. A portion of the proceeds will benefit Baltimore-based youth charities, The Associated Press reported.
