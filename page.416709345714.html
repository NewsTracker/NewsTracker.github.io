Boehner: Clinton needs to bring Democrats along on trade

1House Speaker John Boehner says Hillary Rodham Clinton needs to back trade legislation that President Barack Obama wants - and help get Democrats on board.
2Boehner says Republican votes alone aren't enough to give Obama the negotiating authority to complete an Asia-Pacific trade deal.
3He tells NBC's "Meet the Press" that Obama "needs her help" and Clinton should be more involved.
4Boehner says Clinton "can't sit on the sidelines and let the president swing in the wind."
5Presidential candidate Clinton so far is noncommittal about a trade deal she promoted as Obama's secretary of state.
6And she says any deal must produce U.S. jobs, raise wages and protect American security.
7Liberals and labor unions worry that that the pact would cost American jobs and lower environmental standards.
