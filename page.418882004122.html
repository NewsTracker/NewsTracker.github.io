Egypt factory fire kills at least 25

1At least 25 people have died in a fire at a furniture factory north of the Egyptian capital Cairo, officials say.
2A further 22 people suffered injuries from burns and smoke inhalation from the blaze in the city of Obour, media reports said.
3The fire is thought to have started after a gas canister exploded while it was being transported in a lift.
4The factory had not obtained a government safety certificate, Egyptian state media reported.
5More than 20 fire trucks eventually brought the blaze under control.
6Such accidents are relatively common in Egypt.
7A separate fire at a food market in Alexandria, Egypt's second-largest city, has injured at least 11 people.
