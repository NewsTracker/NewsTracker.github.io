Islamic State beheads women civilians for first time in Syria

1Islamic State militants have beheaded two women in Syria, the first time the group has decapitated female civilians, the founder of a group monitoring the war says.
2The beheadings took place in the eastern Deir al-Zor province this week, the head of the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights, Rami Abdulrahman, said.
3One of the women was beheaded along with her husband in Deir al-Zor city.
4In al-Mayadeen city to the south-east, the group beheaded another woman and her husband.
5Islamic State has beheaded local and foreign men in Syria, including enemy combatants, aid workers and journalists as well as people it has deemed as violating its hardline interpretation of Islamic law.
