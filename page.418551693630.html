44Germany says an outright debt cut would be illegal under European law and argues that there's only limited room for maneuver on lesser forms of debt relief.

47The European Commission says there are "serious concerns" about the sustainability of Greece's debt load amid a worsening in its economy.
48The Commission says in a report that its main forecasts are for debt to reach 165 percent of GDP in 2020, 150 percent in 2022 and 111 percent in 2030.
