Canada sending military trainers to train Ukrainian troops, joining US and British efforts

1Prime Minister Stephen Harper and Defense Minister Jason Kenney announced the move Tuesday.
2The troops will join U.S. and British soldiers early this summer, and the mission will last until March 31, 2017.
3Harper says Canada's military contribution addresses a number of requests from the government of Ukraine.
4He said it is intended to help the country in its efforts to maintain sovereignty, security and stability in the face of Russian aggression.
