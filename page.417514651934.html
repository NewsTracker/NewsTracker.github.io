3The tech giant confirmed some models of the smartwatch will be offered at Apple retail stores "within two weeks," says Jeff Williams, Apple's senior vice president of operations, in a statement Thursday.

5"The response to Apple Watch has surpassed our expectations in every way, and we are thrilled to bring it to more customers around the world," said Williams.
6"We're also making great progress with the backlog of Apple Watch orders."
7When pre-orders for the Apple Watch launched April 10, the company revealed the device would initially be available only through Apple's website and select luxury boutiques.
