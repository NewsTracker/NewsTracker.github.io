Austrian court closes jurisdiction hearing on Facebook suit

1The Austria Press Agency says the court on Thursday ordered Facebook to give representatives of the plaintiffs translations of documents before the court within three weeks and for the plaintiffs' lawyers to respond within an additional three weeks.
2The suit is being brought by Austrian law student Max Schrems and 25,000 Facebook users.
3They claim Facebook is illegally tracking their data, including through alleged involvement in the PRISM surveillance program of the U.S. National Security Agency.
4The plaintiffs are each claiming 500 euros (about $540) in damages.
5Facebook has said the suit is inadmissible under Austrian law.
