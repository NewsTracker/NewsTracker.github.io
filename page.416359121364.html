Button out of Bahrain F1 Grand Prix

1McLaren's Jenson Button pulled out of the Bahrain Formula One Grand Prix shortly before the start on Sunday following a technical problem.
2Button had been due to line up 20th after suffering technical problems on Saturday in qualifying.
3According to the Formula One official website, the McLaren team made numerous changes only for the data to flag a potential problem with the energy recovery system.
4"Jenson's issue is very sad for the team - but especially his crew who have worked incredibly hard on a weekend beset by problems," McLaren tweeted.
5McLaren started with Felipe Alonso 14th on the grid.
