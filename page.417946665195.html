Egypt Names Ambassador to Israel After Nearly 3 Years

1Egypt has named a new ambassador to Israel nearly three years after it had recalled the last one at the outset of the 2012 Israeli offensive in Gaza.
2A statement released Sunday said President Abdel-Fattah el-Sissi named ambassador Hazem Khairat as part of a regular reshuffle of Egypt's diplomatic corps.
3Khairat replaced Atef Salem Al-Ahl, who was in his post for less than a month in late 2012.
4Islamist President Mohammed Morsi recalled Al-Ahl to protest Israel's offensive.
5But Morsi played a key role with the United States in negotiating a truce, making him the first Muslim Brotherhood leader to mediate between Israel and Gaza's Islamist rulers.
6El-Sissi has kept good ties with Israel since he came to office after ousting Morsi.
7Relations have been strained with Gaza's rulers.
