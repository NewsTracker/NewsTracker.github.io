Man injured after confrontation with Baltimore police

1BALTIMORE (AP) - Baltimore police say a man is in critical condition at a hospital after a confrontation with officers.
2Deputy Police Commissioner Jerry Rodriguez said Monday that a man was stopped at 9 a.m. on Sunday by four officers on bike patrol.
3Rodriguez said the man tried to run from the officers but was detained and arrested.
4Shortly thereafter, the man was taken to the hospital by ambulance.
5Rodriguez said the situation is under investigation and the department is working in conjunction with the Baltimore City State's Attorney's Office.
6The four officers involved have been placed on routine administrative assignment.
7Rodriguez would not elaborate on the nature of the man's injuries, but he did note that "at the end that encounter" with the officers "the individual was suffering from a medical injury."
