Prosecutor: Banned FIFA official in US to face charges

1Prosecutors have confirmed that a top former FIFA official has arrived in the United States to face racketeering and bribery charges.
2Assistant U.S. Attorney Evan Norris told a Brooklyn judge Friday that Jeffrey Webb had been extradited from Switzerland.
3The prosecutor says no court date has been set for Webb.
4The 50-year-old Webb was among seven FIFA officials detained in Switzerland in a case that sent shock waves through the soccer world.
5The rest are fighting extradition.
6There was no immediate response Friday to messages left for Webb's lawyer.
7Since the indictment, Webb was banned provisionally and replaced as FIFA vice president from the North and Central American and Caribbean region.
8Webb also was president of the regional governing body CONCACAF.
9He is from the Cayman Islands.
