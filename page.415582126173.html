2A judge told jurors to return to court Friday morning so prosecutors and defense lawyers could sum up the case of 49-year-old Joyce Hardin Garrard.
3Garrard was the last witness, denying she meant to harm 9-year-old Savannah Hardin.

1The World Cup in Qatar keeps provoking outrage and breaking taboos.
2Seven years before the 2022 tournament kicks off in the wealthy emirate, FIFA recommended a break with soccer tradition on Tuesday - moving its marquee tournament to November and December instead of the usual June-July time slot.
3ZURICH (AP) -- The final match of the 2022 World Cup in Qatar will be played on Dec. 18, the country's national holiday.
4FIFA spokesman Walter De Gregorio confirmed the date Thursday, but did not say when the tournament would start.
5The governing body's executive committee prefers a 28-day tournament - starting Nov. 21 - but did not make a decision in the opening session of its two-day meeting.
6De Gregorio said the executive committee could confirm the kickoff date on Friday.
7''It is a major step because finally we know and we can move forward,'' De Gregorio said in a hastily arranged statement at FIFA headquarters.
8The November-December option was expected after being recommended by a FIFA-appointed task force which met last month in Doha, Qatar.
9The task force sought to take FIFA's lucrative event out of the Qatari summer heat in the June-July period when World Cups are traditionally played.
10FIFA President Sepp Blatter then said he preferred a Sunday, Dec. 18, final.
11Every World Cup final since 1966 has been played on a Sunday.
12By rejecting UEFA's preferred Dec. 23 final, a Friday, the FIFA executive committee helped protect the English Premier League's traditional Dec. 26 program.
13A 28-day World Cup, four days fewer than usual, would cut the time clubs must release players to national teams.
14European clubs and leagues, which must shut down for several weeks in the middle of their seasons, have been the strongest opponents of an end-of-year tournament.
15Qatar bid to stage the tournament in June-July when it was chosen as host in December 2010.
