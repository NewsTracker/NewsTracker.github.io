The (former) Fab Five: A look at One Direction as they were

1The British singer announced Wednesday he was quitting the band "to be a normal 22-year-old."
2His bandmates Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Louis Tomlinson and Liam Payne - currently in the middle of their "On The Road Again" world tour - offered their best wishes but said they'd continue as a quartet.
3One Direction formed in 2010 after five teenagers - Harry Styles, Liam Payne, Niall Horan, Tomlinson and Malik - auditioned individually for the British TV talent show "The X Factor."
