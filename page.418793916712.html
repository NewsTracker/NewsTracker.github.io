Barack Obama in Kenya: 'Africa is on the move'

1President Barack Obama heralded Africa as a continent "on the move" Saturday as he opened a US-sponsored business summit in Kenya, the East African nation where he has deep family ties.
2"Africa is one of the fastest growing regions of the world," Obama said.
3"People are being lifted out of poverty."
4Obama's visit to Kenya - the first by a sitting US president - has been highly anticipated in a nation that views him as a local son.
5The president's late father was born in Kenya and many family members still live here, including his elderly step-grandmother.
6"This is personal for me," Obama said.
7"There's a reason why my name is Barack Hussein Obama."
