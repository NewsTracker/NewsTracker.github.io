France touts Cannes prizes as vindication for film subsidies

1PARIS (AP) - France's president says his country's strong showing at this year's Cannes film festival is no accident - suggesting it's partly thanks to government subsidies.
2France has had long dry spells without prizes at Cannes.
3This year, unusually, five French films were selected for competition.
4The jury, led by American directors Joel and Ethan Coen, handed three prizes to French winners, including the top prize for Jacques Audiard's migrant drama "Dheepan."
