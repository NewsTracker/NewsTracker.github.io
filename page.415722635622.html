Defense seeks mercy for grandma convicted in running death

1(AP) - Defense lawyers are asking a jury to spare the life of an Alabama woman convicted of running her granddaughter to death.
2Jurors will be back in court in Gadsden on Wednesday for a sentencing hearing for 49-year-old Joyce Hardin Garrard.
3The jury convicted Garrard of capital murder last week in the death three years ago of 9-year-old Savannah Hardin.
4The girl's mother has joined with prosecutors in seeking the death penalty for Garrard.
5But the defense is trying to convince jurors to recommend life without parole for the woman.
6Garrard's lawyers can introduce evidence showing she doesn't have a criminal record.
7They could also ask relatives to testify on Garrard's behalf, and the defendant could take the stand again.
