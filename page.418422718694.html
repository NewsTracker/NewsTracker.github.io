Colima Volcano blasts ash, lava in western Mexico

1MEXICO CITY (AP) " A volcano in western Mexico has erupted, spewing ash more than 4 miles (7 kilometers) into the air and sending lava down its flanks.
2The activity at the Colima Volcano began Thursday and continued Friday morning.
3The volcano is also known as the Volcano of Fire.
4Luis Felipe Puente is director of Mexico's civil protection agency.
5He tweeted Friday that preventive protocols were activated.
6A statement from Colima state's civil protection agency on Thursday said the initial eruption occurred just after 11 a.m. Ash was falling to the southwest of the crater.
