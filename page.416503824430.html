TV: 2 dead, 100 injured in Bangladesh from Nepal quake

1DHAKA, Bangladesh (AP) - A TV report says that two people were killed and more than 100 injured in Bangladesh in an earthquake that originated in Nepal.
2Somoy TV station reported that one man was killed in the capital Dhaka and another in the northwestern district of Pabna on Saturday when the magnitude-7.9 quake hit Nepal.
3Its tremors were felt as far away as India, Tibet, Pakistan and Bangladesh.
4Somoy said the injuries occurred as people rushed out of homes and shopping malls.
5It did not give details of the two deaths.
6Local TV stations and fire services control room aid several buildings were tilted in Dhaka and other towns and cities.
