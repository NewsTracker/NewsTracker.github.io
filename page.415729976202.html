US has discussed possible Tikrit airstrikes with Iraq

1The talks raise the highly sensitive issue of U.S. air support for an offensive spearheaded by Iranian-backed Iraqi Shiite militias.
2The Pentagon on Tuesday said the Iraqi offensive had "stalled."
3Two U.S. officials confirmed the talks about possible U.S. airstrikes.
4They were not authorized to discuss the matter publicly on the record and so were granted anonymity.
5On Tuesday, other officials said that at Baghdad's request the U.S.-led coalition had begun aerial surveillance over Tikrit and is sharing the collected intelligence with the Iraqi government.
