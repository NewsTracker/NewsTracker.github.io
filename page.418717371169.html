Greece: Parliament to vote on more bailout conditions

1ATHENS, Greece (AP) " Greece's parliament has begun an emergency debate on a second round of conditions demanded by international creditors for a new bailout " a vote that could threaten the coalition government.
2Lawmakers late Wednesday will vote on judicial and banking reforms set as a requirement by other Eurozone countries for a third Greek rescue package worth 85 billion euros ($93 billion).
3Prime Minister Alexis Tsipras is already relying on support from pro-European opposition parties to gain parliamentary approval for bailout conditions, amid growing dissent within his left-wing Syriza party.
4Tsipras has imposed a tough new round of austerity measures this month and needs to conclude the bailout negotiations before Aug. 20, when Greece must repay loans worth more than 3 billion euros ($3.3 billion) to the European Central Bank.
