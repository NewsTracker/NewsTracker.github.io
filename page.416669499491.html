New wife arrives for Bali Nine funeral

1Andrew Chan's new wife has arrived in Sydney with Myuran Sukamaran's family, ahead of the funerals for the Bali Nine men.
2Febyanti Herewila, known as Feby, touched down on Saturday morning, after her husband of two days was executed in Indonesia on Wednesday.
3Less than a week after she wed her love in his last hours on death row, Ms Herewila has to face planning his funeral.
4She was escorted by government officials through a private exit at Sydney airport, along with Sukumaran's mother Raji, brother Chintu and sister Brintha.
5Looking tired, with hands on their bowed heads, the families made no comment leaving the airport before getting into three white cars that had been waiting for them.
6Chan, 31, and Sukumaran, 34, were shot by a firing squad with six other drug offenders at 12.25am (0325 AEST) on Wednesday, 10 years after their part in the Bali Nine heroin-smuggling plot.
7A container believed to be carrying their bodies was escorted from the tarmac at Sydney airport about 6.30am on Saturday.
8Chan's brother Michael and mother Helen arrived back in Sydney ahead of the bodies on Friday.
