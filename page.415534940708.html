Three stabbed at Baltimore's Morgan State University: Report

1Three football players at Baltimore's Morgan State University were stabbed on campus on Tuesday and one was seriously injured, the Baltimore Sun reported.
2The incident happened near a football field and a campus apartment building.
3A police spokesman said a man was stabbed in the chest and taken to a hospital in very serious condition, and two other people were also injured, the newspaper said on its website.
4Police Commissioner Anthony Batts said the victims were football players.
5He said a fourth person was involved and could be a suspect or person of interest, the newspaper said.
6Spokesmen for the school and police were not immediately available to comment.
7The stabbing was the second such incident on the school's campus in five days.
8Police also were called when fights broke out at an on-campus party on Saturday, the Sun reported.
