Gravestone of Hillary Clinton's dad toppled in Pennsylvania, maybe by vandals, police say

1- The gravestone of Hillary Rodham Clinton's father has been found toppled over at a Pennsylvania cemetery.
2Police say vandals may be responsible for upending the gravestone of Hugh Rodham in the Washburn Street Cemetery in Scranton.
3Police Chief Carl Graziano tells The Times-Tribune (http://bit.ly/1Oc7yGg ) someone at the cemetery reported the stone was tipped over Tuesday, two days after Clinton announced she's running for president.
4The chief says it's unknown how it tipped over but it may have been vandals.
5He says he's "not sure how else it would have fallen over."
6Police say they receive occasional reports of cemetery vandalism.
7Rodham grew up in Scranton.
8He owned a small textiles business in Chicago.
9He died in Little Rock, Arkansas, in 1993 at age 82.
