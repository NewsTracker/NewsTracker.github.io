Student who grew up in Baltimore area wins $62,900 literary prize

1BALTIMORE - A senior at Washington College has been awarded a $62,900 literary prize.
2Alexander Vidiani, who grew up in Hunt Valley, a Baltimore suburb, was named the Sophie Kerr winner Friday.
3It is the nation's largest literary prize for undergraduates.
4Vidiani submitted a portfolio with poems about loss, masculinity, fatherhood and other topics.
5Washington College professors reviewed 25 writing portfolios for the prize.
6Vidiani said in a phone interview that he is elated and honored to have been awarded the prize.
7In the fall, Vidiani will start the master's program in poetry at the University of Maryland.
8He says he hopes to eventually earn a Ph.D. and return to Washington College to teach.
9The award recognizes a graduating senior at the school who shows literary ability and promise.
