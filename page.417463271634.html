Helicopter crashes in Nepal mountains, killing 4 on board

1KATHMANDU, Nepal (AP) - A police official says a helicopter crashed in the mountains in northeast Nepal and four people on board were killed.
2Nepal Police spokesman Kamal Singh Bam says the helicopter was returning to Kathmandu when it crashed on the Yamuna Danda hill near Balefi village.
3He said bodies of three men and a woman have been recovered from the crash site and police rescuers are trying to return the bodies to Kathmandu.
