Trade body rules against US on 'dolphin-safe' tuna

1The U.S. revised rules in 2013 following a previous WTO ruling but Mexico returned to the Geneva-based trade body, arguing they still treat Mexican tuna less favorably than fish from elsewhere.
2A panel found Tuesday that tougher tracking and verification requirements are applied to the Eastern Tropical Pacific, where Mexican boats operate, than elsewhere.

4Schools of tuna tend to gather and swim around some species of dolphins.
