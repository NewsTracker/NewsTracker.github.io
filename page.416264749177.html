Ousted Ukraine president's ally found shot dead in Kiev home

1A former ally of deposed Ukrainian president Viktor Yanukovych has been found shot to death in his home in Kiev.
2The Ukrainian interior ministry said Oleg Kalashnikov, a former member of the Ukrainian parliament, was found dead Wednesday evening.
3It said he had died of gunshot wounds but did not say whether it was believed to be homicide or suicide.
4Kalashnikov was a deputy for the Party of Regions in Ukraine's previous parliament.
5At least six former Ukrainian government officials from Yanukovych's time have died over the past two months in various circumstances that police have said were most likely suicides.
6Yanukovych fled in February 2014 after months of increasingly violent protests.
7He later resurfaced in Russia.
