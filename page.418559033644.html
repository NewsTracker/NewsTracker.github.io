Mexico searches nationwide for escaped drug lord

1MEXICO CITY (AP) " Mexico's interior ministry says it has distributed 100,000 photographs of drug lord Joaquin "El Chapo" Guzman to highway toll booths around the country since his weekend escape from a maximum security prison.
2Hospitals, hotels and funeral homes are being checked.
3Forty-eight canine teams are searching passenger and cargo vehicles.
4Airports are on alert, closely reviewing passengers on private planes.
5Guzman is once again Mexico's most wanted criminal since he escaped late Saturday through a mile-long tunnel dug to come up under the shower of his prison cell.
