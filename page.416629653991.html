France sells 24 Rafale fighter jets to Qatar

1PARIS (AP) - France has announced a 6.3-billion-euro ($7 billion) deal to sell 24 Rafale fighter jets to Qatar.
2The deal is to be signed next Monday in Qatar's capital, Doha, in the presence of the French president, Francois Hollande, presidency services announced Thursday.
3With this sale, French arms exports already exceed 15 billion euros ($16.8 billion) this year, a defense official said on condition of anonymity because he was not authorized to speak publicly on the matter.
4Qatar becomes the third buyer of France's delta-winged Rafale, manufactured by Dassault Aviation.
5India has announced plans to buy 36 Rafale earlier this month and Egypt bought 24 Rafale in February.
