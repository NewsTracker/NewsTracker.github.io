Massachusetts police reach out to drug dealers on Facebook

1Authorities in a Massachusetts town are reaching out to drug dealers who want to eliminate their competition.
2The Charlton Police Department made a tongue-in-cheek post on their Facebook page Friday offering drug dealers the chance to provide information on their competitors.
3It asks dealers to complete a fill-in-the-blank form that gives them the person's name, address, phone number, what drugs they're selling and where they are being sold.
4Officer Richard McGrath says the post was meant to be entertaining, but says his officers would pursue any leads if a form was filled out.
5McGrath told the Telegram & Gazette he found the form online and tailored it for Charlton police use.
6The department so far has not received a filled-out questionnaire.
