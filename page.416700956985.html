Gray 'victory rally' in Baltimore

1Baltimore's mayor has lifted a citywide curfew six days after riots were sparked by the death of a black man who suffered a severe spinal injury while in police custody.
2Protests since Monday's riots have been peaceful, and the announcement of charges against six officers involved in Freddie Gray's arrest eased tensions.
3Mayor Stephanie Rawlings-Blake said today that her goal was not to maintain the curfew any longer than was necessary.
4The six officers face charges ranging from manslaughter to second-degree murder.
5Baltimore's unrest came amid a fierce national debate over police treatment of minorities and follows the deaths of unarmed African-Americans in New York, South Carolina and Ferguson, Missouri.
