11Silicon Valley rival Google Inc. said shortly afterward that it also planned to increase the use of stronger encryption tools.

13President Barack Obama and British Prime Minister David Cameron have expressed concern that turning such privacy-enhancing tools into mass market features could prevent governments from tracking militants planning attacks.

15An Apple spokesman pointed to public statements by Chief Executive Tim Cook on privacy, but declined to comment further.
