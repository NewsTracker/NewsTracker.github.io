Islamic State militants target Moroccan Embassy in Libya

1TRIPOLI, Libya (AP) - A bomb placed in a garbage bin targeted the Moroccan Embassy in the Libyan capital, Tripoli, early Monday without killing or wounding anyone, in an attack claimed by a local Islamic State affiliate.
2Security spokesman Essam al-Naas confirmed the details of the attack.
3Morocco condemned the attack and called for an investigation.
4Libya's branch of the Islamic State group said in a statement on its Twitter account that "soldiers of the caliphate" targeted the building but gave no motive.
