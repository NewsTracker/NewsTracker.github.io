Boko Haram video allegedly shows militants killing soldiers

1MAIDUGURI, Nigeria (AP) - A new Boko Haram video allegedly shows a militant shooting wounded soldiers in the head, and charred parts claimed to be the remnants of a downed Nigerian jet fighter.
2The first video released since the group pledged allegiance to the Islamic State group has no sign of Boko Haram leader Abubakar Shekau.
3There was no explanation for his absence.
4Shekau has been the mainstay of all previous videos.
5An armed fighter with his face covered by a scarf claims Boko Haram still holds several northeastern towns.
6It says thousands of fighters remain in a northeastern forest stronghold.
7The video gloats over what it claims is the disunity in the multinational force fighting to bring it down.
8The home-grown Nigerian extremists swore allegiance to the Islamic State group in March.
