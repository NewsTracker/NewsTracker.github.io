Mexico's Pemex, US investment firm First Reserve announce $1 billion energy investment deal

1Petroleos Mexicanos says in a statement that the agreement covers potential projects including infrastructure, sea transport, cogeneration and processing.
2First Reserve is a global private equity and energy infrastructure investment firm headquartered in Greenwich, Connecticut.
3It said in a statement Tuesday that the two companies will invest in infrastructure projects throughout Mexico.
4Last month, Pemex announced a $900 million deal with First Reserve and BlackRock in return for a 45 percent stake in a pipeline that will transport natural gas from Texas to Mexico.
5Mexico passed a landmark energy reform last year opening up the sector to private investment for the first time in decades.
