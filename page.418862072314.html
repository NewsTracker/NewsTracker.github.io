Cardinals name woman to training camp coach position (Yahoo Sports)

1Arizona Cardinals head coach Bruce Arians talks to the media after an NFL football organized team activity Monday, June 1, 2015, in Tempe, Ariz.
2TEMPE, Ariz.
3(AP) -- The Arizona Cardinals have hired Jen Welter to coach inside linebackers through their upcoming training camp and preseason.
4The Cardinals say Welter is believed to be the first woman to hold a coaching position of any kind in the NFL.
5Welter played running back and special teams in 2014 for the Texas Revolution of the Indoor Football League, becoming the first woman to hold a non-kicking position for a men's professional sports league.
6''I am honored to be a part of this amazing team,'' Welter said on Twitter on Monday night.
7Welter coached linebackers and special teams for the Revolution last season, becoming the first woman to coach in a men's pro football league.
8Her general manager with the Revolution was 2015 NFL Hall of Fame inductee Tim Brown.
9Welter played linebacker for more than 14 seasons in the Women's Football Alliance, mostly with the Dallas Diamonds, where she helped the team win four championships.
10Welter holds a master's degree in sports psychology.
11A rugby player at Boston College, she also earned two gold medals on Team USA at the International Federation of Football Women's World Championships in 2009 and 2013.
12A news conference was scheduled for Tuesday to introduce Welter and Levon Kirkland, a former Pro Bowl linebacker who is the inaugural participant in the Bill Bidwill Coaching Fellowship established to give recently retired NFL players a chance to coach in the league.
13He will work with outside linebackers for the next two seasons.
14Four months ago at the NFL meetings, Arizona coach Bruce Arians was asked about the possibility of a woman coaching in the NFL.
15''The minute they can prove they can make a player better, they'll be hired,'' Arians said.
16Speaking to azcardinals.com on Monday, Arians said: ''Coaching is nothing more than teaching.
17One thing I have learned from players is 'How are you going to make me better?
18''I really believe she'll have a great opportunity with this internship through training camp to open some doors for her,'' Arians said.
19It's the second such barrier to be broken in the NFL this year.
20The league announced in April that Sarah Thomas would be the first woman to be a full-time NFL official.
21In the NBA, Becky Hammon is an assistant coach with San Antonio and served as the head coach for the Spurs' team that won the Las Vegas Summer League championship.
