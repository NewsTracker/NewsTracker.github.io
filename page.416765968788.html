Pemex: 2 workers dead in oil rig accident in Gulf of Mexico

1MEXICO CITY (AP) - An accident on an oil maintenance platform Tuesday killed two workers and injured another in the Gulf of Mexico, state oil company Petroleos Mexicanos reported.
2The company, commonly known as Pemex, said the Troll Solution rig was maneuvering to perform offshore well maintenance at the time of the accident.
3Pemex blamed a structural failure in one of the platform's legs.
4The rig was "listing" but had not sunk, the company said via Twitter.
5Pemex said in a statement that the platform belonged to oil services provider Typhoon Offshore.
6Ninety-eight of the company's workers and three Pemex employees were removed from the rig and taken for medical evaluation.
7Pemex said production was not affected.
