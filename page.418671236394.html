Fifa presidential election planned for February

1The Fifa presidential election to choose Sepp Blatter's successor will be held on February 26 next year.
2The decision was taken by Fifa's executive committee today.
3The decision gives Mr Blatter seven more months in power before leaving the scandal-tainted governing body.
4Fifa is reeling under the weight of American and Swiss criminal investigations into corruption, which led to 14 officials being indicted in May when Mr Blatter won a fifth term.
5Fifa's 209 members will return to Zurich next year to select a new president almost than nine months after Mr Blatter's resignation statement.
