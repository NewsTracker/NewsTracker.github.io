Documentary explores decline of Atari, dumping of flopped "E.T." game in New Mexico desert

1"Atari: Game Over" details the downfall of video-game giant Atari.
2The documentary began airing on Xbox last year and was released on Netflix on Wednesday.
3The documentary investigates claims that Atari hid its biggest flop, 1982's "E.T.," by burying the cartridges in an Alamogordo, New Mexico, dump.
4The "E.T." game had the reputation of being the worst game ever made and contributed to the company's demise.
5Around 300 and 400 games uncovered in the New Mexico landfill last year were later sold on eBay.
