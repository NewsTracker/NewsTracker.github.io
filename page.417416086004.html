Enrique Iglesias recovering after fingers sliced at concert

1NEW YORK (AP) " Enrique Iglesias was recovering Sunday after some of his fingers were sliced when he grabbed a drone during a concert in Tijuana, Mexico.
2A representative for the singer said in a statement to The Associated Press that Iglesias was "semi-treated" after the accident at the side of the stage to stop the bleeding on Saturday night.
3He was advised to end the show but went on to perform for an additional 30 minutes.
4"During the show a drone is used to get crowd shots and some nights Enrique grabs the drone to give the audience a Point of View shot. Something went wrong and he had an accident," the statement read.
5"He decided to go on and continued playing for 30 minutes while the bleeding continued throughout the show."
6Concertgoers at Plaza de Toros de Playas posted photos on social media of Iglesias, 40, with blood all over his white T-shirt.
7"He was rushed to the airport where an ambulance met him there. He was then put on a plane to L.A. to see a specialist," the statement read.
8Iglesias' show for 12,000 fans is part of his Sex and Love World Tour.
9His website says his next show is in July in Mexico City.
