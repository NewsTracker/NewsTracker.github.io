2One Direction star Louis Tomlinson is facing fresh family drama after his estranged father was arrested on Tuesday and later placed on suicide watch in hospital.

5He was subsequently taken to a local hospital and placed on suicide watch.
6During a hearing at Doncaster Magistrates' Court on Wednesday, prosecutor Joanne Lewis-Crookes said, "This is an unfortunate set of events...
