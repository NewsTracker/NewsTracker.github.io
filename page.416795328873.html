Police study threat against activist behind cartoon contest

1NEW YORK (AP) - The New York Police Department says it's assessing a threat posted on a website related to the Islamic State group against the organizer of a cartoon contest about the Prophet Muhammad.
2Blogger Pamela Geller organized the contest over the weekend in Texas, where two men started shooting before they were killed by police.
3NYPD spokesman Stephen Davis says the threat names Geller.
4He says investigators from counterterrorism and intelligence bureaus met with her on Wednesday and will do a "comprehensive threat assessment" to decide whether it's credible enough to require security for her.
5He hasn't commented on the specific nature of the threat or what any security measures would look like.
