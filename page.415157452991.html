Bill Gates repeats at top of Forbes' list

1Forbes says that Bill Gates's net worth rose to $US79.2 billion ($A101.27 billion) in 2015 from $US76 billion last year.
2That put him at the top of the magazine's list of the world's billionaires for the second consecutive year.
3The co-founder of Microsoft Corp. has topped the list for 16 of the last 21 years.
4In second place is telecom mogul Carlos Slim Helu, with a net worth of $US77.1 billion.
5He had topped the list in 2013.
6Next is investor Warren Buffett, who moved up one slot this year with a net worth of $US72.7 billion.
7Forbes says there were 1826 billionaires on its list this year, up from 1645 in 2014.
8It's the 29th year for Forbes billionaires' list.
