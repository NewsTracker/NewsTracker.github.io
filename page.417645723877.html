Egypt's CIB buying Citibank's local retail operations

1CAIRO (AP) " Egypt's Commercial International Bank, the country's largest private-sector bank, has signed an agreement to acquire Citibank Egypt's retail operations that had been up for sale.
2CIB said in a statement on its website on Tuesday that the acquisition is subject to pending regulatory approval.
3It also says the move would integrate Citibank's staff and add significant value to clients and shareholders.
4Citibank Egypt's website says it has 100,000 retail customers and operates 9 branches, including 8 in Cairo, and one in Alexandria.
5Citigroup is streamlining its global retail banking operations to focus on corporate and investment banking and other services.
