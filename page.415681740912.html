Gareth Bale's car attacked by angry Real Madrid fans following defeat by Barcelona

1The world's most expensive footballer was confronted by fans as he left the club's Valdebebas training base after the 2-1 loss that saw Barcelona move four points clear of their rivals.
2As Bale pulled away, one angry fan struck the 86million signing's white Bentley with his hands and feet, while his Madrid team-mates Jese and Sergio Ramos were also targeted.
3Bale was unlucky not to score at the Nou Camp in front of an estimated 400 million watchers on television as his first-half goal was ruled out for Cristiano Ronaldo being offside.
4The Wales international, despite helping Madrid win the Copa del Rey and Champions League by scoring in both finals last season, has found life difficult in Spain this year.
5Bale scored both goals in a 2-0 win against Levante less than two weeks ago, and celebrated ending his 829-minute drought by covering his ears as if to block out the criticism.
6It came after the former Tottenham Hotspur winger was whistled at his own stadium last month and a fan poll that found 40 per cent of supporters wanted Bale to be dropped by manager Carlo Ancelotti.
7Bale has been linked with moves to Manchester United and Chelsea previously, with the majority of Madrid fans seemingly hoping he decides to return to the Barclays Premier League.
