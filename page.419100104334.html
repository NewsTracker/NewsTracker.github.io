Apple says music service has more than 11m members

1Apple says more than 11 million people have signed up for a trial of its music streaming service since it launched on June 30.
2The three-month trial membership is free.
3After that, individuals can pay US$10 a month, while families of up to six people can pay $15 a month for unlimited access to Apple Music on their devices.
4Apple says 2 million people have signed up for the family plan trial.
5In addition to the streaming subscription plan, Apple Music also includes a free Internet radio station and a media platform for artists to upload songs and videos for fans.
6In comparison, popular music streaming service Spotify has more than 20 million paying subscribers.
