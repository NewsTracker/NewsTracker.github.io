Microsoft makes cheaper version of Surface Pro 3 tablet

1NEW YORK - Microsoft is making a cheaper version of its Surface Pro 3 tablet computer in an effort to reach students and budget-conscious families.
2The new $499 version will have a smaller screen - 10.8 inches rather than 12 - a slower processor, and the built-in kickstand won't be as flexible, just three angles rather than the unlimited positions offered on the Pro 3.
3But the Pro 3 ranges from $799 to $1,949.
4A keyboard cover, one of the Surface's distinctive features, will cost $129 extra.
5Microsoft bills the Surface line as a laptop replacement when used with the cover.
6Microsoft Corp. expects to ship the new device around May 5.
7Advance orders starting immediately.
