3 French charged in IS-inspired plan to attack military base

1PARIS (AP) - The Paris prosecutor's office says three young men arrested this week in a plot to attack a military base were in contact with an Islamic State group jihadi who told them to "strike in France."
2The prosecutor's statement said the suspects, who were arrested Monday, told investigators they planned a late December or early January attack on the Mediterranean military post Fort Bear, where the 23-year-old Djebril A., a former Marine, once worked.
3The two others, identified as Ismael K., 17, and Antoine F., 19, said the plan was to decapitate the base chief - a plot that Djebril denied.
