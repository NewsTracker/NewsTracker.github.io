Islamic State torches oil field near Tikrit as militia advance

1Islamic State torches oil field near Tikrit as militia advance Add to...
2Islamic State militants have set fire to oil wells northeast of the city of Tikrit to obstruct an assault by Shia militiamen and Iraqi soldiers trying to drive them from the Sunni Muslim city and surrounding towns, a witness said.
3The offensive is the biggest Iraqi forces have yet mounted against the militant group, which has declared an caliphate on captured territory in Iraq and Syria and spread fear across the region by slaughtering Arab and Western hostages and killing or kidnapping members of religious minorities such as Yazidis and Christians.
4The witness and a military source said Islamic State fighters ignited the fire at the Ajil oil field to shield themselves from attack by Iraqi military helicopters.
5Black smoke could be seen rising from the oil field since Wednesday afternoon, said the witness, who accompanied Iraqi militia and soldiers.
6Control of oil fields has played an important part in funding the Islamic State.
