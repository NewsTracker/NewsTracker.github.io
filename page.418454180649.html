Boston places RHP Buchholz on 15-day DL (Yahoo Sports)

1Boston Red Sox's Sandy Leon, left, signals to the dugout beside Clay Buchholz during the fourth inning of a baseball game against the New York Yankees in Boston, Friday, July 10, 2015.
2Manager John Farrell came to the mound and Buchholz left the game.
3BOSTON (AP) -- The Boston Red Sox have placed right-hander Clay Buchholz on the 15-day disabled list with a strained right elbow.
4To take his place, the club selected left-hander Brian Johnson, a highly touted prospect, from Triple-A Pawtucket Saturday before their game against the New York Yankees.
5The 30-year old Buchholz walked off after throwing a cutter clocked at 84 mph to Stephen Drew in the fourth inning of Friday night's loss.
6The 24-year-old Johnson is 8-6 with a 2.73 ERA with 81 strikeouts and 26 walks in 85 2/3 innings.
7Buchholz was 4-0 with an 0.87 ERA in his last four starts before losing Friday.
