12Febyanti, also known as Feby, is a fellow pastor who has known Chan since 2012, when she began helping prisoners at Bali's Kerobokan prison.
13A friendship formed, which last year developed into a loving relationship.
14Last month, soon after hearing his bid for clemency from execution had been rejected by Mr Joko, Chan proposed.
15Febyanti made a dramatic last-minute dash through police lines to Kerobokan early on Wednesday morning to see Chan before his transfer to Nusakambangan.
16As Febyanti and Michael Chan frantically negotiated with prison officials, she was in tears.
17The bid to see Chan was futile, and he and Sukumaran left the prison in an armoured personnel carrier 30 minutes later.
18In an interview with News Corp, Febyanti said: "Andrew is one of the strongest, kindest people I have ever met.
19"I have never seen him as just a prisoner or someone who is on death row.
20Chan and Sukumaran are being held at Besi prison in a four-cell block along with man due to be shot alongside them, Nigerian Jamiu Owolabi Abidin, also known as Raheem Agbaje Salami.
21The head of corrections in Central Java, Ahmad Yuspahruddin, told Fairfax Media, the pair were not permitted to mix with the general prison population but this will change if the executions are delayed for a lengthy period.
22They are allowed out of their cells after breakfast until noon.
23After lunch, they can then leave their cells again until lockdown in mid-afternoon.
24Sukumaran has not been able to paint yet, but that could change if there is confirmation of a lengthy postponement to the executions, Mr Yuspahruddin said.
