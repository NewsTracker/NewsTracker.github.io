Oklahoma City Thunder fire coach Scott Brooks after 7 years (Yahoo Sports)

1OKLAHOMA CITY (AP) -- The Oklahoma City Thunder have fired coach Scott Brooks.
2The team made the announcement Wednesday.
3Brooks coached the Thunder for seven seasons.
4Starting in 2010-11, he led the team to the Western Conference Finals three out of four years, and the Thunder reached the NBA Finals in 2012.
5The Thunder failed to make the playoffs this past season, as injuries limited them to a 45-37 finish.
6Brooks was 338-207 (.620) with the Thunder and was the 2009-10 NBA Coach of the Year.
