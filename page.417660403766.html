FIFA executive committee to choose election date

1ZURICH (AP) - FIFA President Sepp Blatter will chair an executive committee meeting next month to choose a date for an election to replace him.
2FIFA says the executive committee will meet "in July, the precise date to be confirmed within this week."
3That meeting "needs to confirm a date and agenda for the extraordinary elective Congress."
4FIFA says there are "various date options" for the election.
5When Blatter announced his resignation plan last week, he said his successor would be elected between December and March.
6FIFA rules require an election to be held four months after the deadline for prospective candidates to apply.
7Would-be candidates need to be nominated by five of FIFA's 209 member federations who vote in the election.
