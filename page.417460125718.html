Hamas kills Islamic State supporter in Gaza; 1st fatality in anti-IS crackdown

1GAZA CITY, Gaza Strip - Hamas forces have killed an Islamic State group supporter, the first fatality since Gaza's Islamic rulers launched a crackdown on the more-radical group months ago.
2Hamas spokesman Eyad Al-Bozum says Tuesday that a 27-year-old man died "during an attempt to arrest him."
3He says after the man refused to surrender, Hamas forces opened fire and planted explosives in the house where he was holed up.
4The killing comes hours after Islamic State supporters gave Hamas a 48-hour ultimatum to end the crackdown and release dozens of suspected IS loyalists rounded up in recent months.
5Hamas blames local Islamic State loyalists for a recent string of small explosions that have targeted Hamas security posts, but often cause no casualties.
