Apple recalls Beats Pill XL speakers

1June 3 Apple Inc said it is recalling Beats Pill XL portable wireless speakers due to a fire hazard from overheating batteries.
2The iPhone maker will recall about 222,000 speakers in the United States and about 11,000 in Canada, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) said on Wednesday.
3Apple has received eight reports of overheating speakers, the CPSC said in a statement.
4The company did not disclose the number of speakers affected by the recall or the number of complaints it received.
5The company said the recall does not affect any other Beats or Apple products.
