Baltimore mayor calls for federal investigation into police

1Baltimore's mayor is asking federal investigators to look into whether the city's police department uses a pattern of excessive force or discriminatory policing.
2Mayor Stephanie Rawlings-Blake said Wednesday that the community has a "fractured" relationship with police.
3Her request came a day after new Attorney General Loretta Lynch visited the city.
4The mayor says Lynch understands the urgency of her request.
5Baltimore saw days of unrest after Freddie Gray, a black man, was taken into custody and suffered critical injuries.
6He died a week later.
7The Justice Department is already investigating whether his civil rights were violated and six officers are charged in the arrest and death.
