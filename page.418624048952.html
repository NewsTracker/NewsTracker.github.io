Banned FIFA official to appear in court in US

1A top former FIFA official is due in court in the U.S. to face racketeering and bribery charges in a corruption case that has sent shock waves through the soccer world.
2Prosecutors say Jeffrey Webb will be arraigned in federal court in Brooklyn on Saturday.
3The 50-year-old Webb was among seven FIFA officials detained in Switzerland.
4The rest are fighting extradition.
5Prosecutors allege the defendants plotted to pay bribes of more than $150 million over a 24-year period.
6The payments were tied to the award of broadcasting and hosting rights for the World Cup and other tournaments.
7Since the indictment, Webb was banned provisionally and replaced as the FIFA vice president from the North and Central American and Caribbean region.
8Webb also was president of the regional governing body CONCACAF.
