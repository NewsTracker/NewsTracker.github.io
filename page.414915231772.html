Channel24.co.za | Suge Knight taken by ambulance from court for second time

1Los Angeles - Former rap mogul Marion "Suge" Knight was taken by ambulance on Thursday from a Los Angeles courthouse for an undisclosed medical issue.
2It was the second time in recent weeks that Knight has been taken to a hospital as he faces murder and robbery charges in separate cases.
3Knight had been scheduled to appear on Thursday for arraignment on a criminal threats charge, but he never made it to the courtroom.
4His attorney David Kenner said bailiffs and a judicial officer told him Knight had stomach issues and was taken for an evaluation before Kenner had a chance to see him.
5The Sheriff's Department did not have immediate information on Knight's condition.
6Earlier this month, the Death Row Records co-founder was rushed from a Compton courthouse with chest pains after he pleaded not guilty to murder, attempted murder and hit-and-run charges.
7Knight, 49, spent more than a day in the hospital.
8He is charged with killing Terry Carter, 55, in the parking lot of a burger stand in late January, and seriously injuring another man.
9Knight remains jailed without bail in that case.
10Knight was charged with robbery after a celebrity photographer reported that Knight and comedian Katt Williams stole her camera in September.
11Knight has pleaded not guilty to robbery, and prosecutors recently added the threats charge.
12Williams also pleaded not guilty to robbery.
13Knight was shot six times at a nightclub in September, days before he was accused of taking the camera in Beverly Hills.
14His attorneys have previously cited complications from the wounds as the reason for his medical issues.
