Letters signal Bali Nine execution close

1Indonesia's latest steps in relation to death row drug offenders including Andrew Chan and Myuran Sukumaran constitute ominous signs, one of their lawyers says.
2Embassies representing the Nigerian and Brazilian prisoners slated for execution with the Bali Nine pair have been summoned to a meeting at Cilicap near the island of Nusakambangan where prisoners are held before facing a firing squad.
3"It's worrying and it's ominous," lawyer Peter Morrissey told Nine Network on Friday.
4All 10 prisoners scheduled for execution are now on Nusakambangan, with the move overnight of Filipina Mary Jane Veloso.
5The single mother was taken by armoured car from a jail near Yogyakarta that her children and other relatives were expected to visit on Friday.
6Chan and Sukumaran were moved to the island in March, where they share a block of cells with Nigerian Raheem Salami.
7His lawyer, Utomo Karim, says based on the experience of the Nigerian embassy in January, when six people were executed, the Saturday meeting means the date is drawing near.
8"Based on experience from the previous execution, they're going to tell them the date for the execution," he told AAP.
9"Last time, when we were asked to gather in the district prosecutor's office, we were then taken to Nusakambangan to tell the convict about the execution time.
10"And three days after that, they were executed. I don't know whether other embassies have been notified at the same."
11Indonesia is required to give 72 hours' notice of the executions.
12Mr Morrissey said another lawyer, Julian McMahon, was flying to Indonesia on Friday.
13"We will keeping our (legal) efforts going. We will keep going right up until they pull the trigger quite frankly," he said.
14But Indonesia's attorney-general says he won't recognise the team's latest bid for a constitutional court challenge on behalf of the Sydney men.
15His spokesman Tony Spontana confirmed his office has sent letters to prosecutors advising them to prepare for the executions.
16The letters asked prosecutors to make preparations and were not the notification letters for the convicts themselves.
17The only Indonesian among the 10, Zainal Abidin, still has an application for a judicial review pending in the Supreme Court.
18Mr Spontana told Indonesian website detik.com the date hadn't been set because of his case.
19"We're still waiting for Zainal Abidin," he reportedly said.
20"When it's rejected then it will be complete, 10 convicts."
21Lawyers for Veloso had planned to file a new request for a judicial review on Monday.
22The EU on Thursday attacked the death sentence imposed on Frenchman Serge Atlaoui, after sharp exchanges between France and Indonesia over the case.
23Chan, 31, and Sukumaran, 34, were arrested 10 years ago in a plot to take heroin out of Indonesia, which has so far rejected diplomatic and legal efforts for mercy.
