Ukraine's disabled condemned to life in institutions

1Disability Rights International said in a report published Thursday that Ukraine is expanding its orphanages and children's homes, going against a global trend toward helping the disabled to integrate into society.
2The group said it found over the course of a three-year investigation that children in institutions in Ukraine are exposed to physical and sexual violence and live in danger of being trafficked for sex, labor and pornography.
3DRI president Laurie Ahern said children with disabilities are the often the worst-abused and the majority languish in institutions their whole lives.
4Estimates for the number of Ukrainian children in care vary from 82,000 to 200,000.
