Morrison wins by 4 in Spain

1Barcelona, Spain (SportsNetwork.com) - James Morrison shot a 3-under 69 in Sunday's final round and the Englishman went on to win by four shots at the Open de Espana.
2Morrison, who shared the third-round lead with David Howell, finished the week at 10-under-par 278.
3It is Morrison's second victory on the European Tour and first since the 2010 Madeira Islands Open.
4Defending champion Miguel Angel Jimenez holed out for eagle in his round and shot a 5-under 67.
5He tied for second at 6-under 282 with Howell (73), second- round leader Edouard Espana (69) and 2012 champion Francesco Molinari (71).
6Emiliano Grillo finished alone in sixth at minus-5 following a 1-under 71 at Real Club de Golf El Prat.
