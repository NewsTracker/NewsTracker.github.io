Clinton agrees to testify on Benghazi, emails this month

1Hillary Clinton has agreed to testify on Capitol Hill later this month about the attacks in Benghazi, Libya, and about her email practices.
2Her lawyer David Kendall wrote to lawmakers Monday telling them she would agree to the request from a special panel investigating the September 2012 attacks that killed four Americans.
3But Kendall said Clinton would testify only for one session.
4The committee chairman, GOP Rep.
5Trey Gowdy of South Carolina, had requested that she appear twice, for a hearing on Clinton's use of private emails and for a separate session on Benghazi.
6Kendall said Clinton would answer all lawmakers' questions during one session and it would not be necessary for her to appear twice.
