Apple removing games with Confederate flag from App Store

1CUPERTINO, Calif.
2The move comes days after Apple CEO Tim Cook called on people in a tweet to honor the victims of the Charleston, South Carolina, church shooting by eradicating racism and the symbols that feed it.
3Apple said in a statement Thursday that apps are being removed "that use the Confederate flag in offensive or mean-spirited ways, which is in violation of our guidelines."
4Other apps that depict the flag in educational or historical contexts are not being removed.
5The company is working with developers to get games that have been removed " such as the Civil War battle game "Cannon Shooter" " back in the store after changes are made.
6Apple Inc., which is based in Cupertino, California, is the latest company to distance itself from the divisive symbol after the shooting suspect, Dylann Storm Roof, appeared in photos holding the flag.
7This week, a host of retailers including Wal-Mart, Amazon, Sears, eBay and Etsy said they would remove Confederate flag merchandise from their shelves and websites, while Google blocked digital ads featuring the flag.
