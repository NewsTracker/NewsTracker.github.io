The (former) Fab Five: A look at One Direction as they were

1Zayn Malik delivered a shocker to his fans when he announced he was quitting the chart-topping band One Direction.
2The British singer announced Wednesday he was quitting the band "to be a normal 22-year-old."
3His bandmates Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Louis Tomlinson and Liam Payne - currently in the middle of their "On The Road Again" world tour - offered their best wishes but said they'd continue as a quartet.
4One Direction formed in 2010 after five teenagers - Styles, Payne, Horan, Tomlinson and Malik - auditioned individually for the British TV talent show "The X Factor."
5The quintet soon became one of the world's top pop acts with multiplatinum records, sold-out tours and even a hit movie.
6In the wake of Malik's departure, The Associated Press offers a look at One Direction's best moments in pictures.
