Mexico arrests alleged Sinaloa cartel cocaine trafficker

1Mexico's National Security Commissioner says Cesar Gastelum Serrano was arrested in the resort city of Cancun.
2Monte Alejandro Rubido said Sunday that Gastelum Serrano was carrying a pistol and a package of cocaine.
3The U.S. Treasury Department placed Gastelum Serrano on its drug kingpin list in December.
4It called him "one of the most prolific cocaine suppliers for Mexico's Sinaloa Cartel."
5The department said he headed "a vast criminal network to lead a cocaine trafficking organization capable of moving tons of cocaine per week through Honduras and Guatemala to Mexico. "
6Rubido said Gastelum Serrano had operated in Honduras, and had ordered the killing of law enforcement officials there.
