2 Separate Shootings Near Ferguson Protests Against Police

1FERGUSON, Mo.
2Gunshots, fires and looting rocked Ferguson overnight, as protests erupted following the death of a black man in police custody in Baltimore.

5No details have been released on the conditions of those shot.

7Protesters rioted in Baltimore Monday after 25-year-old Freddie Gray died of a spinal-cord injury in police custody.
