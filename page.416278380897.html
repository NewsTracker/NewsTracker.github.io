Bomb kills soldier, wounds another in Egypt's Sinai - sources

1CAIRO, April 16 (Reuters) - An Egyptian soldier was killed and another wounded on Thursday when a bomb targeted an armoured vehicle in the Northern Sinai, medical and security sources said, the latest in a wave of violence in a region hit by an Islamist insurgency.
2The attack was in the town of Sheikh Zuweid, security sources said.
3North Sinai is the epicentre of an insurgency that has killed hundreds of members of the security services since mid-2013, when then-army chief Abdel Fattah al-Sisi ousted Islamist president Mohamed Mursi after mass protests.
4Sisi was elected president last year.
