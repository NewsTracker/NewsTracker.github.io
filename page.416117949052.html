Crash of band's tour bus in Mexico kills 4, injures 10

1The Civil Protection Office for Jalisco state said in a statement that the accident happened Thursday afternoon on a highway between Zapote and Teocuitatlan.
2The bus driver was killed as were three passengers.
3Authorities did not immediately release names of the dead.
4They said the band was from the town of La Barca in Jalisco.
5Photographs of the scene provided by authorities show the band's bus at the bottom of an embankment, propped up by logs.
