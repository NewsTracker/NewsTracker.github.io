Mexico Candidate Slain; 1,000s of Ballots Burned in Attack

1A Mexican congressional hopeful was shot dead in a town bordering the capital Tuesday, becoming the fourth candidate to be slain ahead of Sunday's midterm elections.
2Miguel Angel Luna, mayor of Valle de Chalco in the state of Mexico southeast of Mexico City, was attacked by armed men at his campaign offices, according to a statement from his Democratic Revolution Party.
3Luna died shortly afterward at a hospital.
4An assistant was wounded.
5Since March, three other politicians running in mayoral races have been slain in the southern states of Michoacan and Guerrero.
6Also in Guerrero, an armed group broke into electoral offices in the city of Tlapa and burned more than 116,000 ballots late Monday, the state electoral institute reported.
