Bodies retrieved from US chopper in Nepal

1A US Marine helicopter that went missing in Nepal has now been recovered.
2Rescuers have recovered the bodies of all those aboard a US Marine Corps helicopter that crashed in Nepal during an earthquake relief mission.
3"Eight bodies have been recovered," the Nepal Army said on Saturday.
4"The chopper was carrying two Nepalese Army personnel and six US Marines onboard. All bodies are not recognisable."
5The aircraft was discovered on Friday in remote and rugged terrain between Dolakha and Sindupalchowk districts, in the north-eastern region.
6The UH-IY Huey helicopter went missing during a relief mission on Tuesday in the Dolakha district, the epicentre of a 7.3-magnitude earthquake that killed 117 people.
7It was the second earthquake in less than a month to hit the Himalayan country.
8More than 8,300 died in an 7.8-magnitude quake that struck Nepal on April 25.
