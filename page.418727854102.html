HBO signs ex-ESPN personality Bill Simmons to exclusive deal (Yahoo Sports)

1NEW YORK (AP) -- HBO says it has struck a multi-year, multi-platform deal with the multi-faceted Bill Simmons.
2Under the deal, which begins in October, HBO will be Simmons' exclusive television home, the network said Wednesday.
3Among other projects, Simmons will host a weekly talk show premiering next year.
4It will feature stories and guests from across the sports and cultural landscapes.
5He will also have a production deal to produce content for the network and its digital platforms, delivering video podcasts and features.
6In addition, he will consult with HBO Sports.
7A top personality at ESPN until they parted ways in May, Simmons has served as a sports columnist, TV host and analyst, and best-selling author.
