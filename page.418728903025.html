27WASHINGTON (AP) - When Barack Obama visited Kenya for the first time nearly 30 years ago, he was astonished that an airport worker recognized his last name.
28It was a striking experience for the future president.
29He was a young man struggling to understand how a country that he had never seen and a Kenyan family that he barely knew had shaped his identity.
30This week, Obama will make his first visit to Kenya as U.S. president.
31It's a trip that will bear little resemblance to the 1988 one, when he arrived aboard a commercial flight and his luggage got lost.
32Now, Air Force One will carry Obama to a country where children, roads and schools bear his name - and he's seen as a local son.
