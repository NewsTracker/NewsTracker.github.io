Turkey's Erdogan criticises Egypt over Mursi death sentence bid

1ANKARA, May 16 (Reuters) - Turkish President Tayyip Erdogan criticised Egypt over a court decision to seek the death penalty for ousted Islamist president Mohamed Mursi and accused the West of hypocrisy, the state-run Anatolian news agency said on Saturday.
2"While the West is abolishing the death penalty, they are just watching the continuation of death sentences in Egypt. They don't do anything about it," Erdogan was quoted as saying.
