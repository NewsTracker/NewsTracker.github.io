Egypt names Cuper from Argentina to lead its soccer team

1CAIRO (AP) " Egyptian Football Association has appointed Hector Cuper, from Argentina, as the new manager of its national team.
2Cuper takes over a squad that won the African Nations' Cup a record seven times but failed to qualify for the last three editions of the biannual tournament.
3Egypt's team has been without a fulltime manager since American Bob Bradley quit in late 2013 after he failed to take the Pharaohs to the World Cup in Brazil.
