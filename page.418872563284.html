Police: Officer shoots, wounds man in Baltimore

1Police say an officer shot a man in the face in southwest Baltimore after the man ran his car into a cruiser.
2Officers responded to reports of gunfire Monday night and police say they approached a car in the area.
3That's when police say the driver reversed into the cruiser and the officer fired at the driver.
4Officials say the man wasn't fatally wounded and the officer had minor injuries.
5Interim Police Commissioner Kevin Davis says the man drove around a corner and surrendered, telling police he was armed.
6Police searched for a second man who fled the vehicle.
7Officers escorted a gathering crowd away from the area and Davis says two people were arrested for disorderly conduct and hindering a police investigation.
