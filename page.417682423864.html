Landslide hits 6 villages in Nepal; 15 feared dead

1KATHMANDU, Nepal (AP) " A landslide triggered by heavy rainfall has buried six villages in Nepal's mountainous northeast and at least 15 people are believed to have been killed.
2Government administrator Surendra Bhattarai says the landslide hit the villages in Taplejung district at night when people were at home sleeping.
3He says another 12 people are believed to be missing but details are sketchy because of the remoteness of the area.
4It takes several hours on mountain trails to reach the villages.
5Landslides are common in mountainous Nepal during the rainy monsoon season, which began in June and ends in September.
6The Himalayan nation is still recovering from earthquakes in April and May that killed more than 8,700 people and caused massive damage.
