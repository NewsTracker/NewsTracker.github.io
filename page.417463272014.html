Helicopter crashes in Nepal mountains, killing 4 on board

1KATHMANDU, Nepal (AP) - A helicopter crashed in the mountains in northeast Nepal on Tuesday and four people on board were killed, police official said.
2The helicopter was returning to Kathmandu when it crashed on the Yamuna Danda hill near Balefi village, Nepal Police spokesman Kamal Singh Bam said.
3He said bodies of three men and a woman have been recovered from the crash site and police rescuers are trying to return the bodies to Kathmandu.
4Another helicopter has reached near the crash site.
5Other details were not known, but the area is in the region worst hit by last month's devastating earthquake.
6The government and aid agencies are both using helicopters to carry relief materials to the mountainous area.
7A U.S. Marines UH-1 "Huey" helicopter crashed last month in a nearby district while on a rescue mission, killing six Marines and two Nepalese soldiers.
8The earthquakes on April 25 and May 12 in Nepal have already killed more than 8,600 people.
