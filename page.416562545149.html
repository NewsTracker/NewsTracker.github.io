Islamic State kills five journalists working for Libyan TV station-army official

1BENGHAZI, Libya, April 27 (Reuters) - Islamic State militants have killed five journalists working for a Libyan TV station in the eastern part of the country, an army commander said on Monday.
2The reporters had been missing since August, when they left the eastern city Tobruk to travel to Benghazi.
3Their route took them through Derna, a militant Islamist hotspot.
4Faraj al-Barassi, a district army commander in eastern Libya, said militants loyal to Islamic State were responsible for the killing of the journalists, whose bodies were found outside the eastern city of Bayda.
