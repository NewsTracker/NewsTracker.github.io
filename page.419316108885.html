2DENVER (AP) -- Matt Kemp has become the first San Diego Padres player to hit for the cycle, finishing off the feat Friday night with a triple in the ninth inning at Colorado.
3Kemp had a two-run homer in the first, a single in the third and a double in the seventh.
4His triple in the ninth went off the fence in center and he easily scampered into third for the first cycle of his career.
5The Padres retrieved the historic baseball.
