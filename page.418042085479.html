Yahoo's Stamos to become Facebook's chief security officer

1NEW YORK (AP) - Yahoo's cybersecurity chief says he's heading to Facebook to become its chief security officer.
2Alex Stamos said in a Facebook post that he will be joining the social networking company on Monday.
3He succeeds Joe Sullivan, who departed Facebook Inc. in April to head to Uber.
4Stamos served as Yahoo Inc.'s chief information security officer for a little over a year, having started there in March 2014, according to his Linkedin account.
5Shares of Facebook added 37 cents to $89.23 in Thursday premarket trading.
