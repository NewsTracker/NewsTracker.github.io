Obama, Hillary Clinton meet privately at the White House

1WASHINGTON (AP) - President Barack Obama and Hillary Rodham Clinton met privately at the White House Monday afternoon.
2The White House says the former rivals-turned-colleagues met for about an hour and discussed a range of issues.
3Obama and Clinton have met occasionally since she left the administration in 2013 after serving as secretary of state.
4Monday's meeting comes as Clinton prepares to launch another presidential campaign.
5Moments after the White House confirmed the meeting, Clinton posted a message on Twitter praising Obama's health care law and its provisions aimed at covering young people and those with preexisting conditions.
6In a jab at Republicans who want to get rid of the law, she wrote: "Repeal those things? Embrace them!"
7To emphasize her point, Clinton shared an old photo of her and Obama hugging.
