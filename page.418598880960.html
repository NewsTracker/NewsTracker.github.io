6The Internet giant GOOG showed strong growth in mobile advertising revenue and in its YouTube business and tighter controls on costs in second-quarter financial results released Thursday after the close of trading.
7That standout performance led to a flurry of upbeat research notes from Wall Street analysts on Friday.
8And the stock surge sent the Nasdaq composite index to a record intraday high.
9Google had already gained 13.4% this year.
10Google trades under two tickers on the S&P 500: (GOOGL) and (GOOG).
11The first is the "Class A shares," which have voting rights, the latter is the "Class C" shares, which do not.
12Combined, the two classes added more than $50 billion in market cap, eclipsing Apple's gain of $46.4 billion in April 2012.
