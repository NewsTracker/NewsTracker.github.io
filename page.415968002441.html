Judge Orders Facebook and Zuckerberg to Turn Over Documents

1A New York judge says Facebook and its founder must release documents to a defense lawyer whose client has fled from criminal charges that he falsely claimed a majority ownership in the social media giant.
2Manhattan federal Judge Vernon Broderick issued an order Friday against Facebook and Mark Zuckerberg.
3He gave them until Monday to relinquish documents requested by Paul Ceglia's (SEHG'-lee-uhz) lawyer.
4The judge rejected a request documents not be turned over until Ceglia is caught.
5Documents requested include all electronic communications Zuckerberg had about a Ceglia contract in an 18-month stretch beginning in 2003.
6Ceglia cut off his electronic ankle bracelet last month and fled.
7His wife, two children and dog also are missing.
8The family lived in Wellsville, 70 miles southeast of Buffalo.
