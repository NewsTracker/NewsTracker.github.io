One Direction fans respond with dismay to report Louis Tomlinson to be a father

1LONDON - One Direction fans have reacted with dismay to a report that band member Louis Tomlinson is to become a father.
2Publicist Simon Jones declined to comment Wednesday on a People magazine report saying 23-year-old Tomlinson is expecting a child with American stylist Briana Jungwirth.
3Fans responded on Twitter using the hashtag #OhNoLouis.
4On the streets of London, some refused to accept the news.
5Clara Foxonet, a 16-year-old fan from France, said "It can't be real! It's not possible."
6Seventeen-year-old Australian fan Kiri Deane-Spread Speir said the news was "a shock. But I am not going to get angry ... If he is happy, I am happy."
