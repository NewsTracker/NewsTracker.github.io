Microsoft WILL allow you to use a MIDDLE FINGER emoji in Windows 10

1MICROSOFT has confirmed it will include a middle finger emoji in its upcoming Windows 10 operating system.
2Aside from a bemused pile of poop, the emojis preloaded on your smartphone and desktop devices are all in good taste.
3But Microsoft could be set to change that.
4The Redmond firm has confirmed it will be including the middle finger emoji in its upcoming Windows 10 operating system.
5The rude emoji will come in a choice of six different skin tones.
6Microsoft is the first major technology company to include the new middle finger in an operating system.
7But the middle finger is not the only change to the emoji in Windows 10.
8The increasingly popular smileys will be grey by default in the new Microsoft operating system.
9The decision was made after Unicode - the group responsible for the standardised set of emoji - recommended the default skin tone of emoji characters should be nonhuman.
10Windows 10 is expected to arrive on desktop, tablets and smartphones this summer.
11Microsoft claims the new operating system will bring a seamless and cohesive user experience across all devices.
12Users will see the return of the traditional Windows Start Menu as well as the all-new Internet Explorer replacement, Microsoft Edge.
