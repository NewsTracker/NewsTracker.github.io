FIFA to decide 2018 World Cup qualifying slots in May

1FIFA says it will decide on May 30 how many qualifying slots each continent will get for the 2018 World Cup.
2The meeting allows FIFA's six confederations to pitch their case for extra places for their teams.
3FIFA secretary general Jerome Valcke says the decision is scheduled the day after the FIFA presidential election, by a newly composed executive committee.
4UEFA had 13 of the 31 qualification slots at the 2014 World Cup, and also will have Russia as host nation in 2018.
