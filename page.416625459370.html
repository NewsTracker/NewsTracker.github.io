Baltimore death sparks protests in New York, Boston, Philly

1NEW YORK (AP) - Hundreds of people have rallied and marched in New York and Boston to protest the death of a Baltimore man who was critically injured in police custody as Philadelphia activists prepare for their own demonstration.
2At least 60 people were arrested in New York Wednesday night after protesters spilled into the streets of Manhattan, holding a die-in at Times Square and blocking some streets and a tunnel.
3In Boston, activists gathered in a park behind police headquarters and continued with a peaceful march to a park.
4Philadelphia protesters plan to demonstrate Thursday at city hall.
5They've drawn parallels between the death of a local man shot during a traffic stop and the April 19 death of Freddie Gray in Baltimore.
6Police say Gray died of a "significant spinal injury."
