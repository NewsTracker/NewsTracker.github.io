Officials: Roadside bomb kills 5 soldiers in Egypt's Sinai

1EL-ARISH, Egypt (AP)?
2Military officials say a roadside bomb has hit an armored vehicle in Egypt's restive northern Sinai, killing five soldiers, including an officer.
3The officials said the explosion Sunday occurred in the village of Kharouba south of El-Arish, the capital of North Sinai province.
4They spoke on condition of anonymity because they were not authorized to brief journalists.
5Insurgents have targeted police officers and soldiers there.
6The attacks surged following the 2013 military ouster of Islamist President Mohammed Morsi.
7The Sinai-based group Ansar Beit al-Maqdis, which pledged allegiance to the Islamic State group last year, has claimed many of the attacks.
8Earlier this month, the group killed at least 16 soldiers and three civilians and kidnapped a conscript.
9The group posted a video Friday of a militant repeatedly shooting the soldier.
