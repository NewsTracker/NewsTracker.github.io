Names of Suspended Baltimore Police Officers Released

1Baltimore police have released the names of six officers who have been suspended with pay after the death of a man who was critically injured during an arrest.
2- Lt. Brian Rice, who has been with the department since 1997.
3- Sgt. Alicia White, who has been with the department since 2010.
4- Officers William Porter, Garrett Miller and Edward Nero, who all joined the department in 2012.
5The officers were involved in the arrest and the van transport of 25-year-old Freddie Gray, who died a week after suffering a spinal injury while in police custody.
6Police have not said why Gray was arrested or what caused the fatal injury.
