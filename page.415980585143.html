Russia's FM critical of legislation on eastern Ukraine

1BRATISLAVA, Slovakia (AP) " Russia's foreign minister says legislation approved by Ukraine on territories held by Russia-backed rebels in eastern Ukraine goes against a current cease-fire agreement.
2Sergey Lavrov says "the law goes in the opposite direction" to the spirit of the deal brokered by Western leaders in Minsk, Belarus, that has calmed the conflict.
3Lavrov told reporters Saturday the move is a departure from the Minsk deal and Moscow will work to stop it.
4The law defines the area inside two eastern regions, Donetsk and Luhansk, which will receive autonomy, but says they will only get it when local elections are held.
5Lavrov is in the Slovak capital, Bratislava, to mark the 70th anniversary of its liberation by the Soviet troops in 1945.
