Marines to honor troops killed in Nepal in helicopter crash

1The Marine Corps will honor six Marines who were killed in a helicopter crash while on a rescue mission for earthquake victims in Nepal.
2The memorial ceremony will be held Wednesday afternoon on the flight line at Marine Corps Air Station Camp Pendleton, north of San Diego.
3The six Marines and two Nepalese soldiers were aboard the U.S. military's UH-1 "Huey" helicopter that went down May 12.
4The Marines were part of the Marine Light Attack Helicopter Squadron 469 of the 3rd Marine Aircraft Wing.
5An investigation into the cause of the crash continues.
6The Marines were helping with humanitarian efforts after recent earthquakes in Nepal that killed more than 8,600 people.
