Greek PM Tsipras replaces rebels in reshuffle

1Greek Prime Minister Alexis Tsipras (R) sits next to Finance Minister Euclid Tsakalotos during a parliamentary session in Athens, July 16, 2015.
2Greek Prime Minister Alexis Tsipras on Friday kicked out of his government rebel ministers who voted against reforms demanded by international creditors in return for a rescue deal.
3Tsipras was weakened by a revolt in his left-wing Syriza party, who voted against the measures on Thursday, forcing him to rely on opposition votes to pass the package Greece needs to stay afloat.
4Labour Minister Panos Skourletis will become energy minister to replace Panagiotis Lafazanis, one of the hardline rebels in the Syriza party.
