Google lets Pac Man forage through maps in early April Fools? prank

1Google lets Pac Man forage through maps in early April Fools' prank Add to...
2The virtual streets of Google Maps are being transformed into Pac Man's chomping grounds in celebration of April Fools' Day.
3Google added the option to convert its popular navigation service into the Pac Man video game on Tuesday morning, around the same day the calendar turned to April 1 in Asia.
4That ushered in a day when Google shows off its playful side by injecting more fun and humour into its services.
5The gag on Google Maps enables visitors to click on a Pac-Man symbol in the lower left of the screen to play the video game on whatever location is listed in the address bar.
6As has been happening for decades, Pac Man eats blinking dots while trying to elude four "ghosts" - Pinky, Blinky, Inky and Clyde.
7The game can be played in Google Maps on desktop computers or mobile devices that have the latest app update for Apple's iOS or Google's Android software.
8This isn't the first time that Google Inc.'s engineers have paid tribute to Pac Man.
9In May 2010, Google tweaked the doodle on its main search page to accommodate games of Pac Man.
10Planting Pac Man into maps probably won't be Google's only April Fools' stunt, based on the Mountain View, California, company's colorful past.
11Among other things, Google previously has promised to introduce search by smell, provide a translation tool for animals and posted notices for jobs on the moon.
