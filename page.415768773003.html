Yahoo says it will buy back $2 billion in stock

1Investors were pushing Yahoo to use some of the proceeds from the Alibaba spinoff to buy back stock, and the company had said it would keep buying back stock to return cash to shareholders.
2The stock repurchase program will expire March 31, 2018.
3Yahoo has $726 million remaining on previous stock buyback plan, which was approved in 2013 and expires at the end of 2016.
4In January, Yahoo Inc. said it will spin off its stake in Alibaba into a separate company later this year.
5The move will allow the new entity to pay lower taxes on Alibaba stock sales than Yahoo would have.
