Egypt names Cuper from Argentina to lead its soccer team

1The Egyptian Football Association announced on Monday, March 2, 2015 that they have appointed Cuper as the new manager of its national team.
2The 59-year old previously led the Emirati al-Wasl club, and the Spanish Racing de Santander.
3Egypt's team has been without a fulltime manager since American Bob Bradley quit in late 2013 after he failed to take the Pharaohs to the World Cup in Brazil.
4Egyptian soccer suffered a massive setback when more than 70 soccer fans were killed in post-match violence in 2012.
5Last month, 20 fans died in a stampede outside a stadium as police fired tear gas.
6CAIRO (AP) - Egyptian Football Association has appointed Hector Cuper, from Argentina, as the new manager of its national team.
7The appointment was announced on Monday.
8Cuper takes over a squad that won the African Nations' Cup a record seven times but failed to qualify for the last three editions of the biannual tournament.
