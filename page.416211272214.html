Artist Britto sues Apple, design firm over use of art, style

1The lawsuit seeks unspecified damages from Cupertino, California-based Apple and the design duo Craig Redman of New York and Karl Maier of London, whose company is called Craig & Karl.
2Britto's lawsuit, filed on behalf of his Miami Beach-based company Britto Central Inc., makes a number of claims, including copyright infringement and unfair competition.
3It also asks a judge to halt the alleged misuse of Britto's images.
4Apple's "Start Something New" marketing campaign was cited in the lawsuit as one recent example of misuse of Britto art.
5Officials at Apple and Craig & Karl did not immediately respond Monday to emails seeking comment.
