One Direction determined to continue without Zayn Malik

1Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, Liam Payne and Niall Horan were devastated when Malik announced his decision to leave the band last month so he can live like a "normal 22-year-old", but they promised to continue performing without him.
2Now they have spoken out to underline their determination not to break up entirely, insisting One Direction will live on.
3Tomlinson tells British newspaper The Sun, "All four of us are 100% committed to One Direction. We want to thank the fans for being so incredible."
4Payne adds, "We're gutted Zayn left. But after a few performances as a four-piece we feel confident and determined to carry on stronger than ever."
5It comes after unconfirmed reports that Malik has been in a studio working with British producer Naughty Boy for his first material as a solo artist.
