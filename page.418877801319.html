Wisconsin Assembly approves Milwaukee Bucks arena deal (Yahoo Sports)

1MILWAUKEE, WI - JUNE 23: This is a view of the Milwaukee Bucks new court on June 23, 2015 at the BMO Harris Bradley Center in Milwaukee, Wisconson.
2MADISON, Wis.
3(AP) -- The Wisconsin state Assembly has passed a bill to have taxpayers pay $250 million to help pay for a new Milwaukee Bucks arena.
4The bill passed on a bipartisan 52-34 vote Tuesday.
5The measure now goes to Gov. Scott Walker who has been working with lawmakers to reach a deal.
6Under the agreement, current and former Bucks owners will pay $250 million toward the cost of the arena.
7The taxpayer portion will eventually grow to about $400 million over 20 years.
8Supporters say the new arena will bring jobs to Milwaukee and help improve the economy of the state.
9They say a new arena will help the city and state continue to move forward.
10Bucks coach Jason Kidd watched the debate from the Assembly gallery.
