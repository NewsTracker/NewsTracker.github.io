Google executive among hundreds dead from Nepal quake

1NEW YORK (AP) - Dan Fredinburg, a Google executive who described himself as an adventurer, was among the hundreds who died in a massive earthquake that struck Nepal on Saturday.
2Google confirmed his death.
3Lawrence You, the company's director of privacy, posted online that Fredinburg was in Nepal with three other Google employees hiking Mount Everest.
4The other three, he added, are safe.
5Google would not give further details.
6The company says it has launched a "person finder" tool for Nepal to help people find loved ones and "is working to get updated satellite imagery to aid in the recovery effort."
7Google says it is committing $1 million to the quake response.
8Fredinburg started at Google since 2007.
9He served as product manager and the head of privacy at Google X.
