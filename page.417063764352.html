Illinois Marine among 8 dead in Nepal helicopter crash

1KATHMANDU, Nepal (AP) " A U.S. Marine Corps combat photographer from Illinois is among the eight dead who were aboard a Marine helicopter that crashed during a relief mission in earthquake-hit Nepal.
2Officials identified the victims Sunday.
3Cpl. Sara A. Medina, a combat photographer with Marine Corps Installations Pacific, Okinawa, Japan, and a native of Illinois, was among them.
4Six Marines and two Nepalese soldiers lost their lives in the crash.
5A statement from the U.S. military joint task force in Okinawa identified the Marines killed.
6The UH-1 "Huey" helicopter went missing Tuesday while delivering rice and tarps.
7The wreckage was found Friday following days of searching in the mountains northeast of Kathmandu, Nepal's capital.
8The cause of the crash has not been determined.
