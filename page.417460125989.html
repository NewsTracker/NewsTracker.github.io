Greece seeks agreement on deal, has submitted draft plan

1ATHENS, Greece (AP) - Prime Minister Alexis Tsipras says Greece has submitted a proposal for an agreement with its creditors, as Athens seeks a deal that will to unlock desperately needed rescue money.
2Tspiras said Tuesday the proposal, which he did not give any details of, was submitted to the country's creditors Monday night.
3His statements come the morning after the leaders of Germany and France held an emergency meeting on Greece in Berlin along with those of the European Commission, the European Central Bank and the International Monetary Fund.
