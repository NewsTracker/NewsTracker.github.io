Mexico arrests man wanted in massacre of 72 migrants

1Investigators said the migrants were killed for refusing to join the Zetas drug cartel and their bodies were dumped on a ranch in the town of San Fernando in the border state of Tamaulipas.
2Federal police announced Tuesday they had arrested Jose Guadalupe Reyes Rivera Monday at a mechanics shop in Ciudad Victoria, the capital of Tamaulipas state.
3Police said Reyes Rivera was sent to a federal prison in Matamoros.
4In December, Mexico's national human rights commission criticized the government investigation, authorities failed to gather forensic evidence, preserve bodies and perform autopsies on time.
5Twelve of the dead remain unidentified and buried in a common grave.
