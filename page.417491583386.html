Yahoo to Live-Stream Oct. 25 NFL Game in London

1Yahoo Inc (YHOO) will broadcast the Oct. 25 National Football League game between the Buffalo Bills and Jacksonville Jaguars in London in the first live-stream of an NFL game, the league said.
2The International Series game will be streamed exclusively free of charge on Yahoo properties including Yahoo Sports, Yahoo Screen and Tumblr, the NFL said on Wednesday.
3Yahoo paid at least $20 million for the streaming rights, technology news site Re/code reported, citing industry executives familiar with the negotiations.
4The game will also be televised in the Buffalo (WIVB-TV) and Jacksonville (WTEV-TV) markets.
