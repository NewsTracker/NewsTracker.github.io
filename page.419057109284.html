3Apple denies it plans on launching a wireless phone service following a report that it's exploring such plans for the U.S. and Europe.
4According to Business Insider, the tech giant is looking into the launch of a mobile virtual network operator (MVNO) service in the U.S. and Europe, which basically means they would pay an established telecommunications company to sell talk, text and data plans directly.
5However, in statements to CNBC and Reuters, which were later confirmed by USA TODAY, Apple says they have not even discussed plans to launch a wireless phone service.
6"We have not discussed nor do we have any plans to launch an MVNO," said a statement from Apple.
7BREAKING: Apple says it has not discussed & is not planning MVNO cellular service following reports saying it was planning on doing that.
8This would not be the first time we've heard reports of a tech giant jumping into the wireless service business.
9In April, Google unveiled Project Fi, its wireless service project that will run on the networks of Sprint and T-Mobile.
