4KATHMANDU, Nepal (AP) - Surya Bahadur Thapa, who served as Nepal's prime minister five times, has died in a hospital in India where he was being treated for stomach cancer, an official said Thursday.
5Thakur Sharma, a spokesman for Thapa's Rastriya Prajatantra Party, said he was hospitalized at Medanta Hospital on the outskirts of New Delhi last week and died Wednesday night.
6He served as Nepal's prime minister three times before 1990 when kings ruled the Himalayan state with a rubber-stamp parliament and a ban on political parties.
7After a multiparty democracy was established in 1990, he formed the Rastriya Prajatantra Party and held the post of prime minister twice.
8He stepped down in 2005.
9He was the oldest member of Nepal's 601-member Constituent Assembly which is currently drafting a constitution.
10His body will be flown to Nepal's capital, Kathmandu, for cremation on Friday, Sharma said.
11Thapa is survived by a son and three daughters.
12His son, Sunil Bahadur Thapa, is Nepal's commerce and supply minister.
13His wife died some years ago.
