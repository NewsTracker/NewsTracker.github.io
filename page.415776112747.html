Boko Haram headquarters destroyed, Nigerian military claims

1Boko Haram headquarters destroyed, Nigerian military claims Add to...
2Nigeria's military claims it has destroyed the headquarters of Boko Haram in Gwoza, a town in northeast Nigeria.
3It was not possible to verify the victory that comes the day before presidential elections.
4The official Twitter account of the Nigerian Defence Headquarters announced "FLASH: Troops this morning captured Gwoza destroying the Headquarters of the Terrorists self-styled Caliphate."
5It followed with "Several terrorists died while many are captured. Mopping up of entire #Gwoza and her suburbs is ongoing."
6There was no mention of the Sambisa Forest, in Gwoza local government area, where Nigeria's homegrown Islamic extremist group is believed to have camps.
7Warplanes have been bombarding the area for weeks.
