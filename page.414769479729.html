1The sensational allegation is contained in a letter sent by the legal team of Andrew Chan and Myuran Sukumaran to Indonesia's judicial committee, claiming there had been a breach of ethics.

11The letter to the judicial committee is the third appeal this year by the pair's lawyers.
12A bid for a second judicial review foundered after it was found it contained no new evidence.
