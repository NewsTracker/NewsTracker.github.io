Suicide attack kills two on Egypt's Sinai Peninsula

1The crater left by a suicide car bomb outside a police station in Egypt on Sunday.
2Cairo: A suicide car bomb targeting a police station in Egypt's restive Sinai Peninsula has killed two people and wounded 30 others, police and medics say.
3The bombing came hours after a roadside blast that targeted an army vehicle killed six soldiers and wounded two in the peninsula, where security forces are battling an Islamist insurgency.
4One of the two killed in the suicide car bombing was a civilian, while several policemen were among those wounded, medics said on Sunday.
5Police said the attacker drove a straw-covered truck loaded with explosives and detonated it close to the police station in North Sinai's provincial capital of El-Arish.
6The facade of the police station was damaged, police said, adding that several nearby buildings also suffered damages.
