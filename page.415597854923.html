Nepal soccer head stands down during FIFA financial probe

1FIFA says Nepal's top soccer official has extended his self-imposed ban while the governing body's ethics committee investigates possible financial wrongdoing.
2The 90-day sanction bars All Nepal Football Association president Ganesh Thapa from attending the Asian Football Confederation election congress in Bahrain on April 30.
3Thapa has been accused of embezzling $6 million by Nepalese lawmakers and initially recused himself from football for 120 days last November.
4He is a member of the FIFA committee organizing the Under-20 World Cup, which kicks off May 30 in New Zealand.
5The latest exclusion runs through most of that tournament.
6Thapa has led Nepal's soccer body for two decades.
7He was an ally of former AFC President Mohamed bin Hammam, who FIFA expelled from soccer in 2012 for financial wrongdoing.
