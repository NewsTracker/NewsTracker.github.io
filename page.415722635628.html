Islamic State group's affiliate in Libya claims car bombings that killed 12 in Benghazi

1TRIPOLI, Libya - The Islamic State group's affiliate in Libya has claimed responsibility for a series of car bombings that killed 12 people in the country's eastern city of Benghazi.
2The Islamic State affiliate claimed responsibility for the Tuesday bombings in a post on an Internet bulletin board site known to be used by the group and its sympathizers.
3The claim included a picture of an alleged Tunisian suicide bomber it said was involved in the attack.
4A Libyan security official said Wednesday that the bombings targeted forces of Libya's elected government and allied fighters in Benghazi.
5He said the blasts killed 12 people and wounded 25.
