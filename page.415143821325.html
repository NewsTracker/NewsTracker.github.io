18Neither man smiled or spoke substantively as they shook hands at the start of the talks, which took place less than a week after Kerry told Congress that Russian officials have lied "to my face" about Moscow's role in Ukraine.
19That comment drew a rebuke from the Russian foreign ministry.
20U.S. officials have pointed out that Kerry did not specifically accuse Russian Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov of lying to him.
21While Russia denies its troops are fighting in Ukraine, the UN has cited "credible reports (that) indicate a continuing flow of heavy weaponry and foreign fighters" from Russia.
22Ukraine has been steadily intensifying its war-readiness since the conflict in the east broke and has embarked on numerous waves of partial military mobilizations.
23On Monday, Poroshenko presented legislation to parliament to boost the size of the country's armed forces to 250,000.
24Interfax-Ukraine news agency cited Prime Minister Arseniy Yatsenyuk as saying last week that boosting the size of the army to that amount would require enlisting an additional 68,000 troops.
