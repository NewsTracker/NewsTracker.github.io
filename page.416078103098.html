Gold worth $8.5M stolen from Mexican mine with Canadian connection

1MEXICO CITY -- Canada-based McEwen Mining Inc. says armed robbers took $8.5 million in gold from a refinery at a mine in Mexico's western Sinaloa state.
2A company statement says the theft occurred Tuesday at the El Gallo 1 mine and involved 900 kilograms of gold-bearing concentrate containing approximately 7,000 ounces of gold.
3The company said Tuesday that the crime was being investigated by Mexican authorities.
4It said its insurance policies will not be enough to cover the losses.
5The statement said no one was injured and mining operations were not affected, but did not provide any more details about the robbery.
6The Toronto-based company has operations in Mexico, Nevada and Argentina.
