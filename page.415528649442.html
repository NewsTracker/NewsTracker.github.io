Sheriff's Office: Motorcyclist killed, wife hurt in collision with horse on New Mexico road

1SANTA FE, N.M. - Authorities say a man is dead and his wife injured after the motorcycle they were on collided with a horse and rider in New Mexico.
2The Santa Fe County Sheriff's Office says Steven Mudge was thrown from the motorcycle when it hit the horse Sunday evening on a road south of Santa Fe.
3He was badly hurt and died early Monday.
4Office say Mudge was 65 years old, but an online posting by relatives says he was 61.
5His wife, 56-year-old Nancy Mudge, was hospitalized for treatment of her injuries.
6The man riding the horse was treated at the scene.
7The horse ran off, but the rider was able to find it and call a veterinarian to examine the animal.
8It was unclear if the horse was hurt.
